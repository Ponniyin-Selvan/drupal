<< Leonardo was more of a scientist than an artist

Hi; Actually it was the other way around. He was an genius artist with
exceptional science skills. There is a specific art division in the field of
engineering(especially automotive engineering) called Cutaway art. Not many
are aware of that as it requires a certain high degree of skill and
visualization. The Cutaway artist requires some engineering knowledge to do
this work. Here the artist sketches the car/machine as a whole and reveals
the innards with breathtaking detail. Sometimes these are better than most
CAD models. This is a dying art as high resolution CAD models have replaced
this but this is still practiced by some car stylists in the UK and some in
the US.

Leonardo was the earliest Cutaway artist with incredible engineering
knowledge. Remember, most of his sketches remained sketches and he ony
built/prototyped a few of his ideas. He was an artist (thereby the
incredible imagination as most ideas were too radical for his times) with
exceptional engineering/science knowledge.
