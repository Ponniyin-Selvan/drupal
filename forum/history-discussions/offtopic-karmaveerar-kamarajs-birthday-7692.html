Friends,

I was pleasently surprised to find a group in my neighbourhood
distributing Sweet Pongal on the occasion of our former CM Kamaraj's
Birthday. :)

I hope people are able to remember the best contribution of Kamaraj, the
noon meal scheme for school children.
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K._Kamaraj
==============

DEAR NANDAKUMAR,

DEFINITELY FOR EVERY STUDENT WHO COULD READ UNDER KAMARAJ PERIOD, IT WAS
UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE ::

1. School uniform was introduced - to eradicate disparity among the
Children's attitude - create friendly environment - because the difference
between Rich and Poor was vast those days.

2. It was introduced in Primary School level.. then to Secondary School...
then Higher.
This three tier system - a School upto 5th std which upgraded to 8th Std
and then to 11th Std.

subsequently it was changed to 10 + 2.

3. Nutrition Meal and the Milk Powder (under PL 420 UNESCO plan ?).
We heard of the ROLE OF UNESCO during those times only.

4. Oration / Essay Competitions in Schools re GANDHIJI - NEHRUJI -
INDEPENDENCE DAY - REPUBLIC DAY .. ! Some of the ardent Speakers of today
were definitely identified those days !!

5. Arignar Anna was very great inspiration for Oration competitions -in
content, adukkumozhi, more use of tamil dictions, passion to establish a
view point etc.

6. Introduction of Technical Education at School level ...

7. Introduction of English slowly in the Government Schools etc. (under
bi-lingual system).

We had Head masters - who were very much respected.

Even during Sports class - some of us use to go to other classes and stand
outside to hear the impressive teaching method of some very Good teachers.

I recall these Teachers - Head Masters and those very good oradators in
higher classes definitely were my immediate HEROs those days!!

Karmaveerar Kamarajar - C Subamanian and Ne.Thu.Sundaravadivelu
(Educational Director / TN) formed an excellent Team those days !

Ne. thu. sundaravadivelu was berived in losing his son Thirugnanasambandam
(?) in early ages and we paid homage to that brilliant boy !

thanks for refreshing.. dear Nandakumar.

Really my heart was bleeding when came to know very much later that his
sister worked as maid for survival ..

the King Maker did not take care of his Home - leave along his personal
Kingdom !

regards/ sps
