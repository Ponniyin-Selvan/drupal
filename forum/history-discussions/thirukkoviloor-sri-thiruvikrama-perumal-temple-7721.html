About the Temple:
This temple is situated in South Arcot district in Tamil Nadu. 2=20=20

Specials:

According to Older tradition, "Ko" means cow in Tamil and Cow is=20=20
related to Dharma. So, Cow is related Aathma if the people and the=20=20
God is related to the person who is taking care of all the Cows,=20=20
Goats etc. Since, Lord Narayana (Vishnu) is saying this message to=20=20

Both Saivam and Vaishnavam should be treated as a single one. This=20=20
is the primary thing, which is taken care of by the Alwar at this=20=20
place.
There should be no difference between these two castes. In this way=20=20
this village is built without any racism.
This is one among the "Pancha Krishnaranya Kshetram". Other=20=20
Ksthremas are Thirukkannankudi, Thirukkannapuram,=20=20
Thirukkannanmangai and Kabisthalam.
The Sthalam is the starting point of the great, Naalaayira Divya=20=20
Prabhandham explained to the World.
Sthlapuranam :
In this sthalam only, Naalayira Divya Prabandham was explained to=20=20
the world and it can be said that it is the place of origin of it.
Poigai Alwar was born in Thiruvekka, is considered to be the Hamsam=20=20
of Thiru Sangu of Sriman Narayanan, Bhoodhatalwar was born in=20=20
Mahabalipuram, is considered to be the Hamsam of Thiru Ghadhai=20=20

Aadhikesava Perumal Koil in Thiru Myilai (Mylapore). All these 3=20=20
Alwars took their humanity Avathars to explain the specials and=20=20
greatness of Lord Vishnu. All these 3 Alwars born in Sithirai year,=20=20
in 7th Century, Iyppasi month.
Poigai Alwar belong to Ashtami and Thiruvona Natchatiram and was=20=20
born on Tuesday. Bhoodhatalwar in Avittam on Wednesday Navami and=20=20
Pei alwar in Sathayam on thursday (Dhasami). All these 3 Alwars=20=20
born at different places but met all at once in Thirukkoviloor and=20=20
in this sthalam, they got the Paramapadha life.
<4.jpg One day, all these 3 Alwars wanted to worship the Perumal in=20=20
Thirukkoviloor, Sri Thiruvikraman. One fine day, they started from=20=20
their respective places to worship the Perumal, separately. First,=20=20
Poigai Alwar ! wanted to worship, but since it rained heavily, he=20=20
went to a Brahmnar's house and asked for some place to stay for=20=20
some time, since it was raining heavily. The Brahmnar gave him a=20=20
small place in the house (can be said as the small hall).
Only one person can stretch his hands and legs and can feel=20=20
somewhat comfortable with the place. At that time, Bhoodhatalwar,=20=20
who was drowned completely due to rain, came there and asked the=20=20
place to stay for sometime to the Brahmnanar. On seeing=20=20
Bhoodhatalwar, Poigai Alwar who was sleeping in the room, said that=20=20
one person can sleep and if it is two persons, we can sit=20=20

Poigai Alwar said that if it is one person, he can sleep and if it=20=20
is two persons, they can sit and since it is 3 persons, they can=20=20
only stand. And it is explained as a slogan by

"Oruvar Padukka - Iruvar Irukka - Moovar Nirkalam". Like this way=20=20
only, all these 3 Alwars got introduced among themselves and showed=20=20

All these 3 Alwars, started to sing Andhadhees (song praise of God)=20=20

Co - means cow, since, the speciality of cow is very much explained=20=20

Dharma and since it helps largely towards the humanity, the name=20=20
