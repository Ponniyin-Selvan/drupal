"Arulmigu Sani Bhagawan Alayam ,Kuchanur. In this temple Lord
Sani is present in splendid isolation bereft of the other planets. Here
he is known as Kuchanooran. The Chief deity Seneeswaran in the man God
and its Genesis was on it own and not created (Suyambu). In all temples
Saneeswaran in either of Navagrahah or he remain in separate sannithi.

This

also be reached from uppukottai or uthamapalayam via markalankottai.<!--

Vehicle : Crow

Gem : Blue Saphire

Flower : Vanni and black Kuvalai
Fabric : Black Cloth
Food
: Rice mixed with Gingelly
powder

Saneeswaran
is reputed to be both, a giver and a destroyer. A person who prays to
Saneeswaran regularly will be blessed with not only riddance from the problems
and worries faced, but a life that one desires.

Saturn is the planet of responsibility, and symbolizes the ethic of hard work.

Temple is open from 6 A.M.
to 1.00
P.M. and 4 P.M. to 8.30 P.M.
