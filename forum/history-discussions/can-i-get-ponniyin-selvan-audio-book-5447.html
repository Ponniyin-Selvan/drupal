Can I get Ponniyin Selvan in Audio Book?

Just a sugestion. Normally music and commercial mp3 cds are recorded at
128 kbps. But a voice file can be at recorded even at 16 kbps without
any degradation in quality. A 16kbps file will be 8 imes small.

One hour voice recording will be about 57 MB with 128 kbps, but will be
only about 7MB with 16 kbps.

A freeware such as 'switch' will do the conversion.
