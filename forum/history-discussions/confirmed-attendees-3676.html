On 8/6/06, R.venketesh

Hi all

the meet was attended by 8 of us.

Hi all,
It was a great evening. I picked up Madan from a near by place and
reached Gandhi statue aroung 5.30pm. On the way Shankaran called Madan
and said he will be there by 6.00pm. While nearing lighthouse,
venkatesh called and said he was already there. So when I reached, it
was Venkatesh,Madan and me very soon joined by a new face Sivaraman
(when i say new face, its not to the group as such, but for personal
interaction). Muruganandam was held in a office meeting and joined us
by 6.30. Chandra and a

As venkatesh said, it was more of a literery and general meeting and
not much on history. For a change we left cholas aside and talked
about Pandiyas, hoysalas, chalukyas etc. During the course, the
techies got driffted into technical arguments which was protested by
the others. We all missed Swetha and SPS. Swetha for the discussion
and SPS, ofcourse for the sponsorship of snacks from Grand sweets :)
If SPS had been there, i am sure the strenght would have been in 2
digits.

It was a fun filled evening, discussing about our earlier trip, the
novels we had read and other lighter things. never realised it was
more than 3 hours of discussion when we departed.

Seeing only two new face out of 8 was a timers joined by Madan who is fairly new but know to many from the
earlier meeting. Only Seenu and Sivaraman were total new faces to all
of us, and sure from next meeting they will become oldtimers. But
their participation in the very first meeting itself was great.

venkatesh sponsored bajjis to us and Madan showed his negotiation
skills to get one OC bajji for himself from the bajjiwala.

Wish more people attended, but neverthless it was a great evening.

Regards,
Satish

On 8/6/06, R.venketesh

Hi all

the meet was attended by 8 of us.
and a great time we had

it was more oriented to the literary aspects of our group and we had
an opportunity to go indepth in ps again.

that is something we dont have much of an opportunity to do in the
group these days where people talk more of of epigraphy and
iconography.

we discussed ponniyin selvan, the scene of aaditya karikalans murder
and took a vote on who could held the hilt of the sword that killed
him.
we also talked about the greatness of the chalukyas .
sankaran briefed us on the three chola heirs to the throne who were
murdered, and how kulothungan needled his way to the throne. he seemed
rather angry that kuluthungan had been compared to rrc in these
columns.
sivaram, seenu,madan along with chandra, muruganandam, sathish,
sankaran and myself attended.

the menu was sundal, sukku cofee, cauliflower, vaalakkai and chilly
bajji.

it was a wonderful time guys after quite some time, and I hope the
others enjoyed it as much as I did

venketesh
