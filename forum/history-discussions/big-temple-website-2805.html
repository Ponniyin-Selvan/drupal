Hi,

As you are aware, a proposal to setup a nice proper website for the
big temple has been made.I propose the following structure for the
website keeping in mind that there are already many webpages about
the big temple but none are comprehensive and exhaustive in details.

The domain for the site will be registered in a day or two.

The site will be in TWO languages : TAMIL and ENGLISH.
The site has to be built in phases, and we need VOLUNTEERS to
contribute for the cause.We need the permission of Varalaaru.com to
use their images and articles liberally(and those images and content
painfully collected in the

The site shall not seek revenues in any form.The website shall be
free from advertisements.

The below is just a rough draft, please pour in with your suggestions.

1.History
-----------
? Inspiration,Construction
? The later years
? The Big Temple today
? Page About Raja Raja and the Cholas

2.The Temple
------------
? The Deity
? The style of temple (Nagara/Dradiva/Vesara),temple layout etc
? Special features of the temple architecture
? Stories in Stones - coverage on Panels - their background
stories,statues etc.
? Art in the Big Temple - the not so publicised paintings.

Literature,Inscriptions and features on the same(Readings)
----------------------------------------------------------

? Big Temple inscriptions
? Their significance in the study of history(with the complete
inscriptions if possible)
? Pages on the famous novels Ponniyin Selvan (and other works
reg Cholas,Big Temple etc),movies on Raja Raja Chola
? Books on the temple,Cholas and TN history.

Gallery
---------
? With High-Res pictures,wallpapers,3D views,Panaroma
shots,paintings of the Big Temple and such.
? A page about the the site.

And as pointed out earlier, we need VOLUNTEERS.They can choose their
favourite topic from the above and fill in the content.Formatting and
putting it on the site shall be the responsiblity of the
webmasters.Credits should be given wherever due.

If you are intrested in being a part of the team, please contact :
amarnathgovindarajan@yahoo.com

There are some big shots and scholars amidst us,I'd be glad if they
write for the pages....esp the people writing in Varalaaru.com

Rgds
Amar
