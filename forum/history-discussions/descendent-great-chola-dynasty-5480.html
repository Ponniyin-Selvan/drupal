A big hello to Ponniyin Selvan members.I am a new member.Its interesting
to know about the Cholas.Like other people here i am also curious to
know who were the present descendents of the Cholas and Pandiyas.

I too read in junior Vikatan about the Pichavaram zamin family.What we
know is only the Cholas had the right to get crowned in Chidambaram
temple through the history of Kutruva Nayanar history in Periya Puranam.
These zamindars were know as Cholanar.I got some info about these
zamindars.

1844 - Rathinaswamy Surappa Cholanar was crowned

followed by Ramabadra Surappa Cholanar(Year unknown)

1944 -Andiyappa Surappa Cholanar was crowned

1978 - Chidambaranatha Surappa Cholanar was crowned.

It is said that the members of the Zamin family are now living in a
place called Chellappan Pettai near Pandanallur.
In 2004 in Jaya TV Programme "Achamillai Achamillai" film actress
Lakshmi interviewed the Zamin family.In that programme two historians
told about the possibilites of this family to be a Chola.

I came across one info about the existence of the Cholas during the
16th century AD.This is mentioned in the book called "Pandiyar Varalaru"
written by Sri.Mangala Murugesan.
It mentiones about one Chola called Veera Sekara Chola who occupied the
territories of the Pandiyan who ruled some portion of the Pandiyan
country.The Pandiyan is identified as Chandrasekara Pandiyan.

For instance, Rajaraja's daughter Kundhavai marries Vimaladitya,
the Chalukya. Narendra s/o Kundavai married Ammanga Devi,
the daughter of Rajendra. Their
son is Kulothunga Chola.
He marries the daughter Madurantaki of
Rajendra II who is the brother of AmmangaDevi.
============ ========= ========= ======
RAJENDRA HAD 3 SONS : THEY RULED BETWEEN 1044 - 1070.

Rajakesari RAJATHIRAJAN :: 1018 - 1054. Co-ruled with Rajendra
from
1018 to 1044. Defeated Chalukyas in 1044-45. He died in 1054 in
KOPPAM BATTLE On the banks of Krishna River against Agavamallan.
Was also known as King Who died on the Elephant.

Parakesari RAJENDRA CHOZHA DEVAR :: 1052 - 1064. Co-ruled with
Rajadithan till he died in 1054. In the same Koppam battle
Field,
he pronounced himself as the King and defeated Jayasimhan,
Pulikesin, Dasapanman & Nanni Nulamban and killed them.
Agavamallan
left the battle field. He waged a war against Lanka too.

Rajakesari RAJAMAHENDRAN :: S/o aforersaid Rajendra Chozha Devar.
Was Yuvaraja 1060 - 1073.

Rajakesari VEERARAJENDRAN :: 1063 - 1069. Also known as Karikalan.

Parakesari ADHIRAJENDRAN :: 1069 - 1070 S/o Veerarajendran.
============ ========= ========= ========= =====
------------ --------- --------- --------- --------
KULOTHUNGA I was born to RjC's daughter AMMANGA DEVI and RAJARAJA
NARENDRAN (ruled Vengi 1018 - 1059) in 1043 - Star Poosam. He was
also named as Rajendran after RjC in conformity with Chozha
clan.
ALSO WAS KNOWN AS VISHNUVARDHANAN - VII.

There was one VIJAYADITHAN - VII, born to VIMALADITHAN, thro" the
step mother of Rajaraja Narendran. He assisted RR Narendran.
Sakthivarman was s/o Vijayadithan VII. KULOTHUNGA was already
declared Yuvaraja and was in Vengi / Chakrakottam.

After Adhirajendra' s death in 1070, KULOTHUNGA rushed to GANGAI
KONDA CHOZHAPURAM and BECAME CHOZHA KING.
Aforesaid Vijayaditha VII was ruling Vengi.

After this, there were various polarisation among Kings in
various
parts of Deccan ..

For instance, Rajaraja's daughter Kundhavai marries Vimaladitya,
the Chalukya. Narendra s/o Kundavai married Ammanga Devi,
the daughter of Rajendra. Their
son is Kulothunga Chola.
He marries the daughter Madurantaki of
Rajendra II who is the brother of AmmangaDevi.
====================================
RAJENDRA HAD 3 SONS : THEY RULED BETWEEN 1044 - 1070.

Rajakesari RAJATHIRAJAN :: 1018 - 1054. Co-ruled with Rajendra
from
1018 to 1044. Defeated Chalukyas in 1044-45. He died in 1054 in
KOPPAM BATTLE On the banks of Krishna River against Agavamallan.
Was also known as King Who died on the Elephant.

Parakesari RAJENDRA CHOZHA DEVAR :: 1052 - 1064. Co-ruled with
Rajadithan till he died in 1054. In the same Koppam battle
Field,
he pronounced himself as the King and defeated Jayasimhan,
Pulikesin, Dasapanman & Nanni Nulamban and killed them.
Agavamallan
left the battle field. He waged a war against Lanka too.

Rajakesari RAJAMAHENDRAN :: S/o aforersaid Rajendra Chozha Devar.
Was Yuvaraja 1060 - 1073.

Rajakesari VEERARAJENDRAN :: 1063 - 1069. Also known as Karikalan.

Parakesari ADHIRAJENDRAN :: 1069 - 1070 S/o Veerarajendran.
============================================
-----------------------------------------------
KULOTHUNGA I was born to RjC's daughter AMMANGA DEVI and RAJARAJA
NARENDRAN (ruled Vengi 1018 - 1059) in 1043 - Star Poosam. He was
also named as Rajendran after RjC in conformity with Chozha
clan.
ALSO WAS KNOWN AS VISHNUVARDHANAN - VII.

There was one VIJAYADITHAN - VII, born to VIMALADITHAN, thro" the
step mother of Rajaraja Narendran. He assisted RR Narendran.
Sakthivarman was s/o Vijayadithan VII. KULOTHUNGA was already
declared Yuvaraja and was in Vengi / Chakrakottam.

After Adhirajendra's death in 1070, KULOTHUNGA rushed to GANGAI
KONDA CHOZHAPURAM and BECAME CHOZHA KING.
Aforesaid Vijayaditha VII was ruling Vengi.

After this, there were various polarisation among Kings in
various
parts of Deccan ..
