deity " - expalins SR Blasubramian in his book : chozha temples !

thus Madevadigal was Siva woman Sanyasin !

CERTAIN VERY INTERESTING inscriptions are found in respect of one
Ambalavanan Pazhuvur Nakkan @ Vikramasola Mararajan of Kuvalalam
(originated from Kolar, near Bangalore) during Rajaraja's period :

1. AR 168 of 1929 : 3rd Yr of RR : Govindaputhur, Trichy
Dt ::Thula - monday - Navami - Thiruvonam (corresponding to
03.10.0987,) an Agreement is given by the Assembly of Sri
Vanavanmahadevi Chaturvedi mangalam, in
consideration of a lump sum of 200 Kalanju, to pay all the taxes
due on a veli - land endowed for worship of temple - in which the
Srivimanam was built by AMBALAVANAN PAZHUVUR NAKKAN @ VIKJRAMASOLA
MARAHARAJAN OF KUVALALAM, a Nobleman of Mummudichozhadeva.... (This
Donor has figured as an important personage in the inscriptions of
Uthama Choha) :: Vikramachozhan Uthamachozhan (Kulothungan was also
called Vikramachozhan. since the inscription is that of RR, we
deduce Vikrama as Uthama)
=============================
( ALSO PLS NOTE VANAVAN MADEVI CHATURVEDI MANAGALAM )
=========================

2. AR 614 of 1920 : 3rd Rgnl Yr of RR : Olagapuram, Marakkanam :
Ambalavanan Kandradithan @ Mummudichozha Vizhupparayan of KUVALALAM
has purchased about 12 Veli lands from certain "Sarvisara Trinetra
Dasapuriya Bhatan" who himself has purchased it from UTHAMA CHOZHA!
in OLAGAPURAM, Tindivanam Taluk (Near Marakkanam).
------------------------------------
HE HAS PURCHASED A LAND FROM UTHAMA HIMSELF EARLIER
--------------------------------------

3. AR 129 of 1919: 3rd Rgnl Yr of RR : Olagapuram, Marakkanam : The
same Nobleman is again mentioned as "Oymanaattu Olagamadevipurathu
Sri Kailayattu Paramaswamikalukku thirukkatrali euppitha UDAYAR
PERUNDANATHU AMBALAVANAN KANDRADITHANAR ... "
Means the village is named after OLAGAMADEVI (w/o Rajarajan) and
the Noble man has constructed a stone Siva temple here.
===========================
THIS HE HAS DONE IN THE 3 YR OF RRC
--------------------------
4. AR 175 of 1929 : 5th Rgnl Yr of RR : Govindaputhur, Trichy
Dt :On the South Wall of Central Shrine of Gangajatadhara temple :

" Swasthishree Kopparakesari panmarkku Yaandu Anjavathu (5th),
Vadakarai Brahmadeyam Periya shree Vanavanmadevi Chaturvedi
mangalathu Sri Vijayamangala thevarkku, AMBALAVANAN PAZHUVUR
NAKKANANA RAJA RAJA PALLAVARAYN xxxx describing provisions for
several requirement of daily worship, speicial days worhsip .. (The
AR (Annual Report) reports that the Donor figures largely in the
records of Uthama Chozha as an officer of the King with the title
Vikrama chozha!)
==========================================
Means, AMBALAVANAN PAZHUVUR NAKKAN he who named himself after Uthama
Chozha in the 3rd Rgnl Yr of RRC has changed his Title as Rajaraja
in his 5th Rgnl Yr!
=========================================
Kolar has constructed a temple in Olagamadevipuram and another at
Vanavanmadevi Chaturvedi Mangalam.

Re Panchavanmadevi, beloved wife of RR (Nambirattiyar say
Thiruvidaimaruthur inscriptions) is known as Daughter of "Pazhuvur
Nakkan" !

==========================
Therefore it is clear Olagamadevi was very much grown up and was
already weilding Power.

prefer TRIO instead of Catorie - and I also prefer to use the word
STRENGTHENING instead of Controlling - the King RRC.
