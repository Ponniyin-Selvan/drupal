Dear Venkat &

Yesterday been to Thanjai Big Temple with Dr. Nagaswamy and Dr.
Kudavoil Balu.

The Northern Mandap (with lot of Sivalingams) - from where we saw the
Foreigner with Hat - is being dismantled.

The outer Wall of the Mandap (long corridor - almost to the length of
the Temple) was built partly on the Mandap roof which served as
counter-weight. Disregarding this Civil Engineering feat, the
Contractors undertaking the task HAVE JUST BROKEN the mandap roof ..

Imagine this happening to a UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE MONUMENT !

Heart is bleeding to see this Site - sight.

Pillars bearing Inscriptions are also broken to pieces !

What Malikgafur could not accomplish, our Contractors could ..

Paining... We should definitely raise mass opinion on this.

sps
