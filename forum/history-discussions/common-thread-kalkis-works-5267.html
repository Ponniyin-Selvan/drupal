Venkatesh,

Good Topic to discuss. As Thiru has suggested, lets start a fresh mail
for each thread instead of replying to the old one or changing the
subject of the old one. Hence this new mail.

In almost all his stories, the hero will be hunted for by someone or
some group and the hero will be hiding or running to save himself.

abathuthavigal.
Magudapathi - He is a freedom fighter and hence is in hideout
throughout the story. Also, the vilains chase him to kill him
Solaimalai ilavarasi - Same as above. Freedom Fighter and hence is in
hideout.
Kalvanin Kadhali - kekave venam. Being a thief the hero is in hideout.
Alai Osai - Again Freedom struggle, so hideout.

Another common thing is - all stories will have a water body being
descibed. Either a stream or river or kulam or odai.

There will be surely a scene related to food, eating.

Even in Kalvanin Kadhali, Kalyani's athai is (partially) deaf. :)

Regards,
Satish

VANAKKAM,

This is my first mail to the group.interesting topic to
analyse as tis seems analysing kalki himself........

"FORESTS" are highlighted in kalki's works.many interesting and
some turning points in the novel takes place in forests.eg.
in PS
1.ilaiya pandian MUDISOOTU VAIBAVAM by NANDINI in
THIRUPURAMBIYAM PALLIPADAI.
2.vanthiyathevan's first meeting with arulmozhivarmar
happens in a forest.
3.in third part nandini meeting rakkammal ,poonguzhali and
amuthan driving ravidasan into pudhaikuzhi.

in parthiban kanavu
1."vikraman in disguise as a vairavyabaari meets some
unexpected dangers because of kullan"in second part .this also takes

place in a forest.

2."sivanadiyaar discloses that he is nobody other than
the maamalla chakravarthi himself to ponnan " in a forest.
3.neelakesi kidnaps arulmozhi rani and holds her in a
forest where later sivanadiyaar goes and finds nearer to a

waterfalls.

in sivahaamiyin sabatham
1.naaganandi takes away sivagami after she comes out of
the surangapathai along with aayanar and enters a graveyard "
and sivahaami's home itself is forest.

in kalvanin kaadhali
1.muthaiyan after transforming into a thief lives in
forest.and he is caught by the police in forest.

another hold is "YAANAI"
1.in PS , veerapadiya aabathuthavivahalin sathi towards
arulmozhivarmar in the form of yaanai in thiruvaarur,aniruthar

threatening vaanathi while she travels to nagai soodaamani
vihaaram,in second part ponniyin selvan's most of the journey is
on yaanai as yaanaipagan evan in the last when he turns the
elephant violent and escapes from the gang with
poonguzhali ,etc.even in kanavu poonguzhali travels
over "INDIRANATHU IRAVATHAM".

2.in SS the very first scene in which paranjothi saves
sivagaami.
3.in PK when paarthiban is nearing his last moments.his
PATTATHU YAANAI dies witha huge trumpet.

OTHER THING TO BE NOTED IS IN EVERY HISTORICAL NOVEL
THERE WILL BE A POOR FAMILY CLOSE TO THE KING'S FAMILY (KNOWING ALL

THE RAJARAHASIYANGAL) AND WORKING FOR THEIR WELFARE.

eg
1.so many characters in PS ,senthan amuthaninn thaayar loyal
to sembiyan maadevi,murugaiyan who later holds bakthi on ilavarasar

amv,poonguzhali close to ilavarasar arul mozhi varmar, all ordinary
amilies

2."PONNAN,VALLI,VEERABATHIRA AACHAARI" in paarthiban
kanavu ,ordinary padhotti and kammaalan.
3."KANNAN,KAMALI,THAATHA" vey close to mahendrar and maamallar-
arasar's ratha saarathi

i haven't read other books of kalki.eager to know
more.........
