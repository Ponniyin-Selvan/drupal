ecr trip

hi venkat! alas i am back after sending back the minister for tourism
and culture. it is strange that all our ministers are hounourable
ministers indeed.

there are or there were numerous sites of iron age around chengalpttu.
but many are now in the extensions of madras like prumbakkam,.
nanmangalam etc.,

on the Tindivanam road is Sanur, which was excavtaed long back and now
well fenced and preserved. it is right on the road. there is perumbair
kandigai, landmark is the cement hanuman statue near thozhuppedu.

but for real exciting trip please visit Gudiyam a palaeolithic rock
shelter (visit www.sharmaheritage.com details). You know the area 20x20 km around Poondi reservoir was thickly
habited by palaeolithic man!

gift sir is correct. even there is a theory that the iron age persons
were tank-builders!!!

see mamallpuram in detail - newly excavated temple, the punjeri
inscription (near bypass) giving the names of thanchchans. the alignment
of the road was changed due to the effort of Kodumudi Shanmugam.

sriraman
