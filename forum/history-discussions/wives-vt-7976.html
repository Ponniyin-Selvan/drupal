Dear vj

At kaniyakumari near partivapuram and vizilanjam there is hill called Tirumalai birth place of tirumalai naiyakar.this hill is surrounded by Thamiraparani river (kulithurai aaru)

This place was important place for their maritime activity with lakshadeep and maladive.

nearest this place only a place called kalingarajapuram where we can see a kotheswar temple, which was revocered from the sand dune at the depth of 7 meters.temple site is 3 km away from the sea .

note

Munchiarai - Thirumalai Siva Temple:Legend says that Sri Rama worshipped here. History records the association of Thirumalai Nayyakkar's birth with this temple. Top of a small hillock (where Purusha Mirugham originally worshipped Siva) twin temples in one temple complex reside - one for Siva and theleft one for Vishnu. There are beautiful carvings and sculptures.
