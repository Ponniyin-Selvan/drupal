In my feelings,Ellora more or less resembles Mahabalipuram.Never
know may be Pulikesin took the inspiration from Mammallapuram and
could have built it.But it is real wonder.

Ellora is quite different from in the treatment and methodology -
sometimes even richer. It was the work of rashtrakutas

For a striking similarity, compare mahamallapuram and badami caves +
structural temples). The cave temples are sort of comparisons to
m.puram but the structural temples are strikingly similar. Esp there
is one structure which is quite same as the shore temple(s). This
observation of mine is quite primitive by the way and awaits
scholars confirmation.

And as you know badami temples are older than mahamallapuram - they
were built by mangalesan the villan of pulikesin's life.

And one will be forced change the opinion of the villanic pulikesin
after visiting badami.

And after razing badami to flames, narasimha retured - leaving all
those beautiful sculptures intact and without inflicting slightest
damage.

Compare this with the M.Kafur & associated invations of later times.
