Dear Friends,

Because Kamal & his www.varalaaru.com team mates ( Lavanya - Gokul,
Krupa & Ram) are travelling extensively, I take the previlege to
announce the launching of the Spl issue (7th) of www.varalaaru.com by
this Sunday morning. Earlier Dr. Kalaikkovan's research book on
worshipping of female dieties was released by attended.
Pre-function press coverage in The Hindu (Fri - Trichy Edn) and
Dinamani (Sun - Tri Edn) was very good.

Sunday morning around 10 am the programme was commenced at complex, adjacent to Big temple, Thanjavur. Dr.Kalaikkovan
released "Penn Deyva vazhipadu Thotramum valarchiyur" book. Publisher
and other connected people were praised by Dr. Kkvn.
Krupa has brought out a "Vennba".

Kkvn's brother Arasu, rendered speaches. Dr. Nalini gave vote of thanks.

Rajarajeswaram spl CD was released by one among the first to receive ..

Gokul presented the details of archieving and reconstructing
paintings ..

It is pertinent to note here that Dr.Airavatham Mahadevan, who spent
all his life time earnings for History - has created a Trust in the
name of his Manaseeka Guru - Dr. Ku Ra Sreenivasan - who is an
Authority in Cave temples and Dr.Rasamanickanar Historical Research
Centre - under the able leadership of Dr. Kalaikkovan - is conducting
the Annual seminar under the Trust. Previously Dr. Subbbarayalu
(Thanjai Tamil Univ, HOD History - also Dr. Nalini's guide for PhD),
Dr. Sathiamoorthy (Head of calibre have only rendered Lectures.

It is a great pleasure to note that our Gokul is bestowed the honour
of rendering his Lecture this year. Details of his Lecture and
presentation are avl in www.varalaaru.com 7th issue.

Scholars like Dr.Kalikkovan, Dr. Kudavoil Balasubramanian, Dr.
Nalini, Dr. Akila and Dr. Rajavelu (ASSI Chennai) were stunned by the
meticulous presentation by Gokul - which is pertaining to Archieving
and reconstructing paintings of the past with Historical importance.
Gokul presented a power-plant demo and the entire audience were spell
bound.

It is a fusion of Technology & History ... A major contribution by
our group to the cause of History ..
Ram's speech was excellent .. though very casual, he brought out that
the unknown faces about a couple of years back are bonded by the
might of Kalki's writings and today something more purposeful is
achieved ... Very much realistic..
Kamal did the compairing.. very brief and to the point ..
The Function went on till 2 pm. After lunch, many of the members were
guided by

Sankaran & some of their friends attended. Well Organised by our team.

The Team has already covered Kailasanathar temple, some Pallava cave
temples, Chidambaram, Kumbakonam and Nandhipuram prior to this
programme.. and collected lot of details / photographs..

Detailed messages will flow shortly from the Team members...

The entire experience is thrilling ...

PLEASE FIND TIME TO GO THROUGH www.varallaru.com (7th issue) and
opine..

Dear Satish,

Thanks for your kind words. Re Historical Institute under the
guidance of any Scholar - including Dr. nagaswamy, Dr.Kalaikkovan or
Dr. Balu, the findings, benefits, copy rights and
similar such issues will vest with such Scholars and even our casual
observations are bound to marr their reputations.

THE FRAME WORK WE ARE DISCUSSING IS SIMPLE :

WE ARE NOT GOING TO UNEARTH HISTORY - OR PROTECT HISTORY - OR GOING
TO DO ANY RESEARCH IN HISTORY. Such persons can always chose the
Scholars of their choice for such majore accomplishments.

We are planning to ARCHIEVE (Record in a format which will provide
the same atleast till 2100 AD)History... With the help of modern
technologies.

Such facility should be accessible to all the Scholars - freely and
with ease. And we also should be in a position to access such
Scholars with ease. More over, we are planning to take up assignments
with affliations with all such existing set-ups including RMHRC, INTAC and
so on. Hence some Institute of ours is needed.

Satish, a week back, NDTV carried out an interview with Bombay
Tiffinwalas.. Those collecting - segrecating - distributing -
collecting back - redistributing tiffin carriers to the office-goers
of Mumbai are handling about 200000 tiffin carriers in a span of
about 10 hrs on day-to-day basis, with the help of less educated
workforce - hailing from the lands who fought war in Support of
Chatrapathi Sivaji! Their failure rate is about 2 - 3 carriers
missing a month - taken away by the hungry ones! They Charge about
Rs. 300 pm. Means Rs. 6 Crores turn over. Employing about 2500
persons - in suburban trains of Mumbai.

Mind boggling. very much precise .. No computers. purely manual..

To overcome Union problems, all of them are SHARE HOLDERS in this
Business... and each one is earning about 5 digit figures!

Like-wise, unless we make all of History lovers as share holders -
shares by means of labour & materials - in our Proposed Institute, we
may not be able to achieve results - because we are aiming to bridge
the gap of about 100 yrs... those collected by the British and left
unattended to for the past one long Century in the post-independent
India.

Sorry, you are providing thread of something, of which some of us are
aware and others are yet to be.. The time to discuss this is not yet
ripe.

on Gokul's presentation.
