Summary of infant arumozhi discussions :

* Thiruvalangadu plates state that vanavan mahadevi took the
funeral
pyre leaving an infant behind - no reference to who this infant is.
Sadasiva pandarathar quotes the exact lines but does not say who
this kuzhavi is.

* Prof.GR sent another reference from Dr.RN's book - a tamil
potetic
inscription - also saying that vanavan mahadevi had a child in her
hand when she left for the pyre. Infact the way the depiction is
done (surandha mulaip paal etc.) is exactly the same in both cases -

raising suspition that the same poet did both things.

* Who is this child is the question.

* If we take it that it is RR, then it puts few things to rest and
raises a few questions. It explains RR's affection for kundavi who
might have raised him in place of mother. It explains uttama
getting
throne.

* But it contradics Thiruvalangadu plates stat that RR left the
throne in favour of mathuranthaka inspite of people's wishes. The
age of RR also becomes questionable. Sundara died around 971 AD and
in 985 - RR assumed throne. RR will become too young if this theory
is accepted.

* The plates as well as inscriptions do not quote who this infant
is.
