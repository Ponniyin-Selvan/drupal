I saw this topic. I know it is old but wish to give my opinion.

Mukkulathor (3 clans) - Maravar, Kallar and Agamudayar

Maravar - Subcategory will be KaruMaravar,IlaMaravar, SembiNattuMaravar,KondayamKottaiMaravar etc. The Maravar generally use the title Thevar. This is a title given to a person or his family for heroic deeds. It is also a title which reflects the authority of the person. High population of Maravarscan be found in areas like Ramanathapuram, Sivaganga, Madurai, Tirunelveli.

Kallar - They probably form the majority in the Mukkulathor. They have Pattai Peyar. People of the same Pattai Peyar do not intermarry. Examples of Pattai Peyar are Thondaiman, Sendapriyar, Alampriyar, Kandapillai, Vandayar,Kandiyar, Pinnudar. The Kallars form the majority in Thanjavur and Pudukottai. The Piranmalai Kallar are the oldest tribe in India.

Agamudayar - Many Agamudayar use the title Servai or Servaikaran. The Marudu Pandiar brothers belong to the Agamudayar clan. There is a another category of Agamudayar known as Raja Kulam Agamudayar. They are believed to be ofroyal lineage.

Regards,

Sharmalan
