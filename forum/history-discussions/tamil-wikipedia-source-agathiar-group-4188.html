--- In agathiyar@yahoogroups.com, "C.R. Selvakumar" wrote:

Dr. JayBee, thank you indeed for your kind words.

Although Tamil Wikipedia stands 4th among Indian languages in terms
of the number of articles, it stands first in terms of all the
quality measures.

Language Total size |Mean bytes | 0.5k | 2.0k
Tamil 22M 4176 64% 16%
Telugu 20M 810 9% 2%
Bengali 20M 1656 23% 4%
Marathi 9.1M 1401 20% 4%

Bengali and Telugu recently raced past Tamil in the total number of
articles, but most of them appear to be one-line type of entries.
The important thing is how useful it would be for people who are
going to be using these resources. With so many knowledgeable
agathiyars here, and with Dr. JayBee's blessings, it should be
possible to substantially enhance the Tamil Wikipedia. Once again
thanks to Dr. JayBee.

anbudan selvaa

Dear Jaybee and all the agathiyars,

Let me wish you all a Very Happy New Year and advance greetings for a
happy Pongal.

About Wikipedia, I believe it is one of the MOST important
developments and it is up to us Tamils to make it a most valuable
resource. Literally everyone can make a decent contribution.
Dr. Jaybee, I'm sure, can make an IMMENSE contribution. siRu thuLi
peru veLLam. The quality of information is extremely important, as
much as quantity and diversity of information. I had made about 2000

edits (approx), since joining in mid 2006. There are only a few
who are regularly contributing. Among the indian languages, Telugu is

leading with 26,000 plus articles, followed by Bengali 12,700+,
Marathi 7,000+ and in the fourth place Tamil with 5,800+. closely
followed are Hindi Wiki with 4,499, Kannada 4,300+. Malayalam has
1,900+ and Sanskrit 1,000+.

Please take a look at the list of Wikis at

http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/List_of_Wikipedias#10_000.2B_articles

I would STRONGLY encourage everyone to come forward and make
contributions to tamil Wikipedia. Please make a difference!

Let 2007 be the year the Tamil wiki will cross 10K articles
(actually crossing 20K or even 30K is not a problem if more
voluteers would come forward)

Once again Dr. Jaybee, wishing you sincerely a long healthy life and
deep satisfaction and happiness in life.

anbudan selvaa

"jaybee555"

Dear Friends,
I am nowadays doing some other things also which I feel
is also worthwhile.
I check out the Wikipidia entries about Tamil, Tamilnadu,
and Tamil History.
Wherever I feel that the information is not enough, I add
up whatever is relevent.
Just a little run, add up to a useful and reliable database.
I hope to make new full entries also with my own writings
and researches.
I was doing touch-up jobs on and off previously.
One day, I found a list of empires of the world.
In that list, the Cheras were there. But not the Pandyas, nor
the Cholzas.
I opened up the Pandya section.
Likewise there were numerous things not written about Tamil.
I found miserably little on Kilzavan Sethupathi.
But his coin was found with a coin collector from Spain.
He had bought it from a collector from France.
Nothing in the Web about him.
Now, this is the short-fall, short-coming that I would like
to correct.
I would like to ask you people to help.
Thats what I have always done.
But I know that you will NOT help.
You will keep SILENT.
You will NOT care.
So......
Let me save myself the trouble of asking and be insulted with
stony silence.
I will do what I can do.
Thats what I have always done.
One day you will bow your heads in shame for not rendering
help for a noble and selfless non-profittable service.
If at all you have any conscience, of course.

Regards
JayBee
