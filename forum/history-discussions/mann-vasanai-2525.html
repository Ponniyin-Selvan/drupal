Folks,

I was waiting for Saravanan/Suganya to post on yesterday's meet.
Looks like they are busy. Me being relatively, though I could post.

Kamal a.k.a Adhitha Karikalan, A.P.Saravanan, Arvind, Suganya and
myself met up in forum and invariably all of us wondered why we dint

do it earlier. We had a great time discussing Thanjai Periya koil,
Kalki, Nandini, Devaram, Sundarar, SB sir, Kalaikkovan etc. Couple
of hours flew like minutes. While I shall continue to wait for
others to post a detailed mail. Let me just post this.

We were planning to visit a Chola temple near Bangalore
(Thirunandhi eeswaram) to get a better feel for architecture,

sculpture and inscriptions. We were planning do it this Saturday.
The plan is still loosely held as many of us dint know if it is
viable. If any other bangalorean is interested in joining us, pls
let us know by tomorrow. We can plan and visit the beautiful ASI
maintained later chola temple this saturday.

regards,
Ram
