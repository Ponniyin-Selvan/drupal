research work. He informed me that in one of the Nicobar Islands there is a
temple built by Raja Raja Cholan. This island is mentioned as nangavaram in
old Tamizh Literature it seems. But unfortunately this is not accessible as
its a dense forest. Its a 2-3 day travel in the island to reach the temple.
Any idea / inputs about this temple?
