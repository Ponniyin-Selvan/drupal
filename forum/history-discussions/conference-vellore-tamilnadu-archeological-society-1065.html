Hi Friends,

Tamilnadu Archeological Society, Chennai is conducting a two days
conference in Vellore on 19th and 20th of July. Lot of researchers
are going to participate and many research papers are going to be
submitted. Every year they are conducting in various places of
tamilnadu and this year it is in Vellore fort. Approximately 125
people are going to attend.

Mr. Rajavelu of contacted me last week. He asked for some contribution or compliments
