Ponniyin Selvan has been released in Project Madurai for the Tamil
New Year. See the announcement below.

Thanks a lot to the wonderful volunteer team who made this timely
release possible. Everyone who made up the team were so happy to
participate - needless to say enjoying to read Ponniyin Selvan again.

My previous (first) email to our group got more volunteers than
I had expected and so I could not accomodate everyone in the team.

I hope you will all enjoy (again) reading this classic. Feel free
share this news with your friends.

Every effort has been made to correct the errors and format the text. If t=
here were any missed, please send an email to anbumani@to fix this.

Also enjoy reading the other works in Project Madurai. Ponniyin Selvan bec=
ame the 169th work released in Project Madurai. If you would like to contri=
bute any other work to Project Madurai, kindly do so.

Any comments/criticisms ? Do write to me.

Regards,

Anbu.
