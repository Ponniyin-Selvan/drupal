Hellow Everybody,

How come so far we have not touched the famous M A H A M A H A M
(Read forward or back ward or see mirror reflection :: everything
same!) to be celebrated in our Kudandai on the 6th March?

Last Sunday I visited the Mahamaham tank:

They were filling water brought from various rivers all over India
(Thanks to Actor Rajini) mixed with Cauvery water brought from near
by sources;

The sixteen wells located inside the tank are suitably identified and
name boards displayed;

Visitors already started thronging the town / tank;

Police restrictions / hoardings / well-come signs displayed
prominantly;

Thanjavur - Kundadai broad guage conversion is over and trials being
conducted;

On Madras road - near Koranattu-karuppur / on Thanjavur road near
Darasuram / on Mayavarm road near KV pettai a new bye-pass road -
OUTER RING ROAD - is already put into use in less than 4 months time

Near these points all the vehicles will be terminated from the 29th
Feb and van services will be put into use till the 5th. On 5th, 6th
& 7th Mar only cycle-rickshaws will play for senior citizens. Others
will have to walk the distance. No Vehicle will be permitted.

No new lodging house(s) constructed (unlike for World Tamil
Conference in Thanjavur a few years back). I have noticed one Hotel
Paradise - Veg. Restaurant - near Sarangapani temple - as a new
addition. Other wise Kudandai remains the same. Some encroachments
removed. Town looks very neat - free of garbages.

Sign boards display the pilgrimage spots around Kudandai with a route
map and distances marked.

Noticed foreign tourists roaming around in South Indian costumes!

Kumudham Bakthi has brought out a Mahamaham special :: Temples around
Kudandai.

Fondly, SB

thats nice that u start this topic..

i was born and brought up in kumabakonam. i still remember my good old
school days ( previous mahamaham ) in which i took bath in mahamaham
tank at 3 AM in the morning .. after that i viewed everything lively in
CABLE TV... That was a great experience. all the houses in kumbakonam
will be filled with relatives & friends. schools will be closed for
more than 20 days ( what else u want in school days ) .. kumbakonam used
to get as much government projects as possible ...
As u all know kumbakonam is a temple town. sorry to say that i donot
know much abt the historical importance of each temple but sure

1. Nagaeswaran temple
2. sakrapani temple
3. sarangapani temple.
4. Ramaswamy temple
5. swamimalai
6. uppliappan kovil
7. thiru nagaeswaram
8 . Thirubuvanam
9. Darasuram
10 . Thriuvidai maruthur
11. thiru valan chulzi
12. Mahamaham tank
13 . Kumbeswarar Temple
14 Allangudi
15 . Suriyanar Kovil
16 . valangaiman
17 Patteswaram
18. Baburajapuram ............................etc

are worth to see many times ..
Each & every youth in kumbakonam have handful of stories to talk abt
these places ( ofcourse not much of its historical importance but abt
their day to today school , college life ) . these are the meeting
places for friends ( daraswaram is the meeting place for us to escape
from the so called parent torture for studyies ) , study rooms for many
( i still remember those days in which i had studied with my friends in
the second floor of the swamimalai temple) , poothi maram for few ,
romance place for few , walking place for few ... etc. perhaps all the
important decision of the youth life were taken on the floors of these
temples only .

During exam times sure these gods are very busy .. many of the friends
still used to say that they passed the exams bcos of the grace of
sakrapani.

i used to feel that we do not have sujatha ( kind of writers ) to make
these stories popular similar to srirengathu devathigal (of sujatha ) .

Mahamaham is always special for kumbakonam ppl except for the killing
of 45 - 50 PPL in the previous one ..

Hope i will get to know more abt the historical importance of my town
thro this thread.

cheers,
Kamal ( K2) .

-Dear,

Forgot. Tomorrow (25th Feb) the Mahamaham festival starts with
Ganesh pooja at 7 pm.

Flag hoisting in Aadhi Kumbeswarar - Nageswarar - Kasiviswanathar -
Abhimukeswarar :: Siva temples on the 26th;

Flag hoisting in Sarangapani - Sakkarapani - Ramasamy - Aadhivaraha -
perumal temples on the 27th;

10 days Uthasavam in these temples.

All the above plus 9 other (Koteeswara - Kambatta viswanatha -
kalahastheeswara - ekambara- someswara - Guthameswara -
Banapureswara - Amirthakalasanatha & Rajagopalaswamy) temples
THEERTHA VARI ( Uthsava dieties going to the Mahamaham tank &
Cauvery) will be conducted on the 6th March.

Culturally we have been worshipping / thanksgiving to all the five
elements :: This is dedicated to Water!

Fondly, SB
