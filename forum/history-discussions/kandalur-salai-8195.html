Rajaraja-1 waged war on the Kandalursalai for th first time in A.D.988, and

the Cheras reinstated their positions again in Kandalursalai and continued
with the functions of their military acadamy and arsenal centre.

This made Rajaraja Chola-1 to wage war again on Kandalursali in the year
A.D.995, and effectively destroyed the Kandalursalai military acadamy and
arsenal centre, and defeated the ruling Aye king of Kerala country and
captured his capital the seaport city of Vilignam. "

victory was mentioned in his 4th year inscription and 'Kanthalur Salai
Kalamarutha...' in his Meikeerthi. He has 3 types of Meikeerthis and all
give the importance tothe first victory over chera. His 4th year is 988AD.

his inscriptions are found in Tirunangur. So Rajarajan won the Chera King in
988 AD. . Am I correct ? (Meen kuttiki neenja theriyumannu
