--- In ponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Sivapathasekaran"

INDIAN SCIENCE MONITOR ( ISM) (TKV Rajan) has organised the
function
as Archeologist / Editor to falicitate::
=============================

Very glad to inform you that the Felicitation function went on very
well this noon.

Dr. Sudharsana Natchiappan, Chairman, Parliamentary Committee
felicitated our dear Venkat, Dr. Kudavoil and Vijay Parthipan,
Tamil language enthusiast ...

TKV Rajan introduced the Chief Guest.
I invited all present.
Venkat's father, sister and wife did attend the function.

Dr. Thiaga Sathiyamurthy was present.

Our Venkat gave details of Career too in motivating him write Historical Works.. ". There was
a query as to who was the Foreinger... Venkat enthusasistically
discused several views, including a probability of the Europeon who
conducted Survey of India for the East India Company (ref our
Archieves !)

Dr. Sudarsanam Natchiappan observed that Tamil language, apart from
being a Literary language, does enjoy the special status of a
SCIENCE LANGUAGE AND TRADE-LANGUAGE .. and quoted from Jawaharlal
Nehruji's Discovery of India too ... and is willing to take up more
of supporting the cause of heritage preservation with those in
Power ...

With Ms. Kanimozhi MP, Hon'ble Minister Thangam Thennarasu, Minister
GK Vasan and now Dr. Sudarasanam Natchiappan being aware of our Group, our collective voice is like to be taken more seriously on
matters affecting our heritage and culture.

The exhibition is open at Parvathi Gallery (opp to State Bank of
Trivancore) in New # 28, Eldams Road, Alwarpet.

I am sure our friends will post the photos of the occasion sooner.

anbudan / sps
