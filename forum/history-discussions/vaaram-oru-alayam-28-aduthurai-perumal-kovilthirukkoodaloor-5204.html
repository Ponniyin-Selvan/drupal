the two aduthurais are called kurangaduthurais only- north and south.
actually two brothers are involved with the legends of the two
temples - the vanara brothers.

2. (Kurang)ADUTHIRAI - which is now known as ADUTHURAI near
Sooriyanar koil.

thenkurangkaduthurai was originally built by sugrivan. redone by
sembiyan madevi

3. Thirukkudaloor Vinnagaram Since this Vinnagaram is located
close to KAVISTHALAM (Kavi -
Monkey), there may be some connection with Soorianarkoil Aaduthurai

vada kurangaduthurai has a connection to vali whose image is on the
dilapitated rajagopuram.

THANK YOU DEAR NATARAJ PRAKASH.,

Do you know there are Three Aadudurai's in the Kumbknm region?

1. Mayiladuthurai (River - Cauvery - bank where Peacocks used to
dance ! Earlier it was known as Mayavaram !

2. (Kurang)ADUTHIRAI - which is now known as ADUTHURAI near
Sooriyanar koil. Where Monkeys used to Dance ! Interestingly this is
a vantage point.. there are 6 roads branching out to Mayiladuthurai,
Thiruvisalur, Thiruppanandhal, Thirubuvanam, Karaikkal and
Naachiarkoil. In the Aabath Sahayeswarar temple, there are Sembian
Madevi inscriptions.. There were VILLIs (Bowsmen - who were experts
in using Righthand - VALANGAI ; who were experts in using Left hand
IDANGAI. Our Group has discussed this in detail. The Villis were
taxed while transited their Arrows ! Details of these inscriptions
availble in Aaduthurai Siva Temple !

3. In Subject Thirukkudaloor Vinnagaram there is no mention related
to the said two Aduthurais !

Since this Vinnagaram is located close to KAVISTHALAM (Kavi -
Monkey), there may be some connection with Soorianarkoil Aaduthurai
