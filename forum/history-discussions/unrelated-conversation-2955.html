It makes it a lot more difficult if the Subjects are not changed
Second multilingual mails are even more painful....I am not trying to run a yes miss no miss class here but to have a quaorum so that everyone gains....

"R.venketesh" -Dear Mr. Eshwaran

with due respect I'd like to voice my opinion too

I noticed only handful of persons indulge in such "
Adra sakkai" correspondences.

the very handful have contributed some of the best of threads if
you look back at the archives.
if you count me in the handful I ll like to point out a few things.
most of these people were lucky enough to visit tanjore in the recent
trip as a group .
never before has so much friendship risen between so different
people. regardless of age or social chasms friendships were formed
only on the basis of love for kalki and the cholas.
amidst so many groups which have folded up their stuff and vanished
this group thrives and grows because of the home like atmosphere
thats been created.such playful banter and interaction helps to
create a great atmosphere in which knowledge and creativity is
fostered.
what some people want is a class room with yes miss no miss types but
such groups have vanished.
with warm regards
venketesh
