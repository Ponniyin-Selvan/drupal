Dear Gokul/All,

I know Mr. Venkatesh well, but at present Mr. Anna Kannan (whom also I
know) is in charge of tamil.sify.com. I mean, I had/have been in
nonprofessional contact with both of them, like Rajini Ramki.

What we see in tamil.sify.com is not hyperlinks to external sites,
except thisaigal.com which is a different arrangement. You wont find
links for vikatan, kumudham, etc. The other magazine links are within
the tamil.sify.com domain itself. They are the so called
"sitridhazhgal". Sify.com has an arrangement with those sitridhazgal
to bring their magazine content online. That is why they have allotted
a directory for each magazine.

But I feel persons following those links will generally have different
interests than that of varalaaru.com, though a link anywhere in Net
surely counts to increase varalaaru.com site's hit rate and visibility.

Regarding an article in Kalki, I will try my best.
