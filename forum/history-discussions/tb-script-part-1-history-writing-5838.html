The opening verse of the holy Kural puts across a thought to
think: As all scripts have the letter A for their first, so do
the world has the eternal primordial god for its first.

A, as its first of letters, every speech maintains; The "Primal Deity" is first through all the world's domains. (
Kural, 1 )
