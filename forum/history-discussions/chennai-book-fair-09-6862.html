Dear All,

I was in the Book Fair on Thursday as well as today. It is Huge.

On Thursday I had the fortune of meeting our Dhivakar Sir and NDTV Vaidyanathan Sir, along with Sampath Sir - Ex Hindu (Still...... by faith). That was a good 1-1/2 hr for me with them. I also had a chance to meet with "Thamizh Thenee" sir and Saravana Rajendran Sir of Min Tamil Group. It was a wonderful day. I purchased our Dhivakar Sir's Emden and Gokul's Rajakesari.

I also met our Venketesh Sir, on both the days. However the surprise was B Muruganantham, a good friend of mine, who, I did not know was a member of our group and had authored the book Feroze Gandhi.

Today I had a good Hunt and the following are the books that I purchased on these two days.

A. History 
 
1.Kumarik KaNdam (alladhu) Kadal KoNda ThennAdu - By Ka. Appadurai - Poompuhar Pathippagam
2.ThennAttu POrkkaLangaL - By Ka. Appadurai  - Poompuhar Pathippagam
3. 1800 ANduku muRpatta Thamizhagam - By Ka. Appadurai - Poompuhar Pathippagam
3.Pandiya VaralAru - By Sadasiva Bandarathar - Manivasagara Pathippagam
4.Thenkumariyin Kadhai - By A.Ka. Perumal - Thamizhini Pathippagam
5.Azhagar KOil - By Tho.Paramasivam - Padayal (Purchased from Niveditha Pathippagam)
6.Pallava seppEdugaL 30 - Edited by P.Sri and T.N Subramanian - Ulagath Tamizh Araaichi Niruvanam
7. Sangath Tamizharin vazhipAdum saDangugaLum - M Shanmugam Pillai - Ulagath Tamizh Araaichi Niruvanam
8. SangakAla mannar kAla nilai varalARu - By VP Purushottam - AinthiNai Pathippagam
9. Tamizh Ilakkiya VaralARu - By M Varadarajan - Sahitya Academy
10. Ki Mu Ki Pi - By Madhan - Kizhakkup Pathippagam
11. Madarasapattinam - By KRA Narasaiah - Pazhaniappa Brothers

B. Biographies
1. Feroze Gandhi - By B.Muruganatham - The Avenue Press
2. En dEsam en vAzhkkai - By LK Advani (Translated by Vasanthan Perumal) - Alliance Publishers

C. Books Related to Religion
1. PurAdhana India ilakkiya varalARRuk kalaichol kaLanjiyam - By K Krishnamachariar - Narmadha Pathippagam
2. SilappadhikAramum Divyaprabandhamum - By A Seenivasan - Pazhaniappa Brothers
3. Acharya Hrudayam - By Purushottama Naidu - Madras University Publications

D. Historical Novels
1. SMS Emden 22-09-1914 - By Dhivakar - Pazhaniappa Brothers
2. Rajakesari - By S Gokul - Pazhaniappa Brothers
3. Kadal PuRa - Sandilyan - Vaanathi Pathippagam
4. Yavana Rani - Sandilyan - Vaanathi Pathippagam
5. Raja Mutthirai - Sandilyan - Vaanathi Pathippagam
6. CheramAn kAdhali - Kannadasan - Vaanathi Pathippagam

9. KaLLazhagar Kaadhali  - Sri Venugopalan - Poompuhar Pathippagam
10. VolcAvilirundhu Gangai Varai - Rahul Sankruthyayan (Translated in Thamizh)- Thamizh putthagAlayam
11. VanchimAnagaram & KabAdapuram - Naa Paarthasaarathy - Sri Senbaga Pathippagam

E. Other Novels
1. Gopalla Gramam - Ki Rajanarayanan - Annam Publishers
2. Gopallapuratthu MakkaL - Ki Rajanarayanan - Annam Publishers
3. Swamy &  Friends - RK Narayan - Indian Thought Publications
4. Malgudi Days - RK Narayan - Indian Thought Publications

F. Other Books
