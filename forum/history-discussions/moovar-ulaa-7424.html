Dear Sivapathasekaran,

I am sure 'most' of the PSVP Forum Members might already know of the "Moovar Ulaa" a compilation of three 'Ulaas' namely, the "Vikkrama Cholan Ulaa", "Kulothunga Cholan Ulaa", and "Rajaraja Cholan Ulaa", composed by the theTamil Poet 'Ottakkooththar' of the Medieval Chola period.

They speak on the glory of the following Chola Kings namely,

(1) Vikkrama Cholan : A.D.1118-1135 - Son of Kulothunga Cholan - 1

(2) Kulothunga Chola - 2 : A.D.1133-1150 - Son of (1)

(3) Rajaraja Cholan - 2 : A.D.1146-1163 - Son of (2)

and are of much Historical value.

Hence for the benefit of the 'New Members' who are doing Research Study on the these kings, and others who may be interested in same, I provide below the URL of the Website where these could be referred to:

http://www.infitt. org/pmadurai/ pm_etexts/ pdf/pm0116. pdf

Trust same will be of much use to them.

Virarajendra
