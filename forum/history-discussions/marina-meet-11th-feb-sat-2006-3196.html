Dear Friends,

Uma & her brother Chandrasekar, Ram, Venkat, Swetha, Venkatesan
Kasirajan, Keerthivasan (new member) Muruganandham, Satish (and leter
his siter & brother-in-law) were already present when I visited them
behind Gandhi Statue around 530 pm, 30 mts behind the Schedule. Ram's
friend and Krupa joined later. Around 730 pm Dr. Rajavelu us. He was fresh from the excvn site - after furnishing the details
of Kolappakkam to the Media - maeaswaran has cited in the Group
already and The Hindu (Chennai Edn) reported in detail tdy Page 16.

Thread was picked from Earlier Thanjai trip - interpretation of
paintings and History data etc.. Formation of Kalkifans Group to
discuss widely other Non-Characters, dialogues etc., getting thinned at some point..
Availability of very rich data in Agathiar group etc., were discussed.
It was also pointedout that none of the Group while joining Kalkifans. And to utilise the platform provided by
Kalkifans to discuss things other than varalaaru / heritage also by
all the

Re Moderator/s, instead of singular system a collective responsibility
and ceasing the necessity to moderate etc., were also discussed..

As usual, at Micro level, udayarkudi inscriptions - Whether Sembianmadevi was the first to convert Brickthali to Katrali,
Uthama's Non-Involvement in AK's Murder etc., were discussed.

The need to update thebigtemple.com by involving the articles from the
Scholars by a select Group of Ram - Gokul - Swetha - Krupa and
circulate the website for private viewing of Sri, Sofia Madam and
other Senior members (omissions inadvertant) before unfolding for
public view etc., were discussed.

Ram recalled as to how he casually started this Group and looking back
how many mile stones are created by the Group and looking forward
towards more to achieve ..

It was opined that Primarily Kalki was keeping Swadesi in his mind
when he wrote (during the British Rule) Parthibankanabu - Sivagamiyin
Sabatham and Ponniyinselvan on the Historical, Thiagabhoomi etc., on
the Social side and such Swadeshi was not the present South Indian or
Indian, but the PRIDE ANCIENT Chozha - Chera - Pandya - Pallava Desis.

With Kalki being bonding factor - PATRIOTISM - LOVE TOWARDS MOTHER
LAND - obviously assumes significance and things related to History /
heritage / Literature more discussed in Kalkifans.

Dr.Rajavelu discussed more on national and international archlgy.

Around 8 pm we started dispersing. Interesting meet.
