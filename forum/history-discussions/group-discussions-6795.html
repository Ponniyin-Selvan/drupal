Dear Friends,

For those who have been contributing for a long time ( say more than
a couple of years atleast), we are well aware we are avoiding
discussions leading to conflict among fellow members.

For those who joined recently, it has been regularly pointed out to
go through the discussios in the archieves to have a feel of the
forum..

Specifically the first 300 mails re discusios on Kalki's influence of the magnun opum which has caused us to assemble here.

No doubt we discuss so many things outside Literature... more of
History, temple activities, traditions, contemporaries, etc., etc.

Of late we are seeing members complain that a THREAD (TOPIC) started
get derailed ..due to side-tracking the issue ...

And for that the COUNTER is let us discuss FACTs or WHAT IS WRAPPED
UNDER THE CARPET....

We, the moderators, are aware of the freedom of expression of the
members ...
But in a Group Harmony is important..

Discussion on Casteism definitely leads to disruption in Harmony ..

More or less the Values cherished in the Group are highlighted in our
Home Page ... Casteism does not find a place there.

When Dr.Kailasam commenced this thread - soliciting more details on
the LIFE OF T V S ) - I felt very heppy because I read the Biography
of Hundai maker :: THE PATH PAVED WITH GOLD..

Definitely such attempt on a Auto Giant is much needed to motivate
our younger generation ...

The discussion so far produced the basic data that TVS maintained
PANCTUALITY well.. which I am sure even Kailasam is aware and most
of us (I travelled in TVS Buses to my college to Trichy from
Pudukottai - which leaves on time whether there was a Passenger or
Not) are also aware..

But the subject is taking a twist on Caste based discussion .. and
about 20 mails back it was reaching a close naturally and suddenly
again coming up..

FRIENDS, PLEASE BE REMINDED THAT HARMONY IS OUR PRIORITY IN OUR
GROUP ...

Just because we love Ponniyinselvan, it does not mean that EVERYONE
shares all the OTHER IDEALS of all the other members...

For discussing such ideals, there may be Groups available in the net.

Please avoid discussions here..

New members are joining thinking that we are discussing more of
Ponniyinselvan ...

Let us keep atleast 2 - 3 topics going on in this direction ..

I am again and again pained that there is no response to Vairam's
SANGAM LITERATURE or Nataraj's Vaaram Oru Alayam or his audio support
for Kalki's works.

I also see and realise that Moderator Krupa is silent more than the
others. Gokul is preoccupied.. Dr. Sridhar is also very busy due to
his extensive travelling to his work place.

Venkat and me are only vitually keeping the keys of Moderation.

It is painful to put this kind of mail in our Group - which is
considered one among the ELITE in the Literary - History Forum.

Worried these discussions will tend the Scholarly participants too as
Silent members and we will miss to share the best of their knowledge.

Dr. Kailsam Sir, please collect the date through Private e-mailing
which I also will support.

As of now, the discussion on this thread comes to an end.

I request members not to post further on this topic, since such mails
will be deleted without notice.

sps

to
be part of this.

allowed for present /past people, no community /caste related
discussions ( we know where we are headed in the first thread
itself).

go through all this mud slinging. yes rrc our hero had 13 -15 wives,

we take from these discussions is up to each individual. we all have
our own bloated egos and no amount of arguing in such a forum is
going

are not banned here but you got to have the maturity to know what to
say and what not to say. - this forum is dedicated to ponniyin

history of south east asia. A few contemporary posts are ok - but i
