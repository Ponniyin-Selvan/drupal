1.Why should aditha karikalan commit suicide after hearing that

Pazhuvettarayar

***Aditha karikalan dies because nadhini says a secret about veera
pandiyan.She says "Avar enathu....".After knowing this secret
aditha
karikalan commits suicide.What could have nandhini told?By the
logic
of the story veera pandiyan could have only been father,lover or
uncle to nandhini.Aditha karikalan was under the impression that
veerapandiyan was nandhini's lover.So he wouldnt have commited
suicide hearing that.What else could she have told?

She couldnt have said anything apart from "Avar enathu
appa/lover/uncle/family friend/husband...".None of these is earth
shaking news.Already karikalan suspected veerapandiyan was
nandhini's lover,so had she said 'husband' he wouldnt have been
surprised.So I think this is one major weakness of the story.

2. Mandhakini's liason with the Pandya king was when she was in a
post traumatic stressed state and amnesic....The whole emphasis of
her love for her former lover is showered on arulmozhi....Maybe she
has no recollection of her twins at all

*********Then it means pandya king physically exploited a mentally
traumatic woman.I dont think a tamil king would have done like
that.The availaible options here are

1.mandakini tried to forget sundara chola by having a new life for
herself.

2.pandya king exploited a mentally affected woman

If first is the case,then mandahini should have remembered her
children.That did not happen.If second is the case then it means
veerapandiya's character is very bad.

3. Stress and Adrenaline will make a man do anything because it is
life or death so Madhuranthagan fighting with vigour is acceptable
and chinna Pazhuvettarayar unsheaths his sword but did not defeat
periayavar...

****mathuranthakan could have had vigour,but where did he get the
technique from?And we have to remember that kandamaran had thrown a
spear on mathuranthaka and maturanthaka was literally shown as
dead.Further maturanthakas body is shown as falling from a steep
waterfall.Kalki titles that chapter as 'maturanthakan maraivu'.

After mathranthakan was hurt like this,he must have taken a long
time to recover.And he learning swordfight and fighting with
paluvettarayar after this accident in a short time looks a bit
doubtful.He fought with paluvettarayar on the coronation day of
uthama chola,which happened quickly after this incident.

we can say the spear hurt maturanthaka only marginally and he
escaped unhurt and learn warfare with vigour.

But kalki writes kandamaran threw the spear with full force.So we
cannot dismiss the wounds to be light.
