Dear Gokul & Kamal,

Today I scanned through Udayarkudi inscriptions again.
I am furnishing a jist :

On the SOUTH WALL of Central Shrine of Ananteeswaraswami temple,
as
per South India Inscriptions (SII) records of 1920:

Swasathishree Kopparakesari (2nd yr) Uthama Chola & Adithakarikala - Parakesari - Rajaraja were Rajakesari:

These Records are offerings by various persons for burning a
perpetual lamp - called NONDA VILAKKU (mostly in memory of a
departed
soul - even now, we call it Motshadeepam : Personally some of us
did
this after the fateful 11th Sept 2001 massacre. The gift was
either
12.5 Kazhanju gold for buying 96 sheep, from whose ghee, the
lamp
would be burnt perpetually : as per inscription "till Suryan and
Chandran are there?! - "Charadithar ullavarai")

Record #537: Nakkan Pattalaki 12.5 Kazhanju gold -for
Nondavilakku;
540: "Merkezhundu arulina devar Kandradithadevar Deviyar
Mazhavarayar
magalar parandagan Madevadigalarana SEMBIAN MADEVIYAR .." gifted
96
sheep for burning a perpetual (Nonda vilakku) lamp;
541:Servant Woman Diran Tirali 12.5 K gold; 545: Desavalavan Suriyan of Kodandarama THERINJA KAIKKOLAPPADAI
12.5; 552:Aiyaran Sendan @ Nikalanka Muvendavelalan : 12.5K; 555: Daanatonga THERINJA Kaikkolappadai Araiyan Geyavidangan:10; 556: Kattukkuri Yagna Kramavithan 12.5 (3rd yr 18.1.972:Uthama)
558:Thennavan Mirainattu Moovendavelalan of Devangudi in
Miraikkootram
on Tula, Sunday, Jyeshta (Ashtami thithi), corresponding to Oct
17th,
969 of Uthama Chola (period) -= 12.5 K; 559:Solaipiran Kramavithan:12.5K (Uthama 3rd yr)
574: Parantaga Therinja Kaikkolappadai (On behalf of) AAchan
Baladevar .. 12.5 K (It implies that AAchan Baladevar is
deceased!?)
581:Muttavaazhpetra Theirinjakaikkolar Araiyan Vengadavan:12.5k; 582: Muttan Kaman @ Narayana Vizhupparaiyan ::192 Sheep:2
Nondavilakku
584:Muttavazhpetra T.Kaikolar Devadi Vidhyaparan - 12.5 K; 586: Vanavan Kramavithan @ Thennavan Brahmamaharajan 12.5K; NORTH WALL:590: Singalantaka T.Kaikolar Baladevan Panaiyan ..
12.5K; 594:Koilpetral @ Gunavan Madeviyar D/o Bhupalasekhariyar : on
whose
behalf - the King (Devar) (Uthama) - gifted 42.5 K
(Thulaippon) :
inferred she is the concubine of (deceased)King (Aditha
Karikala!):Means she also died along with the King!!
607 : KODANDARAMACHERI Kaanjai Kramavithan:12.5K; On the Wall of the South Verandah in the Prakara, behind the
image
of
Gaja Lakshmi in the Same Anantheeswaraswami Temple of
Udayarkudi
(now known as Kattumannarkudi - near Veeranam @
Veeranarayanapuram -
where our PS Hero - Vandiyathevan commences this epic), as per
Record
AR 609 of 1920, SII Vol XIX : PAZHUVETTARAYAN KODHANDAN
TAPPILDARMAN
also donated 12.5 Kazhanju......

THEN COMES THE LIST OF RAJAKESARI'S (RAJARAJA's) 3RD YEAR
OFFERINGS:
Example :
(Gangan Ambalavana Kandradithan @ Mummudichola Vizhupparayan
(PERUNDANAM OR HIGHLY PLACED OFFICIAL) : 614 of 1920 :: who
acquired
about 12 veli of lands from Sarvisara Trinetra Dasapuriya
Bhattan,
who himself acquired from Uthama)

THE ABOVE INSCRIPTIONS CLEARLY INDICATE THAT :

1. After Aditha Karikalan's death ( Oct 969!), those from
various
Theinja Kaikkolapadai made offerings - recorded in the 2nd
regenal
yr
of Uthama Chola (971 - but Sundarachola was not there, since the
year
is denoted as the ascendency of Uthamaa's year); 2. Atleast some of them committed suicide (on their behalf
offerings
were made!)
2. VIPs included : Sembian Madevi, Periya Pazhuvettarayar and a
- Madeviyar of AK; 3. Again, in the 3rd Regenal yr of Rajaraja - offerings were
made; 4. The famous "KAANDALUR CHALAI Kalamarutharuli" event took

place after this year 988 only.

NOTE: Famous Udyarkudi "Chakravbathy Sreemugam" inscription is
in
the
2nd year of Rajakesari - Rajaraja - who - even though shared the
throne in 987 along with Uthama - has proclaimed himself
CHAKRAVATHY
and called the Assasins of Aditha karikala as TRAITORS (Not
Enemies)-
which list included Irumudichola Brahmarayan Ravidasan (who was
a
Minister in Uthama period) also - and all his relatives lands
confiscated and donated to this temple. We now know that
Mummudichola Vizhupparayar - Obviously the Commander-in-chief of
the
Entire Chola Kingdom has confiscated this - which means an Army
action - further means that the confiscation necessiated the
intervention of an Army Commander in Chief.

Fondly, sps
