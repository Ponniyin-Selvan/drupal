CERTAIN POINTS NEED TO BE ASCERTAINED.

1. Isana Siva Pandithar was NOT KARUVURAR. Both are different
entities.

2. Isanasivapandithar did not give swarna kalasam. It was by the
RRC himself. Isana's IDOL was kept in worship during Rajendra's
period.

3. When the subject Laguleesam is discussed, the first - obvious
reference will be to one CHATURANANA PANDHITHAR - Thiruvotriyur.
Doubt whether he was called Siacharya. They were called Mahavradhi
(n)s. This was Prelude our Veerasaivam - now so much predominent
in
Karanataka.

elsewhere in the chola land."
