We should take into account the important fact that character &
behaviour of every women in PS is greatly influenced by the way they

were brought up/comforts/family background/exposture.Family plays a
very important role as females are more emotionally attached to
everything.No women in Ps is potrayed as blunt rude or villi.

Accept the fact or not male races are greatly influenced by women
in general knowingly or unknowingly.

Kundavai- She had maids to attend her personal chores, all the
comforts and exposture and the freedom of voice and offcource her

drive to learn new things. She involved herself too much in 'porantha
veedu' (like most of the females do after they get married) the only
difference is she was ahead that she wanted to marry a person who
gives her a hand in doing that(mind u she doesn't choose a dumb
person like kandamaran/parthiban who would be even more obliging than
vandiyatevan) . she believed in Horoscope of her brother 100% that he
will go places. Take into fact that chola desam at the stage was
badly in need of a shape and good team co-ordination she goes one
step ahead leading the good set of males she knew
(vandiyatevan/rajaraja and in future rajendra). She doesn't influence
her brother since she although dislikes sendan becoming a king
accepts her brothers decision. Priority for her here is chola desam.

The rest of the females I think have a bit of sitha bramhamai as
kalki says which again influenced by various other factors.

Vanathi- She grew up in diffenent places. She has seen death of her
father. She takes kundavai as her true friend. We can say she might

have manipulated to some extent but her actions was rather ameture
taken into fact that kundavai is very smart lady.

Nandini- She is totally confusing. She can be said to enjoy the
fact that she could make all the males a puppet.Her way of punishing

males who were either arogant or proud. Other than that she doesn't
benefit from her beauty nor manipulation. She has to either marry a
oldie or slog in forest living in disguise .

Poongulali- again she is slightly off..taken into fact that she has
to live all by herself protecting & defending...She is more practical

in understanding that she cannot live in luxury ( a koondu
killi)..she is more a nature lover than any one else..She could have
influenced rajaraja but think she changes her mind after being deeply
hurted by people who claims she trys to captivate a prince..

Regards,
Meena.
