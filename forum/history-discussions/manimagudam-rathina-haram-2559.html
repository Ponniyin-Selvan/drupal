manimagudam & rathina haram

vanakkam,

Rajasimma Pandiyan the second lost miserably to Paranthaka Chozhan.
He took the traditional manimudi of the pandiyas and the raththina
haram, which is believed to have been given by Lord Indiran to the
pandiyas, and left them in Sri Lanka in the custody of the Lankan
Emperor(Mahinda III?).

Chozhas waged war after war with the Lankans, to bring this back
into the Tamil land. They finally succeeded in Rajendra's period.
This is what has been described in the lines you have quoted.

swetha
