"gandhiram"

yesterday sri guruji ravishankar visited chidambaram and gave a
sathsangam. he donated few lack worth of articles to the temple. his tamil
language fluency was very good and his approach to human problemswere
straight forward. he did not look like the usual godmen visiting these
places.
the whole event looked to me like the visit of thirugnanasambandar 1400
years ago. the days have hardly changed. the same music and chantings. only
here instead of neelakanda yazhpanar a small group of devotees accompanying
the guruji did the musical recitatations. guruji looked like an another
sambandar or he is the sambandar of our times!

people behaved exactly how they would have behaved 1400 years ago and so
did the dheekshidhars,police and the public. in chidambaram life is frozen
in time. it is a zealosly guarded secret of hindu heritage.

time will not move here and the clocks will stand still for many
centuries to come!!

gandhi
