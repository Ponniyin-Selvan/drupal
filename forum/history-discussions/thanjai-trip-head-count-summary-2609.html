Thanks Satish, Swetha & Kamal - for the planning sheets.

Summery as stated below:

participants through Chennai :: 19
paritipants Direct Bangalore :: 5
Kudanthai / Erode & other places :: 6

Accommodation :: Taken care for every one who needed it.

Travel arrangement :: Completed. 20 Seater Mini bus.

Satish has agreed to inform Travel Agent to provide Tarpaulin etc for
putting luggages in the top and protect from rains.

Some snacks during travel is arranged.

Socks (to protect from hot sun) / Fruits / biscuits / water bottle /
hot bewerages :: Participants may plan individuallly.

Other things :: Torch / laser / tripod / mike / voicew recorder /
binacular / magnifier etc. for common use. shall plan a wire brush
also for removing dust from inscriptions.

Not prepared any Banner.

Kamal is kind enough to organised to invite Dr. Kalaikkovan & Dr.
Nalini also. That is a real feast. We should hear more from them
on Tiruvalanchuzhi. With Kudavoil Dr. Balu also to share some
thoughts on Thiruvarur / Thanjai etc., this will be a real treat for
History lovers like us.

WE WILL ORGANISE A SUMMING UP SESSION WITH THEM FIRST. INDIVIDUAL
QUESTIONS MAY FOLLOW. SIMILAR TO VIKRAMAN MEET.

questionaires / points for clarification / discussion etc. needed.

Beginning to realise THREE things :

1. The time frame is not sufficient to intereact with such great
scholars

2. Departure from Chennai - TTD boarding - other Boardings - should
be ON THE DOT 6 AM and NOT A MINUTE LATER.

3. Sunday morning:: Departure from Thanjai to Oullamangai -
Tvlnchiuzhi - Darasuram - LATEST BY 7 am. Breakfast on the way.
(Means Saturday night, we are NOT stretching beyong 2130 hrs).

Hope we are all in the same wave-length.

Looking forward to see everyone soon.

Anbudan / sps
