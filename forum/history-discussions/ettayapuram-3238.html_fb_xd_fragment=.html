house the present residant is the sister of popular
writers..bhaskara thondaiman and pulavar
ragunathan.present ettayapuram is dry place but hiding
glorious memories of great people

That is interesting thread, dear Venkat.

For those in Chennai - erstwhile Madras - Music - Dance - Drama
festivals are NOT New and most of them think as part of one's
social
well-being. But for those living in Rural areas - particularly
like
in Pudukottai - Sivagangai - Devakottai - Ramanathapuram etc.,
which are cultural Centres - but lacking the prominence of State
Capital - a FESTIVE SEASON is ear-marked and all the Popular
Artists
from Chennai make bee-line to such Festivals.

This is clearly brought out in Thillana Mohanmbal movie - where
the
Drama / Dance / Music Events take place in Thiruvarur -
Azhagarkoil -
Nagapattinam - but an INVITATION from Madras and a PERFORMANCE in
a
prominant Sabha is what they were aiming at. Once that
recognition
attained, they visit their native places and take other artists
also
to pay respect to their Nativity!

Karaikkudi Kamban Vizha is also one such Festival! Area people
celebrated these as their family functions ! True harmoney could
be
relished in such festivals.

anbudan / sps
