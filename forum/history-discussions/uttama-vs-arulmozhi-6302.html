VIjay,

I dont understand your confusion. A man is termed periyappa or chittappa, not by the age in comparision with the person to whom the relationship is said.

Simply put, no matter if that person is elder or younger than me. If he is younger than my dad, he is my chittappa. Period. No obviously, Periyappa has to be only elder than me as he is elder even to my father. 

So, taking the above, Utthama is much younger to Sundara Chozha who is the Father of AMV. So he has to, necessarily, be Chittappa and not Periyappa at all. How does it matter if Uttama was elder to AMV? Heis not elder than Sundara Choza, who is his cousin brother, right? So I still dont understand why you are confused.
