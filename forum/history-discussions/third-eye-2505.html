Since the word "The Third Eye" used in the mail, I remember another
interesting fact to share with the group. One of my doctor friend
was mentioning this to me,referring his First year syllabus long
time ago:

"In a developing fetus(child at embroyo), the master
gland "pituitary gland" will be in the Shape of another Eye. This
gland at such early stage of the fetu, that is vital for brain
formation, will look like a vertical eye. The western scientist, who
have stuided about this glands development has quoted "I was
surprised to observe this gland looking like a third eye, if
prominent, even during later developments of the child. And I
attended a seminar by Dr. Radhakrishnan on "The Hindu Philosopy" and
wondered one of god who is in Deep medidation(control of all
our "pulangazhal") have a outknown Third Eye. And this Pituitary
gland, the master of our endocrine system, predominant for our self-
control,will take up a third eye shape if One can control One-self".

What I understand is this Pituitary gland has to function if an
individual wants to control his vital senses..and if this gland
becomes prominent, it takes a shape of an EYE.

What a surprising fact.. Long ago, our GOD does have a third
eye.. Any Doctors view?

"Thennadudaya SIVANAE Pottri..Pottrii.."

With regards,
Raj.
