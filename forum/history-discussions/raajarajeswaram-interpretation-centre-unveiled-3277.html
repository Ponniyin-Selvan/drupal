Dear Gokul,
Visited this last Saturday.

The place is identified behind VARAAHI Amman, southern enclosue,
more towards the west.

A compact display of :

1. Brief History on Big temple; 2. RRC Paintings - photographed and displayed about 8' x 6' size.
3. Sarafoji paintings -do- (lesser size)
4. Important Karanas in POP.
5. Big temple photographs - as it looked since 1950.
6. Photos of KN Sastry, Sorry I forgot the name of the gentleman
who
conceived the " evolution of Chozha " paintings .. and other
Archlgists who worked on Bigtemple in early days. None
contemporary.
7. Two boys present to explain.

Basically this arrangement is very good to explain things to
foreigners and other visitors. No entrance fee. With lighting
and
other maintenance cost - wish ASI is able to continue this in
future also.

Very good attempt.. to begin with.

Please whether we can do something through www.thebigtemple.com
also. Like visit this site for more details etc.

And add the Interpretation Centre details in our site.

Visibility of this place is yet to be improved. We will discuss
this with ASI when we visit them next.
