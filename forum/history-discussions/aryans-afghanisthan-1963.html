Adding to Sampath's comments, I like to share one interesting thing.

For millinieums our vedas were preserved only through hearsay. Vedas
should not be written and only should be learnt and practiced orally
(veda adyayanam). Once the westerners started writing down,breaking
the tradition, we started loosing vedas. Those who know vedas are
very less in number today and pariyava says, its becuase of the
lethargy that we have it written and need not memorise it.
