mahendravarma torturing appar is found only in shavite puranas
and
not an ounce of evidence for this is found in any kalvettus or
records.If such an incident happened why dint a single kalvettu
record this?

sampath we have had heated discussions on the aspect whether
kalvettus are the pillar holding up tamil history.

giving so much importance to them without tracing other sources are
a
great mistake we will do as serious historians.

first of all almost all your kalvettus are in temples. the priority
of these kalvettus was to establish grants for them.

for example if you read karuvurars thiruvisaipa he describes
tanjore
town on its hey day( possibly on the kumbabishekam day of periya
koil) and there are people who say he did not even live in that
period because there is no kalvettu)

kalvettus in the big temple are mainly to those who gave to the
temple
( as grants) and those who received from the temple(as salaries)
karuvur siththar was in no position for both.
the thirumurais are perhaps the most popular written
documents
we have from the period we are studying( 6- 10th century anno
domini)
they may not be your history books but they just cannot be ignored.
even periya puranam describing the saints was written in the later
chola period.

two of the greatest kings mahendran and nedumaran of the period
(the
pallavas and pandyas) were converted to hindus during the period of
sambanthar and apar. i for one beleive nothing shot of a miracle
could have persuaded these two bigots to change their spots.

if I have to beleive somebody, I d rather beleive a saint than a
stenographer.
regards
venketesh
