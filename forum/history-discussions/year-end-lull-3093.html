Dear Friends,

Member Chandra was kind enough to invite me to this wonderful Centre -
which is a School - cum - Samadhi - cum Research Centre, in
Chozhapuram, Sivagangai District.

The place Chozhapuram attained prominence for wrong reasons. A
Textile Mill in this place figured in the Parliament debate recently.

The temple was looted recently. We have brought this out in earlier
messages as well.

The Centre - Sunddhanadha Swamiji's abode - is very much serene.
Whenever our Group member passes through, they can visit this place.
We will try to cover this place in our forth-coming Pandianattu ula -
pls ref. member Kasirajan's earlier mails on this subject.

I assume there is less activity because of personal pre-occupation of
majority of members and recent natural calamities in Maharastra,
Karnataka and Tamilnadu. One more in the offin in Tamilnadu.

There was an observation from the High Places that it would heavily
rain in the Tamil month "Parthiba". As it appears the spell may
extend till next Tamil New Year.

Gokul cautioned me to set in motion the commence preparatory
activities to Celebrate Next Sadhayam - 2006 Oct - with an
International Seminar in Thanjavur.

We will start working on this from mid January 2006.
Far East / Lankan visits are also on the Cards..

Let us notify the agenda and start working soon..

anbudan / sps
