Hi All,

A List of Vandiyathevan Encounters from Book 1 to 5(As far as I
could remember).

VD - Vandiyathevan.
AM - Arul Mozhi.
CP - Chinna Pazhuvetaiyar.
PP - Periya Pazhuvetaiyar.

Book 1:

1. VD comes to Veeranapuram Eeri.
2. Meets Azlwarkadiyan with his famous dialogue(hariyum sivanum
onnu, ariyathavay vayil mannu).
3. Goes to Kadambur.
4. Joined by Azlwarkadiyan on the way and comes to know about
Nandini.
5. Enters Kadambur through his quick thinking, meets big heads of
the kingdom, comes to know about conspiracy for the crown againgt
Karikalan. Also sees Manimekalai.
6. Leaves Kadambur to Tanjavur, meets Nandini for the first time
and trick her in to giving the Pazhvur Mudhirai(Actually who tricks
who here).
7. Enters thanjavur using the Mudhirai, meets Sundhara gives the
mail addressed to him by tricking CP that he was asked by PP to give
the mail to Sundara.
8. Uses his quick wit to escape the CP's while giving a message to
Sundara(Abayam Abayam).
9. Realizes that he has lost the mail to Kunadai to CP.
10. Uses his briallance again to get the mail back.
11. Escapes from thanjavur to pazhaiyarai after the encounters with
Nandini, PP and Kandhamaran.
12. Meets Kundavai at Kundanthai, encounters the prank she plays on
Vanathi.
13. Enters Pazhaiyarai disguised as Kamsan.
14. Meets Kundavai in the most romantic encounters. Describes his
adventures.
15. Kundavai gives the task of getting AM to india from Illangai.

Book 2:

1. Meets Poongozhli at Kodikari.
2. Escapes the PP's soldiers and with the help of Poongozhli
escapse's to Lanka.
3. Meets Periya Vellar's army and gets jailed for showing the
Pazhvur Mudhirai.
4. Rescued by Azlwarkadiyan and traverses srilanka to meet AM.
5. Meets AM and gives the mail given to him by Kundavai.
6. VD, AM and Azlwarkadiyan all gets rescued by Mandhakini from the
Pandya conspirators.
7. Witnesses AM rejecting the Lanka kingdom given to him.
8. While travelling back has the best fight in the book with AM.
9. AM decides to go to Thanjavur instead to Kundavai and to
Kraikalan.
10. VD get caught in the Arab traders.
11. VD escapes the ship, but the ship catches fire. AM and VD
escapes the ship and rescued by Poongozhli.
12. AM get Chikengunya?????

Book 3:

1. VD, AM and Poongozhli comes to kodikarai.
2. VD uses his brillance to make AM, Poongozhli and Aumdan escape
Ravidasan.
3. Travels to pazhaiyarai, vows not to lie here after.
4. Meets Nandini again at the forest with the small kid, with the
Pandya conspirators.
5. Enters pazhaiyarai tricking Bhujangan that he is fortune teller.
6. Gets jailed for fighting with Vaithiyar magan, as he accueses VD
of Nandini's spy.
7. Meets Kundavai at the pathala sirai(one of my most favorite
scenes), where she tells him her love.
8. Sent for the task of trying to stop Karikalan from going to
Kadambur.

Book 4:

1. Meets Karikalan where Kadamaran accueses him of attacking from
the back.
2. VD gets an appaluse from Azlwarkadiyan for not telling the truth
and also a lie to Karikalan to escape the Kandamaran's accusation.
3. Along with Azlwarkadiyan unearths the plot to kill Karikalan at
Kadambur.
4. Uses Manimekalai to shadow Karaikalan whereever he goes.
5. Have a nice long conversation with Karikalan about his love for
Nandini, how he feels about VD, Kandamaran and others around him, the
feeling that he his going to die.

Book 5:

1. Get accused for killing Karikalan.
2. Jailed for the same.
3. Escapes from the jail with Karuthiruman.
4. Kundavai get the last mail Karikalan wrote to her before his
death in which he praises.
5. Gets injured by taking the spear meant for Amudan.
6. Saved by Azlwarkadiyan, Poongozhli and Amudan from the injury.
7. Rescues Manimegalai from the River and gets caught again.
8. PP takes the blame for killing Karikalan to save VD.
9. PP praises VD to Azlwarkadiyan and tells him that he did not
kill Karikalan.
10. AM with the help of VD crowns Aumdan.
11. Meets Kundavai to inform of CP fate.
12. Manimekalai dies on seeing VD.
