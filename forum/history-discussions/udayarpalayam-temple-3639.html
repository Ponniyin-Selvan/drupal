That place is not a temple at all. Couple of months before

were

lingam
might have come there because of flood.

========================
Kudanthai N. Sethuraman (of Raman & Raman Transport) wrote on this
place in his book about a 20 yrs back.

There was a press news by some local person 5 - 6 yrs back.

========================

Some historians (they didnt tell the name) visited that place
before
us told the land owner that govt will acquire his land for
building

us inside his field. Nearly 1 hr of explanation that we are not
from

convinced him that there is no evidence to prove it as a temple
and
