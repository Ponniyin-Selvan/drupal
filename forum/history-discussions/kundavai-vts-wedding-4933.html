Amarar Kalki has taken the period as 969 AD and unfolded a Story
line related to the Chozha History containing some of the following
incidents ::

1. Sundara was in power. His Yuva Raja was Aditha Karikalan.
Kandradithan's son Madurantakan @ Uthama was not Yuva Raja. Anirudh
Brahmaraya was his Minister. Periya Pazhuvettarayar Commander.

THIS TALLIES WITH KNOWN HISTORICAL DATA.

2. RRC was not married. Vaanathi was his Love. Rajendra was to
have been born to Vanathi when they get married.

AS ALREADY DISCUSSED, there is a possiblity that RRC would have
married Vanathi by this time. However there is no major departure
from known data which will affect History otherwise.

3. Kundavai - Vandhiyathevan love - not marrying till 969 AD ::

NO DOUBT THEY ARE MARRIED - but do not know when.. prior to 969 AD
or after.

But after the sons of Rajendra Chozha, there was no male legal heir
to Chozha throne (sometime around 1070 AD or so - about 100 yrs
after our story line) and the KULOTHUNGA who came to Power was from
Kundavai - Vandhiyathevan lineage !

Probably keeping this in mind, Kalki would have chosen to maintain
Kundavi - Vandhiyathevan love story on line ..

And RRC being more a warrior & rugged, Kalki would have portraited
him less romantic .. and thus needed Kundavai - Vandvn love story
online!
