27-4-2004

Anbulla Gokul avarkalukku vanakkam,

I must express a special word of thanks to you for introducing me to
the really interested in the study of Chola History.

I will definitely take heed to the advice of the Moderator Thiru
Sridar Rathinam, and try to limit my articles to few per week, as I
too realize as my articles are very long, it will take quite some
time for members to read through and then discuss on same.
I suggest the interested members to take a printout of the long
articles, and read them at leisure

I will return back towards end May, with my article titled,

THE MEDIEVAL CHOLA EMPIRE AND THEIR RELATIONS WITH SOUTH-EAST ASIA
AND CHINA (A.D.985-1120)

In the meantime I am looking forward to Thiru SB's informations on
the Bronzes of Rajaraja & Ulakamadevi at Sarabai Museum, Alahabad.

Miha anbudan
