1. We have expertise in having conducted Vandhiyathevan Vzhiyil.

2. R. Venkat specified that some one for Ph D has traced Kannagi

Vazhi (Poompuhar to Madurai and beyond).

Those in North, are normally familiar with Chidambaram - Srirangam -

Rameswaram - Madurai.

As you are aware, between Chennai and Chidambaram, we have the

recently found Muruga Temple. Saluvankuppaaam / Tiger Cave. Mamallai

is to be explored more.

Beyond Chidambarm, we have Seerkazhi (which promptly figures in Star

TV Yaatra progm)- Thiruvengadu (3 Moorthy - 3 Amman - 3 Theertham -

Ancient Brahma -sthan)- Thirukadayur - (Markandeyar) - Thirunallar

(Saturn) - Thiruvarur (Once had Golden Roof like Tirupathy !) -

Thilatharpanapurai (where Rama performed last rites for Jadayu)-

Thiruchengottangudi (Siruthondar - his son Seeralar - Pillaikkari) -

Thirumarugal ( Chettipenn getting back life) - several

Veerattanams - apart from Sikkal /Ettukkudi / Ennkann of Muruga Tio -

around Thiruvarur alone!

Like wise ample potency is there for Tourism..

We have been thinking of guide training etc.

If Delhi / Bombay Tamil Sangams show interest, an Organiser can be

nominated.

Last time when Sri visited Chennai, we had discussion on opening

bank accounts etc.

If there are issues, they can be handled outside the Group .. and

the interested ones can work on detailed progm.

Dear Vaidhyanathan will be an asset in more areas.

Pls explore.

best wishes and best regards / sps & friends

Respected Mr.Balasubramani,

meet at Delhi. My mobile No.9811202630

A.Vaidyanathan
