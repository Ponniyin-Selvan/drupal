Dear Friends,

Atlast MAGUDAGAMAM is in hand - 200 pages book - printed mostly in
Grantha lipis - copy right: Swaminathan Gurukkal, Secretary, South
Indian Archakars Assn, 11, Thambu chetty St., Ch 1, published in 1977.
Also avl at : NT Dakshinamurthy Gurukkal 28, Duragalaya Road,
Thiruvarur; D.Annasamy Gurukkal, 6, Kandasamy Koil st., Pollachi.
I think any Senior Gurukkal - say Kaligambal temple, Chennai, and
Thiruvarur should be able to help us in getting a copy.

Magudagamam is the 17th among the 28th Agamas, originally consisted of
about one lakh slokahs - Ruthirar received from Paramasivan, comprised
of two sub-divisions MAGUDAM and MAGUDOTHTHARAM in Guru - Sishya
bhawam.. Agamas consist of 4 parts : GNANA - YOGA - KRIYA - SARIYA.

Thiruvavaduthurai Adheenam Sivachariar K.Swaminathan has located some
of thE KRIYA PAADA from palm leaves and translated 12 Sargas -
Thanthravathara padalam to Acharya Lakshna vidhi.

1. Upothkadham.. Sivagnanam .. MAGUDAM .,. to prathyadhikari
2. Pooja palan to Kshetrabalarsanam (this deals with various
Lakshanas - definitions)
3. Directions .. to Acharyapooja etc
4. Angurarpanam .. to pakthotsavam etc.
5. Prayachithapathartham to pasmasodhanam etc.
6. Thurasika namaskaram to sivakshetra visesham; 7. Devadalaya deepa danam to pradimabishekam; 8. Snanabishekamkaalam to snanabisheka palan; 9. Brahmanathithaalam to nishkalathinyasam; 10. Pavithroprayojanam .. to Mahapavithradaranam; 11.Yamadhi ashtangam .. to Hamsadevanirupanam; 12. Sadhacharya lakshnama to Varjyasalalakshnam.

(activities) to be performed related to Establishing deiteis and
poojas etc.
