Thank you sir, very kind of you.

But I feel Irandam yathirai is not the apt word, because there were so
many yathirais, big and small in the past years. I think the first
group who made mudhal yathirai (atleast a part of them) made further
trips before consolidating into the current varalaaru team. And later
there was one big trip to Tanjai and surrounding places and lots of
small trips by few members now and then.

So better we call it PS yathira, without number and avoid confusions.

Regards,
Satish
