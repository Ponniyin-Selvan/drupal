Dear Sri,

Nice to see your mail.

Orumudi :: Original Chozha nadu

Irumudi :: Extended within India;
Mummudi :: Extended overseas

This explanation was given by Dr.Rajavelu ASI when
we both, with a couple
of friends, read the Udayarkudi inscriptions about 6
yrs back.

Kanthalur Chaalai is again an inspiring matter to
write about. It figures
from RR's 3rd Regnl Yr whereas Udayarkudi
inscriptions were of 2nd Regnl
Yr.

In the First Rgnl Yr of any King, inscriptions were
never recorded.

Obviously after Udyarkudi, Kandalur chalai was the
priority for RR! As
earlier mentioned in the Group, Rajendra Chozha
Devar was the Maadanda
nayakan of this Great War !

Probably Ealam was already under Chozha's rule.
Hence RR called himself
Mummudi Chozhan.

Looking from another plane : An obscured Arumozhi
till 984 - shares the
Chozha throne in 985 with Uthama, declares himself
CHAKRAVARTHY and Sri
Rajaraja Devar in 987 after punishing the culprits
responsible for AK's
murder - obviously after eliminating Uthama, defeats
Chera & Pandya -
calling himself MUMMUDI CHOZHA !

This event in particular and the general definition
of Mummudi Chozha
mandalam (Zones - created for Administrative
reasons) may have looked at
different perspectives, I personally think.

I also would like to speculate that after expulsion
from Veeranarayana
Chaturvedi mangalam, Ravidasan with all his
relatives, might have been
offered asylum in Kanthalur chaalai by the Cheras.
There might be a
possibility that they were KC - trained (like 36
Shaolin chambers!) prior
to AK's murder! When they were called Durohins, it
is clear that they
deceived the popular perspection.. During the
course of trials, RR might
not have wanted to award them capital punishment for
several reasons ... He
might have hunted them down in Kandalur chaalai and
had the satisfaction of
eliminating them enmass in War with fair opportunity
to show their strength!

There must be very strong reasons for Arumozhi for
calling himself -
RAJARAJAN - CHAKRAVARTHY & MUMMUDI CHOZHAN within
just 3 yrs of ascendancy
to the Chozha Throne! Even he could not have dreamt
that he was heading to
make the Best innings in next 27 years - which the
World talking about for
next thousand years or more !

Re pazhuvettarayars of Chera origin - which clan
was so powerful 150 yrs
prior to RR, lost all its power and ceased to be
mentioned - save the 7th
Regnl yr inscription of Rajendra - means next 37
years! That too was
pertaining to a mention of a servant maid!

fondly, sps
