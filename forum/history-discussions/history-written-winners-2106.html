Dear friends

I am Jayaprakash Narayanan and this is my first post in this group.
The recent discussion about the greatness of cholas and pandyas has
made me to send my views also to the group.
Through out the history, we can find that the real truth is
suppressed continuously by the winners. Take Dravidian
civilization"DC"(harrapa-mohenjadaro) which existed before the aryan
invasion. The vast amount of literature works explain about the aryan
culture but none has been available about "DC" as the same would have
been destroyed by Aryans. If some later King with a power greater
than Rajaraja has conquered cholas, destroyed the sculptures and said
Rajaraja is Demon we would have believed. Still only the great
architectures & sculptures of Cholas & Pallavas are standing as a
great proof of those flourished Kingdoms.
In my view we haven't got the right history yet, but through the
tamil literature(it is pity that most of the young generation ignore
this) we can imagine that our part of the country was of great wealth
and culture in the past 3000 years.(Thirukural was said to be written
in 2nd A.D) We can find inspiration from the past to make the present
more memorable in the future.

Vazhka Vazhamudan

Jayaprakash narayanan
