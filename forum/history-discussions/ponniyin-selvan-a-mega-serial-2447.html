Just read in the Indian express supplement that 'Ponniyin selvan' is
being made into a TV mega serial with a huge budget. They are
expecting that this will take the television world by storm. Some of
the casting includes Cine-actress Mohini (do you remember her?) as
kundavai, Jayasri(who is this, just given as a VJ in vijay TV) as
Nanthini and Senthil as Alwarkkadiyan. There was a photo also of
someone looking like Vanthiyathevan talking to Senthil's
Alwarkkadiyan. Do any of you have any information on this?. When this
travesty is to take place in our homes and which channel is going for
it etc?. I read it in the city express supplement of the Chennai
edition and couldn't locate it in the web.
