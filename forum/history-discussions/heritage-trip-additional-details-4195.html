YOURS ) AND FILE A TRAVELLOGUE, WHICH MAY BE USEFUL FOR MEMBERS:

1)Nilatharpana puri(Adhi vinayagar temple--Vinayagar has a human
face here).

----------------------------------------------
THILA (JINGILEE) THARPANA PURI is where Lord Rama is supposed to
have performed Tharpanam for Jadayu. There are interesting
Ramayanam connected temples in south - Thanjai - Rameswaram belt.
Darpasayanam - Pullanai - Dasarathar got paayasam which he gave to 3
wives .. birth of Rm - Lx - Brth - Sgn to Nikumbalai destruction ner
Kumbakonam Ayyavadi). NEARBY IS THIRUVEEZHI MIZHALAI -
Kalyanasundareswarar - where RRC II golden roofed the temple.
(inscriptions)

2)Koothanur(Maha Saraswathi Amman) :: SARASWATHI appeared in front
of OTTAKOOTHAR (of Kulothunga period. Students throng before exams.
3)Thirumayichur(Lalithambigai--Here you can see a gold anklet
around ambals legs, just like any human being(goind around the leg

and not just placed on the surface). Meganathaswami. Ialankoil -
Twin Temples - temple within temple (both are hymned). Ideal for
Rathasapthami. Southern Wall Siva - Parvathy must see. RRC
inscriptions).

4)Thirunallar(Saniswaran temple--presiding deity-
dharbaranyeswarar) ONE AMONG THE SAPTHA VIDANGA STHALAMS - Major SP

Sthalam - Thiruvarur- having wings - since Indira was deceiving
Musukunda - Chozha King - who helped Indira and Siva flew to Trvr)

5)Thirupugalur(here thirunavukarasar got mukthi)- VAASTU STHALAM.
PEOPLE KEEP BRICKS HERE PERFORM PUJA BEFORE COMMENCING HOUSE

CONSTRUCTION. There is a nice Aghazhi around this temple..
Interestingly Panchavanmadevi's Servant maid gave 9 Golden Flowers
to Appar Bronze in this temple.

6)Thirukannapuram(souriraja perumal)- of the Trio -
Thirukkannamangai - Thrigukkannanur. CAUVERI is idolised and

worshiped here in the Sanctum!

7)Thiruchengattangudi(Siruthondar @ paranjothiyar's birth
place..temple is in ruins now..was really pained to see that..ther

(temple car) is in shambles.presiding deity is uthrapatheeswarar).
Archieve Search will provide interesting details. VAATHAPI
GANAPTHI (Recall SS) is installed here by Paranjothi!

8)mela poodhanur(My father's birth place)
9)Enngann(murugan temple) - Ettukkudi - Sikkal are TRIO. The Diety
is made by the Silpi not having eyes or hands ! Enname Kannaga

irundhu seytha murugan Ennkann murugan ! Nice temple.

10) Needamangalam santhana ramasamy temple ::Recommended For the
Childless.
11)Alangudi(dhaksinamurthy(guru) temple-abathsagayeswarar)- During
Aditha Karikala period, there was a " Ponmari " - says inscription!
12)thiruvidaimaruthur(mahalingasamy temple-here one can get rid of
brahmahatti dhosham-this temple is so beautiful and huge that i was

wonderstruck by it..one shd never miss it)! of the Tio Srisailam -
Thiirupudaimaruthur ! MAHALINGAM surrounded by 9 Parivara temples
for which Vinayaga is Thiruvalanchuzhi - Muruga Swamimalai and
Durgai - Patteswaram ! Uthama - SM Devi - Aditha Kkln -
Panchavanmadevi - inscriptions are interesting ! Very B I G Temple.
Under Thiruvavaduthurai Adheenam - recently consecrated. They are
planning 27 Star Flower Garden !

13)suriyanar koil - Kulothunga period !
14)darasuram(airavatheeswarar temple..excavations r still going
on..beautiful carvings..went at 1:30PM, so nadai was closed..cudnt

see the lord,but had to be contended with the outer prakarams and
carvings..it has been declared a heritage site by UNESCO..one shd
never miss it..resplendent beauty.

15)Patteeswaram(famous for durgai..dhenupureeswarar-saw and
confirmed frm people and inscriptions tat this is the thirumazhapadi

in "ponnar meniyane..mazhapaadiyul manikkame"..also this is
pazhayaarai(if im correct)).. MUTHUPANDAL was presented by Siva to
Thirugnana Sambandar ! Durgai - Bhairavar - Mahaganapthy - Muruga
MUST SEE.

16)Thanjavur big temple(i visited this at around 6:45PM..so cudnt
capture this temple under sunlight..so am really sad..neways it was

awesome as usual..was crowded as i visited it on the thiruvadhirai
day)

17)mariamman temple, thanjavur :: Recently consecreated at Rs. 2
Crores budget ! Thanga Paavadi is now famous.
18) how can i miss the great periya koil of thiruvarur..saw the
sayarathchai of lord thyagaraja and cudnt come out of that

beauty..he is indeed the raja of the lords..kamalalayam is beautiful
as usual.

i cudnt visit GKC..can someone tell where , this is, so tat i can
visit the next time i visit thiruvarur
also wanted to visit thirupurambiyam,thirunaraiyur and
sreemushnam..but cudnt..god had willed that way i think..but i wont

miss the next time.
============================

Really it was a very good trip, I could see. regards/ sps
