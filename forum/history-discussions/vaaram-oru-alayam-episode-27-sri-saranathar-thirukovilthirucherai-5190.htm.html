This week's Podcast is about
"Saranathar ThiruKovil,Thirucherai".
This divya Kshetram
is 15
kms South of kumbakonam, and 4 km north of kodavasal.

The
Moolavar is Saranathan in a standing posture and the thayar is known as
saranayaki.As per Legend it is believed that Markandeya and Kaveri Amman
meditated upon Lord Vishnu.It is also believed that brahma made a clay plotto
rescue and preserve the Vedas during pralaya,but none of the pots made by
Brahma would last.There upon he was directed by Lord Vishnu to proceed to
Thirucherai (Saara Kshetram) and make a pot out of the clay on the banks of
kaveri, and thus the Vedas were protected in a pot made out of tough mud at
this place.

Warden mocking when he wanted water and the King leaving his life in

This happened in KUDAVOIL KOTTAM. That is Kudavasal. History.

(Nector) too, prior to Mahapralayam (Great Tsunami) and boated safely
by THONIAPPAR (Seerkazhi Siva), the Kumbam reached Kumbakonam and was
broken. The mouth of Kumbam fell at Kudavasal. Puranic.
