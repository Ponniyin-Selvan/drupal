Thanks for your concern. However for clarification:

Above the Peruvudayar at Big temple, the inside Vimanam lokks like a hollow
COME. The Vimanam is made of a larger OCTAGONAL piece in the bottom, say
with about 1 foot thick stone-ring and above that another piece slightly of
smaller size, with inside dimension reduced and tilted minutely by about
1.5 degree angle.

Since the entire Vimanam, above the Sanctum Walls - is of about 216 feet,
and when we take about 1 foot thick stone, the Vimanam is likely to be made
of about 216 x 1 foot - Stone octagonal rings of reducing diamension
towards the peak! To make a circular cone effect, each octagonal ring,
when placed above each other, is rotated by about 360 / 216 degree (very
subtly) which is why I mentioned about 1.5 degree - interlocked.

Means - from the Peruvudayar Lingam top till the bottom most Tip of Vimanam
Sthoopi - a space of 216 feet PLUS - was Clear :: Which we mentioned as
AKASA LINGAM in some of the earlier Mails - about a year back.

During Nayak's period a Wooden lid was put. which is recently cemented
also, interfering with the clear space, but one can stand on this centre
and look at the Vimanam - Sthoopi bottom to find out the method of
construction.

Apart from the Main outer wall of the Vimanam (inside of is approacheable
and can be seen) Rajaraja has created another Inner Wall and created a six
feet passage between both these STONE WALLS of about 15 feet High.

ON these Walls (Inside wall of Vimanam and Out side surface of Inner Wall -
opposite to each other - Rajraja has painted SEVERAL paintings - using
Natural / herbal colour base with mix of Gold. (Similar to Sithannavasal /
Ajantha / Ellora etc.,). For instance, the tiger skin of Nataraja is
covered with REAL GOLDEN DOTS - looks so pretty even in photographic
version!

During Nayak's period (about 300 yrs back), they painted certain pictures
(like Tanjore Paintings) over the Rajaraja Paintings.

About 40 yrs back, some one FOUND OUT that beneath the Nayak's paintings,
Nayaks' paintings and try to restore are Greatly successful in restoring about 4 paintings.

assembling bits to produce Master painting / Phtograph of 50% original
version.
