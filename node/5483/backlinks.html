Hi sampath,
i had a different sequence. i saw parthiban kanavu movie first.
then several years later read parthiban kanavu. then i read ps and
then SS.

but then sequences dont matter when you read about the history behind
the writing of the story.

kalki wrote parthiban kanavu and had the entire manuscript within his
table at anandavikadan office.
he did not breathe a word of it to vasan.
people get angry with vasan for firing kalki suddenly - dec 31st 1940.
the hiding of parthiban kanavu from his boss is often quoted as an
example of kalki planning for sometime to strike out on his own.

within a month his plan was ready and the name of the magazine fixed.
within 8 months from an unemployed writer kalki had become the editor
of a own magazine.
in those wartime days was very difficult without years of planning.
parthiban kanavu started soon thereafter as a serial and sales
rocketted.

sivakamiyin sabatham was a pre- quel if there is a word.

the only parallel i could find was indianajones triology where last
crusade is a prequel of raiders of the lost ark.
