Historical Chidambaram :: @ Perumbatrapuliyur. THANIYUR - formed as
Main Village. Attached with Valanadu with 22 pidagais around.

Rajendra (1012 - 44) formed this as Thaniyur.

Formed Vikramapandya chaturvedimangalam on the West. Vishu temple in
centre. Connected to Peruvazhis... Thirunilai Ezhu gopura
Peruntheruvu .. Vidhi suzh nal vilakku .. Arapparanchelvi saalai
(boarding inns) Saraswathi pandaram (library)..

Out of 22 pidagais, one was Kumaramangalam @ Thillai Alazhaganallur -
on the East! (Panchavan madevi was also known as Nakkan Thillai
Azhagiar!). Included Kidaramkonda Chozha perilamai naadu! Now 5 KMs
east of Chidambaram. There was 32 Acre flower Garden. Brahmin
Settlement. Erukkattacheri @ Jayamkonda Chozha nallur supplied
flowers from 8 maa land during Thaippoosam festival.

Parakrama Chozha Nallur - was merchant community (Nagarathar)
settlement in the North. Vikrama Chozha nallur was nearby. Sevvaiyay
vaitha thengu thiruveedhi - coconut trees planted in alignment !
Gangaikonda Chozha nallur was also neaby. A Thiru odai existed in
neaby Parakesasri Nallur.

Irundhacheri @ Chandeswaranallur was Western settlement - present
Thandeswaranallur. Acheri @ Mahibalakulakala Nallur was also nearby.
Comprised of Thillaimoovayiravar vilagam - residential complexes.

Ganapathy Nallur, now known as Karappadi,was situated in 2 Kms South
of Chidabaram.

Pavithramanickanallur @ Korrangudi is reported named after
Hiranyavgarman, Gowda, who built his palace here. Presently known as
Kothangudi, near Annamalai University.

Thillainayaganallur was weavers settlement. Thanavindanallur
comprised of a Sengazhunir pond near Midikudi.

There were Kshatriyasigamani nallur, Ilanagur, Janathananallur,
Sripirantakanallur etc., also comprised of several settlements, all
solely depended on the resources of the Chidambaram temple alone!

Anbudan / sps
