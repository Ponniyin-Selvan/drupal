--- In ponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, Venkateswaran Kasirajan

The unspeakable sacrilage of the plunder of Madurai by
Malik Kafur created such a terror in the minds of
people in Madurai that reminds even today. In the
Meenatchi Amman temple, there is still a lingam whose
top was broken by the crow bars of Malik's army.

As per temple records, the priests sealed the main
shrines and kept dummy statues before the sanctum so
the original deities are not humiliated. The are many
legends of miracles that happened when the Malik's
army camped in the temple. A stone elephant that holds
Lord Somasundarars shrine ate a sugar cane. When the
army left and when the priests opened the shrines, the
lamps were still glowing inside...

One mans ruthlessness can not be used to brand a whole
community. There are many such individuals in human
history that never carried any value for life or
civilization before and after our Malik. Take for
example, the Jalianwalla Bagh massacre by the british
in our own times. We have not banned british or their
language in our lives. Aren't we?

We humans should learn from history how to live and
how not to live. Forgiveness is the greatest of all.

--- "R.venketesh"
