From: "Narayanan Kannan" To: "Tamil in Digital Media" Subject: [MinTamil] THF Blogging gets stronger!
Dear Friends!

Since 29th August 2001, when Tamil Heritage Foundation emerged in
Cyberspace as an entity we work constantly to promote Tamil culture
through technology. Blogging is going to stay and bloggers become
ever more powerful as cyber-citizens! Tamil Heritage Foundation has
made some efforts to reach this cyber population through new,
attractive, interactive BLOGs. Thus we introduce four new blogs to
savour:

Heritage News at http://www.tamilheritage.org/blogcms/
Heritage Tunes at http://www.tamilheritage.org/blogcms/voth.php
Image Heritage at http://www.tamilheritage.org/blogcms/fotoblog.php
Waiting Room (video show) at
http://www.tamilheritage.org/blogcms/video.php

Why new Blogs?

Blogs are more interactive than conventional web pages. Blogs
interact better than eMail forums! Interactivity is the key for
information exchange in an effective way.

Secondly, using an innovative BLOG:CMS technology, Tamil Heritage
Foundation will archive and store all the digital heritage content
in its own server (not in Google/Yahoo/Blogger etc.,)

Searching becomes extremely easy in Blogs. What is the use of a
Tamil content if it is not searchable? Hence, our new releases will
be recorded through these blogs for searchability.

Heritage News
will be our new age Bulletin Board. Watch this Blog for information
about THF in the future. This will serve the Tamil along with our
traditional email forums.

Heritage Tunes!
will be our door to South Indian Music. Modern web tools have made
it possible that whatever you listen to yourselves, you can share it
with others as well. Watch this Blog to see how it works. It is so
easy that you will have load of links to send us by next mail. No
joking!!

We want to use the leisure times of retired Tamil teachers to speak-
up in Heritage Tunes. If you are not a teacher but a scholar or an
artist or a music lover or what ever! You send us your voice! We
plan to collaborate with VoiceSnap.com in starting a voice mail
group for this purpose. It is easy to share your
opinion/commentaries/reviews in your own voice. Want to know how?
Visit our blog today.

Image Heritage
Everyone loves memory. Photography is the easiest way to share our
memories. In fact photos eternalize our memories. Why don't you
share your photos with us? You may send the photos to us or if you
have seen some interesting photos in a webpage, send us the link or
if you have archived your photos in your own server or in some other
archives, share that link with us. Students and teachers abroad
teaching Tamil needs these pictures for their class room!

Heritage Videos
When we populated the cyber space, it was extremely difficult to
share our video clips with each other. The file size was the bottle
neck. It is no more the case. Initiatives such as YouTube and
GoogleVideos and others encourage us to deposit our home made videos
in their archives. Thus sharing a video is as easy as sending an
eMail. If that is so, why don't we archive those interesting video
clips that deal with Tamil language, culture and heritage in our
Blog. In the long run, "Waiting Room" will be a repository of Tamil
video clips which can be used by academics as well as general
public.

We hope that you like these initiatives and help us to build our
resources. Your interaction is the corner stone of our effort.
Tamils are extremely talented and linguistic. They need to develop a
sense of cyber culture that nourishes itself through sharing and
exchange. For the first time in human history that Information and
Knowledge become so free. The next step in human evolution depends
on this knowledge base. Let your contribution be a part of that
foundation.

Welcome to THF Blogs!!
