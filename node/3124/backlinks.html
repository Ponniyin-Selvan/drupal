can collect the database of all the temples in the

We are targetting temples which are ancient ones. Its ok if we

database of temples atleast more than 100 years old.
It would be better if we have the following information.
1. Location(Address).
2. Management.
3. Contact person/s,
4. Background information
5. Special features
6. No. of idols installed and their description
7. Details of poojas, festivals and special events being carried out
8. Details of assets - land, jewellery etc.
9. No. of people working
10 Requirements of the temple! and assitance needed from the public

and so on.
11. God and Goddess name
12. Sacred Tanks in the temple.
13. How old is the temple
14. When was last kumba abhisekam performed.
15. The time when the temple is open.
16. How many kala poojas are conducted.
The information would be helpful in identifying temples that need
renovation and support for maintenance. Also it would help
in identifying the assets that is with the temple, so that these
assets can be saved from being vandalised.
