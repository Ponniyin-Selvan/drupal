Many of us say that these things don't work in India .
Couple of months back, the Faridabad Municipal Corporation laid new roads in
his area and the residents were very happy about it. But 2 weeks later, dug up the newly laid roads to install new cables which annoyed all the
residents. A resident used the above listed grievance forum to highlight his
concern. To his surprise, served a show cause notice and the guy received a copy of the notice in one
week. Government has asked the MC and that both the government departments were not in sync at all.

So use this grievance forum and educate others who don't know about this
facility.
This way we can at least raise our concerns instead of just talking about
the 'System' in India .
