of reading. So from my school days I don't have a proper guidance or
suggestions to read what. I made myself contented to read magazines, some
story books. 3 years back only I came to know about "ponniyin selvan" novel.
My colleague use to praise the novel and the novelist "Kalki" a lot. Out of
became a big fan of Kalki. Towards the end of the novel, I felt a
emptiness.. that I am going to miss this novel as well as kalki by finishing
the novel. I experienced a loneliness to disuss about this novel. No one
around me know about this novel. The thirst for reading kalki novels made me
search for his other dimensions. I got kalki's sivagamiyin sabatham,
pathiban kanavu. But that puts an end.. I didn't came to know about the
other historic novels written by kalki. But I don't want to miss him. So I
read his other stories like solamalai ilavarasi, kamalavin kalyanam (not
sure of this book name). Again I became lonely in the world of Kalki. No
outlet to discuss about him and his great novels.

I felt very lucky that I got into this group and unlucky that I missed all
these fortunate years and golden words of discussion of my favorite novelist
kalki.

Please anyone refer me the other historic novels written by kalki.

I took the privilege of the person/team who took the initiative of bringing
the fans of kalki and lovers of his novels together.

Long Live Kalki's great creations.

Lavanya
