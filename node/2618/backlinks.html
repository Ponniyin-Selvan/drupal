bats are a perpetual problem in temples
the odour and trash that they make .
in many temples today they hang bunches of thorns to prevent their
flying
one innovation that i saw in a temple near tharangam badi( a fishing
town) was that they had stretched a fishnet on 4 hooks at two ends of
a passage and the gurukkal claimed it was 100% bat free.

but architecture also has its say
just like mada koils have construction to preven elephant entry there
are mandapams designed to prevent bats from hanging.
