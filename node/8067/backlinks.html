I would like to know whether we can start a PVSP Historcial blog.

Lot of scholars and researchers are there in this forum. This mailing list/yahoo group serves it purpose. But thinking about the peruvizha souvenir , lot of members specifically wrote articles for it .

Stating a PVSP Blog and members contributing essays or articles to it on aregular basis, say once in a month - would encourage people to write as well as be storage house of lot of information .

Writing in Blog format would allow the main essay to be seprate and responses/comments to be separate. Unlike yahoo groups the topics wont get diverted and more focus can be given on the topic.

I own a theme based blog(Tamil Literature) - but I come across lot of information on History (which is not suitable for Tamil Lit blog) but can be contributed so that the knowledge gets shared. I also know how much wealth of info people like VJ have but cant contribute in their blog since their blogis also based on some theme.

Either this can be made as a blog or PVSP online magazine which releases every month or some specific period.

I guess Thiru is testing or using Yahoo group blogs already.

Taking such a step would encourage all the group members to regularly writeand also we can have easily searchable pages with valid infomation(its difficult to search articles in yahoo groups).

SPS and other Group Moderators,senior members please consider this.

Regards,
Vairam
