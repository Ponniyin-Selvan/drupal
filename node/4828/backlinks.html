Dear friends (reposting)

RRCs ambition to reach the abode of Siva is also explicit from
the portraits of Cheramanperumal - Sundarar !

Therefore Emperor Rajraja - like the Great Ashoka after Kalinga
War renouncing Power - also would have become to be known as
a "siva-devotee" ... and in this process Easana Siva Pandithar
would have played some inportant role... as Sarva siva pandithar
would have played important role in Rajendra calling himself Siva
Sarana Sekaran !

AYYANAR was also known as SAATHAN. " Saathanai Maganaga Kondar"
says Appar.
There were TWO CELESTIAL WHITE ELEPHANTS. 1. Airavatham of
Indira. 2. Airavanam of Siva. Saathanar @ Ayyanar was the Mauth of
Airavanam - Siva's white elephant.

when Siva wanted to bring Sundara to Kailash, he sent Airavanam
with Saathanar ..to Vanchi - Chera's capital where Sundara was with
Cheraman Perumal.
Seeing that Cheraman uttered " Namasivaya" in the ears of his
Horse which also reached Kailasam with Airavanam where Cheraman sung
Thirukkayilaya Ula in front of Lord Siva.

This was brought to the Earth by MaaSaathan and sung at
THIRUPPATTUR @ Thiruppidavur - between Samayapuram and Perambalur,
near Trichy.

Even today there is Ayyanar temple in Thiruppattur bearing
inscriptions of RAJENDRA CHOZHA and subsequent rulers !

========================

Thirukkural in Thirukkayilaya Gnana Ula by Cheraman Perumal :

" ILLARAI ELLARUM ELLUVAR; SELVARAI
ELLARUM SEYVAR SIRAPPU "

Everyone will be mocking at the poor. On the Contrary every one
will try to glorify the Rich. - THIRUKKURAL :: 752

" UNDU KETTU UNDU-UYIRTHU ATRU ARIYUM AIMPULANUM
ONETHODI KANNE ULA "

All the five senses is within your system.. One should realise the
significance of this. THIRUKKURAL :: 1101

CHERAMAN PERUMAL's " THIRUKKAYILAYA GNANA ULA " also known as
AADHI ULA describes Siva's abode to the finest detail and
also speaks of the ladies around. It describes the stages fro,
Pethai to Perilampenn (age wise classification of women).

In line 137 - 138 .. Thirukkural 752 figures without a letter
missing ! ( quoting Sollale .. as told earlier ! )

In line 176 - 177 .. Thirukkural 1101 figures without a letter
missing ! ( quoting .. Pandyor Katturayai .. "as quoted by the
Ancient Scholar " )!

/ Coutesy : Kudavoil Dr. Balasubramanian /
----------------------

Evidenced that Siva vitually listened to " Thirukkural " as Sung
by Cheraman Perumal - which was considered ANCIENT in 11th Century
itself!
