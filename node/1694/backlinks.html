--- In ponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Sridhar Rathinam"

We are talking about Kalkis charecterisation,,,,in a novel...

Kalki's characterisation is very much intrigate and interesting:

1. Vandiyathevan :: As earlier observed by our respected member Prof.
Rangarajan, there is a possibility of Andhra mix in regionalising him.
Still Kalki maintained the balance very well and make us believe Kundavai
had the right choice. If I remember - Gokul - once posted that
Vandiyathevan had another wife ! Surprising .. If I am wrong, pls ignore.

2. Madurantakan :: Since Sastry felt that AK's murder was a plot which did
not absolve Madurantakan @ Uthama fully (prior to 1950 when Kalki wrote
this Novel, I assume he had the opportunity to check this personally with
Sastry) Kalki again maintained the balance by introducing "not a heroic
Marurantakan" and later "a God fearing herioc Senthan Amudhan !" -
splitting the character into two - accommodating the oft-quoted "Udaya
Pirattiyar Sembiyan Madeviyar thiru vayiru uditha Uthamar .. " in Senthan
amudhan.

3. Very cleverly Kalki left Rajarajan ... He discussed only ARUMOZHI ! But
topped his characterisation in line with the "would-be coming nice!

4. Inspite of Rajendra .. the Greatest ( Here again Gangaikondan theory - another son for
Vanathi .. not through Crown Queen Danthi Sakthi Vidanki @
Olagamahadeviar ! Hence the characterisation of Vanathi ! (Publisher
Thirunavukkarasu is so much impressed with this characterisation, he calls
his publishing housing Vanathi - even today!) Such was the force of Kalki.

5. Ravidasan & Co., as Pandiya's Aabathudavigal .. looks obvious - though
Udayarkudi identifies them as Cheras ..

Probably Kalki does not want to get into a controversy and plays safe.

6. Alwarkadian - the typical Saiva - Vaishnava exchanges .. very lively
exchanges ...

7. Anirudha Brahmarayar - If there is another soul which can complete with
Kundavi in aspiring for the Menmai of Chozhas, it is him - as per Kalki's
characterisation... Anbil copper plates were then recent finds..prior
to '50s...

After a long time, Group is discussing

Thanks for the opportunity and participation of enthusiasts..

nadu. Those who can contribute a lot for this were
given good treatment/importance by her. Those who can
be a hindrance were looked down by her.

She likes Arulmozhi very much cause he has the same
ambitions like her. They think alike. She was not that
keen on Aditha karikalan once she comes to know of his
weakness for nandhini. She says in one place "
Manathai kattupadutha theriyadhavarkal sadhanaikal
padaika mudiyaadhu". She has full hope/confidence in
Arulmozhi that he can take chola nadu to grater
heights.

wants Arulmozhi to marry vanathi for
two reasons

* She has brought up vanathi and vanathi after
marrying AV cannot go against her in any situation and
thus the brother-sister bondage will continue for
long.

kudanthai jothidar the reason for the marriage becomes
apparent. After her brother, vanathi's son is the
person who can create history. She gets strong valid
bases for her plans and then she goes ahead in
implementing those. So she keeps poonguzhali at a
distance.

Hi All,

Kodumbalur velir belonged to a different caste and
i don't think vandiya devan was of the same caste of
cholas. Not very sure.

As far as KD is concerned, right from the beginning
she is concerned only about the 'menmai' of chola
nadu. Those who can contribute a lot for this were
given good treatment/importance by her. Those who can
be a hindrance were looked down by her.

She likes Arulmozhi very much cause he has the same
ambitions like her. They think alike. She was not that
keen on Aditha karikalan once she comes to know of his
weakness for nandhini. She says in one place "
Manathai kattupadutha theriyadhavarkal sadhanaikal
padaika mudiyaadhu". She has full hope/confidence in
Arulmozhi that he can take chola nadu to grater
heights.

One important thing to be noted is Kundavai believes
in Astrolgy. She wants Arulmozhi to marry vanathi for
two reasons

* She has brought up vanathi and vanathi after
marrying AV cannot go against her in any situation and
thus the brother-sister bondage will continue for
long.

* After she hears the prediction of vanathi's son by
kudanthai jothidar the reason for the marriage becomes
apparent. After her brother, vanathi's son is the
person who can create history. She gets strong valid
bases for her plans and then she goes ahead in
implementing those. So she keeps poonguzhali at a
distance.

Both vanathi and Av have high regard for her and if
they become king/queen, must importance will be given
to her thoughts and all her dreams can be fulfilled
thru them.

She married chola naadu. She tells vanathi in the beginning
chapters before she meets the end.

KD is depicted as a strong willed lady. Her prime
concern is 'cholanattu menmai' others are secondary.
KD would have married any commoner for that matter if
not for her love towards
