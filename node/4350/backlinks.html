a few points I observed in my travels to our tourism has become hoteling industry...
Mussorie is an example the queen of hills has now become a concrete jungle

Cleanliness is a major issue...The Taj and agra fort are great but the minute you step outside its urine and spittle.....same in Jaipur Jantar mantar/...

The Sriraman sir....That might be some thing worth tapping

Its about time we ivest in poilets in these places with baby changing facilities...

there are highway hotels in UP and Uttaranchal which claim green envirorment clean water clean toilets(they do maintain it)

TN needs those not mammandur Urea depot for services!!!

Shatabdi services were great comparable to airlines but the coaches could do with cleaning outside

Jaipur walled city still preserved out developement clean and planned.

Madras is far cleaner than Delhi Jaipur and Dehradun

The raods are great but we could still use lane discipline that would be the need of the hour If thailand can do it why cant we...

The Big temple as majestic as ever Thanks to SPS and Kudavoil Balsubramaniam got to darshan the Akasa lingam and Aruba lingam and see the Karanas

The Musuem is Great Sriraman sir and Sathyamoorthi sir Great work....But audio tours will be great...

In fact we should allow people to see the paintings and karanas for a price because thats art not worship but there are security issues..but since it is a small confined areas metal detectors could be used and people could be frisked.

I thought the digital remaster pictures could be photoed there were no signs saying we shouldnt

The nandhi where people place two fingers just to see the Uadayar is now imprisoned...
