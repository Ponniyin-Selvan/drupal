Dear Venkat & Friends,

We all are aware of the MURUGA TEMPLE at SALUVANKUPPAM near Tiger Cave on the way to Mallai. It is dated to SANGAM PERIOD. Now under the Control of ASI.

We also understand (through our dear Gokul) that the MYSORE inscriptions have been / are being moved to THANJAI TAMIL UNIVSITY for digitalising...

MP Sudharsana Nachiappan - when spoke to me earlier - has observed that he can allocate some building fund ( 40 to 50 lakhs) for Public purpose - like PSVP.

Clubbing all these we can move THREE RESOLUTIONS ::

1. Thanking ASI for bringing Tamil inscriptions to TN;
2. PSVP & REACH will join in rendering technical support in reconstruct SANGAM PERIOD MURUGA TEMPLE at Saluvankuppam.. In this connection, Government of Tamilnadu and Central Government are requested to do the needful to revive worship in this Muruga Temple - which was under worship atleast till the 11th Century AD.
Hon'ble Minister Periakaruppan has agreed to render support already.

3. Hon'ble MP Sudharsana Nachiappan can put a proposal to Goverment to create a TAMIL LIBRARY at par / bettering what has been BURNT in Yazhpanam - Sri Lanka...

PSVP will contribute not less than 500000 (five lakhs) Tamil books when commissioned.

Pls consider the above also during course of discussion..

Dignitary list will have to include Hon'ble HRCE Minister Periyakaruppan also. Once okayed we will invite him, Thangam Thennarasu & Sudharsana Nachiappan in person.

best regards/ sps
