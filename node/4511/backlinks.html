Dear Venkat, Nanda, Sampath, Venkateswaran and other friends,

Re VANDHIYATHEVAN TRIP :

MAY 11TH NIGHT CHENNAI - CHIDAMBARAM FOR 2 DAYS TRIP.

OR MAY 18TH. LET US ZERO ON THESE TWO DATES.

Re PALM LEAVE COLLECTION ETC TO WRITE TAMIL HISTORY:

Venkateswaran, in particular :

The tasks we have in hand are :

1. Collection of estampages of Tamil insctiptions already lying in
Mysore.
2. Beyond SI Volumes, ARE summary published so far need to be digitalised.

When we discussed this, we found Wikipadia is already having certain
details.

In this connection, I brought the statistics of State Archeology that
under special plan last year, published close to about 10000 new
inscriptions. (pls refer mails in the Archieves).

What we need is concise inscriptions avl in hand :

1. South Indian Inscriptions volumes
2. Ephigraphia Indica volumes
3. State Archeology inscriptions published
4. Private - varalaaru - aavanam - thirukkoil.

5. ARE (Annual Extracts only).

Since Wikipadia is not having anything new from the above, we will
leave this.

Qn 1 : How are we going to digitalise the avaiable insctions ? Which
Body ? Funding .. Time - frame.

Qn 2 : What we are we going to do with Mysore inscriptions ?

Qn 3. Are we going to handle palm-leaf digitalisation separately ?

If definite plan is envisaged, we can talk more seriously on this subject.

regards/ sps
