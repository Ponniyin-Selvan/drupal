Ponniyin Selvan was originally published in the Kalki weekly magazine with the artwork of artists initially by "Chandra" (?) and "Maniyam" father ofthe present artist Maniam Selvan. during the years 1953-1958 in 5 parts.? My mother used to exclaim the craze people had every week to chase the magazine.? ?

?

?

?
