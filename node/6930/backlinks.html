ஒடுகின்ற நீர்மை ஒழிதலுமே உற்றாரும்
கோடுகின்றார் மூப்புங் குறுகிற்று நாடுகின்ற
நல்லச்சிற் றம்பலமே நண்ணாமுன் நன்னெஞ்சே
தில்லைச்சிற் றம்பலமே சேர்.

Life runs down ; dear ones desert; Old age closes in; the much loved
Body-wagon’s axle breaks and is cast away
In the open. O! goodly heart ,leave for Tillai Spatium ,well before.

many thanks sir,

I enjoyed doing them and felt slightly bad when they could'nt be featured. Its not like you say, i was not fortunate enough to fit them into your book.

anyway, just a few pointers

Hero. Well the face is loosely modeled on a popular yesteryear actor. triedto show a certain undecidedness or a sense of having lost something, even without know what ?. Young man / boy but a doctor. the Mustache didnt come out well.

Radha - captivating beauty, charming yet with a stealy resolve, spiritual.

Noble - Toughest one, had to show youth, vitality yet serene calmness. So took safe way out with a yoga pose.

Srestiyar - special man, penetraing eyes, rustic feel - the mustache came out well to give him a character.

front cover - the essence of the book. Yogic powers - inset with details ofthe fateful day - the burning oil tanks due to emden's bombing of chennai - inbetween them the charming nobel. On top the majestic ship with her ensigns.

Even the novel's name is fashioned out of the original emden crew's labels

The first chapter. was wanting to put this alongside the first chapter. Kind of teaser. A simple yet profound image. Of an empty lamp holder set against a wall with white and red markings..

thanks again for hosting

I took almost more than a week to finish the book. It was a good read.
The story starts off with SMS Emden's bombing the chennai port and

Radha's journey to prove his innocence from his fathers death mystrey.

the logical.

But Chidambaram's kidnap, his time on the ship and the way he
