SPS used to say that history is like a 'kallu kadai'.
I would say this group is a 'kallu kadai'.
'thanni thelichi vita' case kelvi paturukeengala....
sometime our family does that to us so that we manage to go out a
couple of days. :)

As R.Venkatesh said, we used to meet quite often earlier. I think it
came down in 2007 and gradually stopped.

But there are people who travel every week. you can find SPS in
Kumbakonam or tiruchy or tanjavur during weekends and can never see
him in Chennai during a saturday or sunday. Kamal of our group used
to call him 'weekend vishnu' because during weekends you can find
him anywhere.... morning he will be in kumbakonam, afternoon you can
see him in mahabs, evening may be in beach meeting the next morning
in tanjai and so on :)

Even the varalaaru team, when they were all in chennai used to
travel every weekend.

Being bachelor is an additional previlege but that is not a criteria.

Regardss,
Satish
