andru chozhanin puhazh paadiyathu kalingathu parani
indru meendum thodarkirathu inayathil avarin payanam
maamannar Raja rajan saathanaigalin motha vadivai
nirpathu periya koil.avarukko inayathil matrumoru koil
ponniyinselvan....RAJA RAJAR
ponniyin selvan...KALKI
ponniyinselvan...children(WE)
muhangal anaithum mahizhchi pookkalai malarnthirukkum
kaatchi.
aayiram aandugalaanal enna...innum ethanaiyo aayiram
aandugalai kaanappogum varalaatru chinnam inaya thalam
purappattuvittathu perum padai
ponniyin selvanukke yahoovil idam pidithavargal
ulagathil muthirai pathippathi enna viyappu
peyaril vijathai kondavan pillayar chuzhi pottu vittan
kaapu paadiyai vittathu....kappiyam pirakkum
RAJARAJAA
IMAYATHIN SIHARAM NEE
KOBURATHIN KALASAM NEE
NEEYO IYAL .NAANAGAL ISAI NUMMINAIPPU NARKOOTHU
MUTHAMIZHAI PAADIDALAM..MOOVULAGAI VENDRIDALAM

UN PUHAZH PARAPPUM PILLAIHALUKKU UN AASIGAL
UN SAATHANAIHAL MATRAVARKU PAADAMAGA IRUKKATTUM
---------------------------------------------------
congratulations to all
mezhi selvi(lakshmi)
