atemple

temple, the pallava king ( possibly rajasimhan) prepares for the
kumbabishekam of kailasanathar temple.

at

appointment at thirunindravur.
the enraged king rushes to thiru nindravur to see the rival temple
but finds none. when he learns of poosalars mental temple the king
is humbled before superior bakthi.
i guess he undertakes building the actual hiruthayalishwarar temple
at tirunindravur.
