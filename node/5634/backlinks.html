Hi all

The above conference was held in New Delhi 2 weeks back. It is an
annual conference held every year in which scholars discuss various
aspects of Indian aagamas.

I think they have also published few books on this subject

This year, among a host of dignitaries and scholars,

Dr Nagaswamay has presented a detailed paper on Makutagama

Dr Kudavoil has spoken about Gangadhara - In sculptures and Iconography

Few other scholarly friends of mine have also presented
papers..details awaited
