Today I read Dr. Kudavoil Balasubramnian's article in RASANAI issue
(Tamil monthly) Janaury issue - published from

Essentially he discussed Buddha Vihars (3rd AD or so) where the
verticial feature (sectional view) would appear that of an elephant
sectionally... GAJAM

This concept is developed by Pallavas in Hindu temples - Rajasihma @
Adyanthakaman - who built Sree Kailasanathar temple in Kanchi - and
who also created those Five Rathas in Mallai - adopted the GAJAM in
Draupathi ratha - and explicitly created an Elephant nearby !

Chalukya Vikramadithan - who wanted to avenge Vaatapi ablaze - on
having beheld Kanchi Kalilasanathar - laid down all the loots he did
in Kanchi palaces at the feet of Kailasanathar and gave more grants
and retreated .. of course taking Pallava Sirpis to create PADAKKAL
in Chalukya Kingdom ..

Rastrakud Krishnan adopted the concepts and developed ELLORA again
Gajam... concept..

Meanwhile Kochenganan to Brishtam concept in South Indian temples...

Now look at Cave temples of Mahendra ..

Pillayar patti appears to be that of Early Pandyas - with Samana
(Jain) influence in relief (relieving unwanted stones to make
artificial caves) works.. of 4th AD .. The Earliest Hindu Temple ..
with Siva worshipped as main deity and Ayyanar in worship! .. much
ahead of Ayyappan !

However, the CREDIT goes to our Dr. Sathyamurthy - who found the
Sangam period MURUGA Temple in Saluvankuppam - near Tiger Caves -
Mahabalipuram.!

Dear members pls search for SALUVANKUPPAM for earlier postings..

This is the Ancient temple located in TN to the best of my knowledge.

Will check Dr. Sathyamurthy himself and post.
