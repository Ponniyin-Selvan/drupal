Godesses : Balakuchambigai

During

remind intact hence the name Vazhuvur. Demon Gajamuhasuran was killed

Hi sir

recently i went to perur ( near coimbatore) - the hall has one of
the best sculptured pillars i have seen. the gaja sambaramurthy is
superb.

http://www.hinduonnet.com/thehindu/fr/2005/05/27/stories/200505270039
0300.htm

however what interested me was the the Alankaattu Kali and Urthuvaa
Thandava muthy facing each other ( opp pillar)

i had earlier thought the lady underneath the dancing shiva was
umai, but this time closer inspection revealed it to be a beautiful
decpition of karaikkaal ammai ( with her shrivelled front portion).
another interesting point was brahma ( here is shown with five
heads - maybe this dance happened before the head plucking incident)
and vishnu - accompanying the dance with musical instruments.the
back side of the pillar has naradha ( bearded)
