--- In ponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Sridhar Rathinam"

*****Kavichakravarthy is great in his literary skills but he had a
sory line He had charectoristics of his charectors...He couldnt make
Jadayu a human or vice versa....Where as Kalki had to decide if he
wanted to give Vikrama kesari a beard...So creativity is an integral
part of literary excellence...

Creativity,story line,characetrization all are
skeletons.Kudaikkul mazhai had everything you say,new story,new
characterization and creativity.It failed.How you say that is
important.Except that everything is secondary.For example if you
take a photograph of ulapialar statue and send it to any newspaper
they wont publish it.It has been published on millions of
occasions.But if you send it with the caption "Are they still
relevent in the knowledge economy?" you might get published.Even
that is a form of creativity.

And I dont think that you meant kambar had no creativity.The
Raman and seetha in kamban are so uniquely different from
valmigi.One has to read the mithila scenary kambar describes to
understand his creativity.

Creativity is good in writing.But to recreate the original
better than the benchmark is not something which everybody can do.If
somebody writes achievement.Many tried and failed.Kamban did that to valmigi.He
snatched away the top spot which valmigi occupied.

*****Second point Ill beg to differ is Kalkis charectors are human
and down to earth...They are not praised because they are the
heroes...

Thats the greatness of kalki.Had he shown
Vanthyathevan as having rajinikanth's skills we would have got
bored.He made them humane and made us like them.

***The great prince Adithya accepts he was may be wrong in killing a
wounded Veera pandyan...
Adithya was portrayed as an unstable and hallucinating prince
Not as hero.

***A chaste Arul mozhi dreams of going to faraway lands with
Poonguzhali...

Arulmozhi just dreams of going with poonguzali.Compare
this with bharathiyar.He sung it as "Sindhu nathiyin isai
nilavinile,chera nannatilam pengaludane...".Do we assume that
bharathiar is a loafer?We dont.Arulmozhi would have appeared more
humane if he had fallen in love with poonguzali.Like how chandanu
fell in love with sathyavathi in mahabharatha.

*****Kundavai is partial to Vanathi....
she has a valid reason.Kudanthai jothidar said that vanathi
will give birth to a son like thirumal,and thats why kundhavai was
partial to her.

****All because they are Human and at no point does Kalki Glorify or
Worship them
Kalki did not glorify them in superhuman way.He
glorified vanthiathevan and arulmozhi and kundavai in a humane way
which was loved by all of us.Therein lies his greatness.

****Ram Deserved it and he was a divine avatar....but human and thats how Kalki has potrayed him...

That is where greatness of kambar lies.It is very
easy to heroworship a human.But how do you do that to god?Millions
of poets would have done it already.So coming with new words to
describe the oldest entity in the world and defeating the original
in the process is no mean achievement.

Kambar had a mountain before him.Now imagine.Will
any poet today dare to rewrite ramayanam again in poetary form?He
wont.He knows that if he does that immediatly his work will be
compared to kamban.But when kamban wrote kambaramayanam,valmigi was
a towering giant.Kamban snatched away the top spot from him with
pure literary skills.That is greatness.
