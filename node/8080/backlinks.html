hi,

The branding ( physical branding - refer kamal's tatoos in dasavataram
starting scenes) - means they are married to vishnu and anything else would
amount to adultery.

But look at this from another point of view - the Ligothbhava icon - when
did it gain prominence - once its got fixed into shivaite temple agama, how
do you expect any self respecting vaishnavite to enter !! This practise
seems to hv led to a healthy round of new iconographic and literary salvo's.
numerous tales showing who is the best, even to the extent of extreme
representations like the sarabamurthy. vishnu is parivaara diety ( one of
the rest )in shiva temples and similar is the fate for shiva in vaishnavite
temples. But where does it leave the earlier superpowers - Indra, Brahma.
All thrown off their pedestals by numerous puranas and their iconographic
representations.

references to vaishnavite shrines - earlier ( apart from the vibeeshana
vimanam ref) mean structural temples

Mahendra Pallava: Mahendra vishnu graham - the only cave built excl for
Vishnu by him. Ofcourse his first effort Mandagapattu was for the trinity.
One thing to note, the famous trichy lalitangura cave, mahendra claims to
return from a hostile faith to worshiping linga!

Mamalla to Rajasimha - somehwere inbetween, something happened during this
period - there was a defn shift towards shivaite prominence, but it wasnt
still anti or against vishnu. I will write on this shortly.Fyi, There is
speculation that the shore temple sayana perumal could be an earlier shrine
around which rajasimha built his two shiva shrines,

The trimurthi mandabam in mallai,holds vital clues - maybe first time, we
see evidence of forced conversion of a shrine inside hinduisms own folds.

Also regarding intercaste/ religion - we see examples of great ( for want of
better words) tolerance or acceptance. Pallava king ( a nayanmar) has a jain
wife - that he cut off her hand is another story.

etc, but his soul was shiva.
