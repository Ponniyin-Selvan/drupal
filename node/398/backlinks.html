I have read ponniyin selvan quite a few times but I think Kalki has
confused a between the thin line of reality and Imagination.......
In one perspective we can conclude that
Nandini's father is the Pandiya king who took Oomai
raani with him when she as unconcious...the pandia king who died
in the battle with Karikalan is his son....so nandini is his
sister...
In another perspective.......
i gathered that nandini and mathurandakan were born to oomai rani,
but still it is not clear about who their father. most
presumably
it was Pandiya king. but then again kalki raises a doubt because
"how can nandini refer to her father as her lover" and in her
hallucinations, nandini refers to the pandiya king as
"anbe"........
Not only me lot many people in the world are confused abt.
If someone could throw some light on this I would be grateful.
endrendrum anbudan,
Ram
