" According to R.D. Banerjee, "the invasion of the great southern
conqueror, Rajendra Chola I, seems to have left a permanent mark in
Bengal.
Some obscure Karnata chief seems to have followed Rajendra and settled
in
to be the founder of the Sena dynasty". To commemorate this celebrated
victory, Rajendra assumed the title of Gangaikonda Chola, a name given
not
only to his new capital (Gangaikondacholapuram) but also to his temple.
"

=================================
SPS wrote :

I have been reading extensively on Rajendra Chozha.
Summarising : He was called Rajendra Chozha devar in the 4th yr
inscription of then.

Rajendra was the c-in-c for Kanthalurchalai conquest :: 988 AD.
Means he was all along in the limelight for about 25 years till installed him in Chozha throne in 1012, after Big temple consecration
in
1010 and then he had an excellent innings - in the North and Overseas.

Rajendra accomplished two things which his father desired :
(1) brought India mudi and Sundara haaram from Rohana hills, Lanka; (2) Conquering Manyakedam @ Gulbargha :: Jalakkreedai till he conquored Manyakedam!

Rajendra conquored not only Then-Madurai (Pandyas) but also Vada
Madurai (Madura of Lord Krishna!) - confirms his Meykeerthi. He was a
good friend of Bojarajan of Ujjain (Vikramaditha famous), along with
whom he fought against ALLAVIDDIN QUILJI. Fearing the might of
Rajendra, Allaviddin left for Somnathpur and ransacked there.

His ganges expedition was not a mere pilgrimage. His commanders
overstayed
and ruled certain parts of North! (as pointed by his name-sake!)

dreams of any Tamil Kings.

Marghazhi Thiruvadhirai is his birth star. Annabishekam at Gangai
Konda
Chozhapuram on that day - ARUDRA DARSANAM - is very famous even today.

Another interesting thing : In a
Aadalvallan (Nataraja) to Big Temple with 9 Sadais and Ganges in it.
Present one in Big temple is having 10 sadais. Original piece is not
there. Apart from this, there are very few Natarajas which had Ganges
in the sadai till

After Rajendra's Ganges expedition only, it has become a MUST and
praactice to include Ganges in the Nataraja's sadai.
======================
Adding :

1. Like Bageerathan brought Ganges to the Earth by his penance, Chozha
Twilight Rajendra brought Ganges with his Shouder power ! " says
Thiruvalangadu Copper plate !

2. Quote : Udayar Shree Rajendra Chozha devarUTHARAPADATHIL
BOOPATHIYARAI JEYITHARULI (On Nothern Country - on the banks of Ganges -
part of Bengal - conquored the Ruler) YUTHOTSAVA VIBAVATHAL (by virtue
of conquering the Battle) GANGA PARIGRAHAM PANNIYARULINA GANGAI KONDA
CHOZHNENNUM THIRUNAAMATHAL ( and brought Ganges and thus became known
Gangai Konda Chozhan! ) ITH THIRUMUTRATHIL VAITHRULINA UTHAMAKKIRAM
GANGAI KONDA CHOZHANIL.. " inscription in Ennayiram - near South West
of Tindivanam! Where Rajendra constructed a siva temple for his siva
Guru, Sarva Siva Pandithar !

THAT settles all speculations Re Rejendra's Ganges expedition!

regards / sps
