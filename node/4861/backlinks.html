Dear Friends,

Vizag Dhiwakar, with his friend Mr. Sampath - active Vizag Tamil
Sangam member - just retired from The Hindu Vizag promptly arrived
at 1830 hrs and immediately Vijaykumar joined him, followed by
Venkat. I arrived (very) late.

Dhiwakar autographed and gave us the First copies of his book
Visitra Chithan - Mahendra pallava story. We recedived the first
copies on behalf of our Group.

We rushed to New woodlands where already Madam Seethalakshmi @
safanmezhi with her madam friend Director / TN Govt was waiting.
Sivaram Kannan and Chandra joined us around 830 pm.

We exchanged views on dhiwakar's books, current topics on group
discussion etc. Finished dinner and saw off Dhiwakar around 930.
Dr. Sathiyamurthy congratulated Dhiwakar over phone.

Vijayakumar is as impressive as he is in his postings. Dynamic
Sivaraman Kanna (participating in Marathon !), All rounder Venkt and
untiring Chandra followed up and made this quick meet possible.

Special thanks to Seethalakshmi madam.

Congratulations Vizag Dhiwakar..

regards / sps
