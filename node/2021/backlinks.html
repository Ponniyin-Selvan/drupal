We all shared our opinions on this topic.The main accusation against
hindutva is castes and hitting back when violently atatcked.

To quote bharathiar "aayiram undingu saathi,enil anniyar vandhu
pugal enna needhi?"

To quote Gandhi "You can kill even a cow that tries to kill you"

Casteism exists in different forms in every society,religion and
culture.Creating a casteless society is an utopia that will never be
achieved by anybody.Prophet mohammed,buddha and jesus tried it but
failed.

Jesus,buddha and mahaveera said something like "If somebody attacks
you love them"

Prophet moahmmed said "Attack.Bring the world to allah's path".

Krishna said "Hit back,Be a ksathriya,dont be a coward when you are
attacked".

Path of jesus,mahaveera and buddha werent practical.Nobody follows
them.USA,China,korea are war loving nations.Tibet followed it and is
enslaved.

I conclude with krishnas words."When war between dharma and adharma
goes on even adharmic ways followed to attain dharma become
dharmic.Those who remain neutral when such war goes on will be
considered as supporting adharma"

What is dharma,whose side dharma is,whose side adharma is-I will
leave it for every individual to figure it out himself/herself.

Finally to quote paramacharya "Mount meru stood proudly from the
beginning of the world.An ant clibed on it and said "I am going to
finish you off" and started digging the mountain with its
stings.Meru did not reply.It need not."

Periyar,jabali,Mk,annadurai-all will come and go.

Mount meru will stand smiling.

regards
sampath

Gandhi and paramacharya had same views on

Gandhi on casteism:

(1) I believe that if Hindu Society has been able to stand it is
because it is founded on the caste system.

(2) The seeds of swaraj are to be found in the caste system.
Different castes are like different sections of miliary division.
Each division is working for the good of the whole....

(3) A community which can create the caste system must be said to
possess unique power of organization.

(4) Caste has a ready made means for spreading primary education.
Each caste can take the responsibility for the education of the
children of the caste. Caste has a political basis. It can work as
an electorate for a representative body. Caste can perform judicial
functions by electing persons to act as judges to decide disputes
among members of the same caste. With castes it is easy to raise a
defense force by requiring each caste to raise a brigade.

(8) To destroy caste system and adopt Western European social system
means that Hindus must give up the principle of hereditary
occupation which is the soul of the caste system. Hereditary
principle is an eternal principle. To change it is to create
disorder. I have no use for a Brahmin if I cannot call him a Brahmin
for my life. It will be a chaos if every day a Brahmin is to be
changed into a Shudra and a Shudra is to be changed into a Brahmin.

(9)The best remedy is that small castes should fuse themselves into
one big caste. There should be four big castes so that we may
reproduce the old system of four Varnas.
