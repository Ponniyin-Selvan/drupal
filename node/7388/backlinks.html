In Srirangam, there is an entrance named "ariyabatal vaasal". The twofemale figures in this entrance are called Ganga and Yamuna. This entrancewas named after the fierce battles it witnessed during the islamic invation.

Not very far from this entrance you see the VERY FIRST Inscription ofKampana - one Achudha Raya (if my memory is not failing), who was an official of Kampana - recording a grant nearly one hundred and odd years after the last pandiyan inscription.

============ ========= ========= ========= ======
This inscription represents the victory and establishment of vijayanagara forces. There is not even a sign board to help you to identify this inscription.

All structures that you see in front of this entrance - Including the grand garuda mandapa - were built by Nayaks and vijayanagara kings.
==========

WONDERFUL DEAR GOKUL..!!

YEAH...

IN CASE WE ARE REALLY ABLE TO ROLL-BACK INTO THE TIME-MACHINE (Tomb rider?)related to Srirangam, We can see fall of the cholas, ascendancy of Pandyas - and the great renovation work by Sundara Pandya in particular - fall of Pandyas, the Mohammedan invasion (- Mohammed Ismail - in the birth name of Mega Actor Mammooty - was reason for detention in JFK Airport for more than 2 hrs - a couple of days back!! appears US Authorities are ordered to detain and question any one having these two names) - THIRUVARANGAN ULA - Ranganathar going around to Thirumala and return - and Establishment of Vijayanagar Empire on the banks of Thungabatra - and establishment of Nayaks - and restoration of Srirangam temple by them !!

As you rightly say, the ULYIN OSAI which stopped during Sundara Pandiyawould have been heard again after 150 long years while the inscriptions you mentioned were scribed !!

thanks for sharing.
