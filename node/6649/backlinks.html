4. Creating Interaction Groups with Every Police Station and sharing
of information - even in anonymous mode - with more than One Agency;

5. Reinforcing Security systems along Coasts - using Fishmen
Community - Panchyat Heads / Fishermen Unions etc.,

6. District-wise interaction Groups with Disrict Collectors - and an
Apex body of the Disrict Group to interact with Chief / Home
Secretaries of States;

7. Collector / Dist.Revenue Officer - District Police Chief and
District level People rep in Assembly / Parliament CORE GROUP
formation to activate EMERGENCY MECHANISM

8. FORMATION OF EMERGENCY ADVISORY BODY comprised of Retired RAW /
IB / POLICE SENIOR OFFICIALS...

9. Constructive Role of Media in the all the above and beyond...

First few things that struck to me was ::

i. When we are planning a FUNCTION or conduct a CEREMONY - apart
from preparing the list of professionals for conducting rituals,
preparing food or decorations, we also should EMPLOY A SECURITY
system ...and record.

ii. CTV arrangement to record all the entries and exis

In General in any Hotels which accomodates VIPs,

i) Luggages of Guests are also need to be SCANNED.. like they do in
Airports...

ii) Before one enters into a lavish Lobby, there should be a narrow
entry at the Compound level, for the Guest to get down from the
Vehicle & walk ..

Next few months there are going to be MORE OF THESE COMING FROM THE
MEDIA...

1. My Architect experienced the Terror, but escaped luckily from Taj; 2. A Nationalised Bank GM experienced & escaped'
3. Sitting MPs experienced & escaped ... and
4. Unfortunately Times Of India Sabina experienced but passed away..

This represents the Finance community, the Power Centres, the Media
will definitely CARRY FORWARD .. irrespective of the capabilities of
those who OUGHT TO CURB TERROR..
