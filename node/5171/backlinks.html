Moolavar : GajendraVaradhar
Thayar - Ramamanavalli -Potramaraiyal

Theertham ? Gajendra Pushkarani,Kapila Theertham
Vimanam -- Kanagakrutha vimanam
Thirumanzhisaiazhwar
? 2431 ? (Pasuram no as in nalayira Divya prabhandam)
Darshan
Time : 6.30am ? 12, 5-7.30pm

The
legend has it that the temple is located at the spot where Gajendra,the king of
elephants was caught by a crocodile.Lord Vishnu responded to the cry of
Gajendra and saved him from the clutches of crocodile.
Vaali
and sugriva ? the vanara kings are believed to have worshipped at this shrine
and hence the name kapisthalam.This Divya desam is closely associated to
anjaneyar.Gajendra
Moksham is enacted during the tamil month of Panguni.
