carries the child

the amman temple( pillai edukki nayaki)
fantastic thevarams for this place.
very close tokaviri's confluence witht the sea.

paranjothi marries a girl from this place ( in sivakamiyin sabatham)

Being Aadi month - particularly on Velli (Fri - nites) I visited
some Kaalis / Durgais in the 4 Fri's:

1. Mahisasuna marthini - Pallava - Mamallapuram (near Tiger - to be
corrected as Yaali) cave. One of the best few.

2. Tiruvakkarai - Vakra Kaali - Sembiyan madevi

3. Madurakaali (Supposed to be Kannagi) Tiruvachur

4. Tirvengadu Kotravai - dancing Goddes - placed opposite to
Nataraja. This supposed to be the ancient Chozha temple. If early
Chozhas' puhar is submerged, then this is nearest to the
Kvpmpattinam.

5. Tiruvarur - Erisinakkotravai - Rajendra Chozha

6. Nisumbasoodhani - Vijayalayan (Disagreement exists) - Thanjai.
