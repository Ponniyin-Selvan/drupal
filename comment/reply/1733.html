I fully accept venish vijay's views on SUn Tv.Sun Tv is killing
tamil.I will tell you why i think so.

1)Its name is in English.And its founder family(MK)wants Tamil
movies not to have english names.Talk about hypocracy and here it
is,standing before us.

2)Sun TV can clearly say "we are business house. we dont have any
literary motives".Then nobody will object.BUt Sun Tv says that it is
doing yeomen service in promoting Tamil culture.THey point
out 'dinam oru thirukural and veerapandiyan's saturday morning
program as examples.Thats why we point out all this.

3)Even if they criticize they should have objectivity.As venish
pointed out they rank anbe sivam lower than dhool.If its in business
sense i can understand.But its not in business sense which they
judge movies.Its politics.Has anybody seen sun Tv opening its mouth
about vikram's kaasi?It did not.Since that movie was sold to some
other channel.THey now attack silambarasn and T Rajendar since they
speak against MK.They insulted "kilakku chemmayile" since kalaipuli
dhanu was MDMK man.THey gave pathetic rankings to tenali since it
was sold to raj tv.

4)JJ TV doesnt have any tamil promotion in their mind.Its openly
aiadmk and they dont pretend as if they have holy objectives.Its
propoganda piece and write off case.But we expect more from "tamil
ina talapathi's" channel.

Sun Tv doesnt provide what we want.Its trying to make us see what it
wants us to see.It does not want us to see kaasi and tenali.It wants
us to see dhool and dil.It doesnt want silambarasan to become
popular.It does not want comedian vivek to be popular.It demeans
them.It has humiliated comedian vivek in every movie review.It is
trying to change our tastes and preferences using its monopoly
positions.

At times this gets highly comical.YOu only have to see
veerapandiyan's nerukku ner.When BJP was allying with DMK,my god the
leftist,dravidian disciple veerapandiyan was tearing apart
congressmen in his programs even though he is supposed to be a
neutral host.People like valapadi ramamoorthy and communists were
torn apart by him.They actually have an intelligent way of doing
it.They will invite both BJP and communist leaders and ask them to
attack each other.BUt if they ally with BJP,they will edit
communists scathing remarks or valid points and will run the program
to show as if bjp won the debate.If alliances change they will
reverse the positions.Thats all.

Jaya TV is very honest in this.Their debates will have only aiadmk
men and alliance partners.Its very straight forward commercial.do we
expect objectivity from propoganda piece?we dont.But sun tv pretends
to be objective when they arent,and thats why we criticize on it.

All these complaints are valid to other tv's also.I
am highlighting sun Tv only since,it comes from a movement which had
restoration of tamil's past glory as its objective.Now sun tv is
destroying it.MK attacked rajinikanth for showing the movie BABA in
which rajini portrayed some yogi as living for 2000 years.He said
that rajinikanth is promoting superstitions among his fans.Now why
doesnt he attack suntv showing indira sounderrajan's reincarnation
stories and marmadesams?Isnt that also promoting superstitions?

One thing i agree with dr sridhar.He calls for us
to boycott all these junk artists.If i go to india i will see lots
of movies and listen songs only since im nostalgic about the kg
theatre in coimbatore and the urulaikilangu bonda they sell there.I
see sun tv in india only since thats where all my aunts and uncles
sit whenever they have free time.i will talk to them,but they wont
talk back.They will talk only during commercial breaks.

You should see the difference in them.I will be
treated like a nuiscance when serials or movies run.But in
commercial break they will treat me like princess and answer all my
naggings.Pretty saddening this is for me.I will stay with them for
just a day or two,but they will talk to me only in commercial breaks
or suntv news time.I feel as if sun tv is controlling their entire
life and free time.if i call somebody during chithi time,i can feel
the irritation in their voices.They have become slaves of
serials.But they were good earlier on.thats what makes me so sad and
hate sun tv.
