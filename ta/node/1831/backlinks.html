Dear Gokul & Kamal,

Today I found some time to scan through Udayarkudi inscriptions.
I am furnishing a jist :

Key for Sequel to Ponniyin Selvan is at Udayarkudi (i.e)
Kattumannarkudi @ Veeranam - near Sethiathope - Meensurutti, between
Kadambur (Sambuvarayar Palace fame) & Chidambaram.

On the North Wall of temple (behind sanctum) :: Hail Psoperity :: In the
2nd Regnl Yr of Rajakesari, to Veeranarayana Chaturvedhi Mangala Grama
Sabha, CHAKRAVATHI (prior to this Rajararaja was shring throne with Uthama)
issues this Direct Royal Order. The Assasins of "Veerapandiyan thalai
konda" Aditha karikalan are TRAITORS (Dhrohins) (Not Enemies)- who are
IRUMUDI CHOZHA BRAHMADHIRAYAR (A Chozha Brahmin Minister whose order shall
b e honoured in the early Chozha Kingdom and Chozha conquored lands in
India - Mummudi is Ealam and other overseas provinces) Ravidasan and his
brother Parameswaran, Soman Sambavan & co.. and their lands, their
relatives, those who had any matrimonial connections with the male and
female members of the family were confiscated, auctioned and the proceeds
donated to this temple for the purpose of feeding 12 Siva Brahmins ... "

1. In 987, Rajaraja called himself Chakravarthy (means Uthama did not share
the thorne).. In this light please connect the Sembian Madevi's offerings
for "Uthamachozha devarkkaga .. " in Thirupeerumbiyam and Thiruvarur
temples. In her inscriptions, she denotes the year as 17th regnl yr of
Uthama. Means 2nd Rgnl Yr of Rajaraja and 17th Rgnl Yr of Uthama are the
same, but S.Madevi choses to highlight 987 as her son's ruling year!

2. Those who assacinated Aditha Karikalan are DROHINS and NOT Enemies.
(Pandian is an enemy of Chozha. Not Drohin!)

3. Ravidasan was the Minister under Uthama Chozha! - as per these
inscriptions ...
===================================

On the SOUTH WALL of Central Shrine of Ananteeswaraswami temple, as
per South Indian Inscriptions (SII) records of 1920:

Swasathishree Kopparakesari (2nd yr) (Parakesari is one who is NOT
entitled to share the throne; Rajakesari is one who is likely to
succeed the Crown King. Uthama Chola was parakesari and
Adithakarikala - subsequently Rajaraja were Rajakesaris: Sastry)

These Records are offerings by various persons for burning a
perpetual lamp - called NONDA VILAKKU (mostly in memory of a departed
soul - even now, we call it Motshadeepam : Personally some of us did
this after the fateful 11th Sept 2001 massacre. The gift was either
12.5 Kazhanju gold for buying 96 sheep, from whose ghee, the lamp
would be burnt perpetually : as per inscription "till Suryan and
Chandran are there?! - "Charadithar ullavarai")

Record #537: Nakkan Pattalaki 12.5 Kazhanju gold -for Nondavilakku;
540: "Merkezhundu arulina devar Kandradithadevar Deviyar Mazhavarayar
magalar parandagan Madevadigalarana SEMBIAN MADEVIYAR .." gifted 96
sheep for burning a perpetual (Nonda vilakku) lamp;
541:Servant Woman Diran Tirali 12.5 K gold; 545: Desavalavan Suriyan of Kodandarama THERINJA KAIKKOLAPPADAI 12.5; 552:Aiyaran Sendan @ Nikalanka Muvendavelalan : 12.5K; 555: Daanatonga THERINJA Kaikkolappadai Araiyan Geyavidangan:10; 556: Kattukkuri Yagna Kramavithan 12.5 (3rd yr 18.1.972:Uthama)
558:Thennavan Mirainattu Moovendavelalan of Devangudi in Miraikkootram
on Tula, Sunday, Jyeshta (Ashtami thithi), corresponding to Oct 17th,
969 of Uthama Chola (period) -= 12.5 K; 559:Solaipiran Kramavithan:12.5K (Uthama 3rd yr)
574: Parantaga Therinja Kaikkolappadai (On behalf of) AAchan
Baladevar .. 12.5 K (It implies that AAchan Baladevar is deceased!?)
581:Muttavaazhpetra Theirinjakaikkolar Araiyan Vengadavan:12.5k; 582: Muttan Kaman @ Narayana Vizhupparaiyan ::192 Sheep:2 Nondavilakku
584:Muttavazhpetra T.Kaikolar Devadi Vidhyaparan - 12.5 K; 586: Vanavan Kramavithan @ Thennavan Brahmamaharajan 12.5K; NORTH WALL:590: Singalantaka T.Kaikolar Baladevan Panaiyan .. 12.5K; 594:Koilpetral @ Gunavan Madeviyar D/o Bhupalasekhariyar : on whose
behalf - the King (Devar) (Uthama) - gifted 42.5 K (Thulaippon) :
inferred she is the concubine of (deceased)King (Aditha
Karikala!):Means she also died along with the King!!
607 : KODANDARAMACHERI Kaanjai Kramavithan:12.5K; On the Wall of the South Verandah in the Prakara, behind the image of
Gaja Lakshmi in the Same Anantheeswaraswami Temple of Udayarkudi
(now known as Kattumannarkudi - near Veeranam @ Veeranarayanapuram -
where our PS Hero - Vandiyathevan commences this epic), as per Record
AR 609 of 1920, SII Vol XIX : PAZHUVETTARAYAN KODHANDAN TAPPILDARMAN
also donated 12.5 Kazhanju......

THEN COMES THE LIST OF RAJAKESARI'S (RAJARAJA's) 3RD YEAR OFFERINGS:
Example :
(Gangan Ambalavana Kandradithan @ Mummudichola Vizhupparayan
(PERUNDANAM OR HIGHLY PLACED OFFICIAL) : 614 of 1920 :: who acquired
about 12 veli of lands from Sarvisara Trinetra Dasapuriya Bhattan,
who himself acquired from Uthama)

THE ABOVE INSCRIPTIONS CLEARLY INDICATE THAT :

1. After Aditha Karikalan's death ( Oct 969!), those from various

Theinja Kaikkolapadai made offerings - recorded in the 2nd regenal yr
of Uthama Chola (971 - but Sundarachola was not there, since the year
is denoted as the ascendency of Uthamaa's year); 2. Atleast some of them committed suicide (on their behalf offerings
were made!)
2. VIPs included : Sembian Madevi, Periya Pazhuvettarayar and a
Madeviyar of AK; =========================

3. Again, in the 3rd Regenal yr of Rajaraja - offerings were made; 4. The famous "KAANDALUR CHALAI Kalamarutharuli" event took place in
this year 988 only. Rajendra Chozha was the commander of this raid..
========================================
All the above points are strengthening the theory of Neelakanda
Sastry that Maduranthakan @ Uthama (probably he called himself so!)
(Sundara cholar, Aditha Karikalan - Rajara and Rajendra are also
called Madurantakas. Rajaraja has denoted Uthama as "Sembiyan Madevi
Thiruvayiru Uditha Uthama".

This gives liberty to Kalki to speculate of Two UTHAMAS - one Sembian
Madevi Thiruvayiruditha and other a villain !

fondly, SB
============================================
I am reposting this to enable our members discuss more in their Marian meet
on the 20th. fondly, SB
