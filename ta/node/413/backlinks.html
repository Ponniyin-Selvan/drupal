A. Please refer to Karuthiruman Kathai chapter (part 5).
According to the statement made by him the following can be summarised:

1. That he saved Mandakini from lighthouse of Kodiakkari.

2. He met possibly Aniruddha and his men ( a statement corroborated by
Sundara Chola as he sent in his minister & friend to search for her).

3. He took her to a nearby island for money. She did not recognise even
her father as she was having amnesia. At the same time he also gives a
lift to man, who turned out to be the Pandyan king. What was he doing in
mid sea? Why he went to Kodiakkarai? If at he wanted to go to Lanka he
could have easily crossed the sea at Ramesvaram? Only Kalki can answer
this strange coincidence.

4. Karuthiruman leaves Veerapandya, the girl with amnesia (or mentally
unsound) and goes on to meet the Lankan king. Within few days
Veerapandya joins Karuthiruman at the court of Lankan king. Pandyan king
offers gold to him for the girl at Bhutathivu.

5. Karuthiruman meets her again within few days at Kodiakkari. Her
father had already died.

6. She regains her memory. She is found to be pregnant. She delivers the
twins - Nandhini and Old Madurantakan.

7. So when did she become pregnant? BY the Pandyan king when she was not
in her senses? If so, Veerapandyan got the deserving punishment at the
hands of Aditya.

8. She could not have become a mother due to Karuthiruman as he left
immediately after dropping her at Bhutativu.

B.In the chapter 62 Etti Painthathu

1. OId Mathurantakan enquires about his father, fearing that he may
claim the right. But Karuthiruman, informs him that he is a Rajakumaran
and he himself is the servant of his father - obviously Veerapandyan. he
was pleasantly surprised after having informed he is not the rightful
heir to Chola throne.

2. In the Chapter 64 Unnmaiyai Chol, Alwakadiyan extracts some
information from Karuthiruman.

C. In Chapter 90 Ponmazhai Pozhinthatu, in the long conversation between
being the father of Nandini. Nandini, hence Paluvettaraiyar got angry with her. Again Kundavai
express a doubt whether a lady could call her father a lover?. substantiates that it is the character of Nandhini and the situation
warranted as Aditya was so hot headed at that time.

What all this means?

1. Kalki wanted this issue to be live and unsettles one right through
like the secret about the actual assassin of Aditya. These two are the
core issues of the whole story and all the events leads to it until he
changes the track and pleased at the sacrifice of thought.

2. Perhaps he introduced the character Karuthiruman only to confuse this
issue. He suggests that Mandakini became pregnant when she was not in
her senses. Both Veerapandya and Karuthiruman hardly spent few days with
her. How could they all be the father of her children?

3. In fact Kalki, being the master story teller, he wanted to highlight
the spoilt mind of Nandhini due to her lonely childhood and the insults
heaped at her. Nandhini must have developed a complex (can any
Psychologist name it), where she wanted to become a prince/queen. She
must have been afraid about her parentage so she tried to unknot the
secret. Perhaps by establishing her royal lineage she will be on
stronger ground to fight Kundavai. Therefore she repeatedly believed or
rather prayed Veerapandya was her father.

4. So Kalki wanted all of us to keep guessing. Even Nadhini was not so
sure, when she told what she believed to the truth to

As I said it is best kept secret of the novel. A prerogative of any
story teller is to leave loose ends. A tightly written story becomes
history, not a historical novel. Kalki is not writing history for that
matter. He drew a firm line as an historical novel writer and historian.
His post script proves it.
