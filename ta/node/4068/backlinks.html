That prompted me to again go through Glimpses of World History by

Former PM Indira Gandhi wrote in the Foreword in 1980 : ".. Indeed
Glimpses was written for me .." Nehru did write for her only, since
her
13th birthday!

Nehruji ends HIS Preface on 1.1.1934 : ".. I have borrowed my facts
and
ideas from old books... I have expressed opinions aggressively .. ..
but
even as I was writing the letters my outlook on history changed
gradually .. Today if I had to rewrite, I would write differently and
with a different emphasis .. "
HOW TRUE !

And from Dehra Dun Jail, on 22.8.1933, he wrote to Indira : "Indu,
Dates
are not very attractive things.. And yet, they help in putting things
in
their right place so that we may have an ordered sequence in our
minds.
.."

And in the DATA of Dates, related to Egypt - Mesopotamia - India -
China, he has columnised under Indian Data :

3500 BC: Common painted pottery civilisation ( for all the 4 above)
=======================================
2800 BC - Indus Vally civilisation
2300 BC - Dravidian in greater part of India
1300 BC - village republics in ancient India
700 BC - Panini - Grammarian
540 BC - Buddha (620 - 540)
300 BC - Ashoka
320 AD - Chandragupta
550 AD - Chalukya Dominant in South India
800 AD - Rashtrakutas - Ellora
850 AD - Chozha Empire in Tamilnadu
900 AD - Chozha empire expands
985 AD - Rajaraja
1013- 44 - Rajendra - Ceylon - Gaur (Bengal) -Burma & Sea Trade
1100 - 1200 - Ramanuja
1250 - 1300 - Decline of Chozhas / Pandyas leading Tamil Power...
1300 - Mangol raids in North repulsed ... & so on till
1919 - Jallianwala Bagh Masscre
1920 - Non-Cooperation by Gandhiji
1930 - Indian National Congress declaring Independence !
=========================================
It will be useful to keep this graph in mind while reading
Ponniyinselvan - written by Kalki in the year 1950 ! ~
