Temple :This weeks episode is about "Arulmigu Dayanitheeswarar Thirukkovil,VadaKurangaduthurai".

Location&Direction:This
sthalam can be reached 22kms from Kumbakonam which inturn can be
reached enroute Kumbakonam-Thiruvaiyaaru busroute in Thanjavur District.

Audio Link :
Audio Duration : 6m 2 sec

Current Name : VadaKurangaduthurai
MainDeity : Dayanitheeswarar,Kulaivangunathar,Valipureeswarar

Godesses : Jada maguda nayagi
HolyTree : Thennai
Theertham : ValiTheertham
Pathigam : Thirugnanasambantha swamigal

Darshan Time : Morning 8am- 12pm Evening 5.30pm-8pm

Pooja timings : MorningSandhi 8 am,Evening-6pm

The
place where Vali and sugreeva worshipped Lord shiva are named
Kurungadurai. As this temple is situated at the west coast of River
cauvery ,it's called as
VadaKurangaduthurai. Once Vali during war
lost half of his tail (got cut). He worhsipped Lord Dayanitheeswarar at
this temple and got his tail back.Hence the name Valeepureeswarar. Lord
also helped a pregnant women by bending down a cocunut tree to quench
the thirst of that women,hence the name Kulaivangunathar.
Arunagirinathar has also written 3 verses on Lord Muruga who is at the
2nd praharam of the temple.

I visited this place 2 years back. Found this to be a very serene
place with not much commotion. There are two big temples, one shiva(as

nataraj has desribed) and one perumal temple. Both are beautiful.
kaveri flows just behind these temples.

For people who want to go there, you can go by bus from kumbakonam
(KM-Thiruvaiyaru bus) and need to get down at Ullikadai stop.
Enroute you can also visit Swamimalai, kapisthalam etc.
