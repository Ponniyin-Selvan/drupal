Hi all,

When I sent my last mail to the group, it was 4.29 pm local time, Monday. I was waiting anxiously to see our groups' first ever television appearance. Thanks to inputs from participants and reviews and comments from others who have seen it already, I wasn’t pacing in short steps like a new-father-to-be in front of the maternity ward. Just as I was waiting for the so called comedy movie to be over (I wonder why they take 150 minute movies in tamil!), I checked my mails and logged on to the groups' page, and was amazed to see that we had 198 members.

Finally, the movie (!) was over.... the famous “neengal paarthukondirupadhu, sun TV-yin thamizh maalai” came on air, edho, dishnet punniyathla (krupa, it's not a typo!) and then lead for Chennai Times came. I was waiting.

The first segment was not ours, though. It was about auto LPG in Chennai, the conversion from petrol to LPG engines, the how cheap LPG is, how environment friendly it is, etc. I waited.

The next segment was on the Ladies Circle of Chennai's Young Achiever's award presentation. As I was seeing it, I was hoping that our segment doesn’t get the same treatment as this: Anita Ratnam was talking on the screen, and smooth jazz music was playing on the background (the usual Chennai times style). I waited, hoping the next segment would be ours.

Unfortunately, the producer and I were not in resonance, so, the next segment was on a leather export company’s (HUNT) exhibition. They presented actor Napoleon with a leather jacket. I was still waiting... Poruthaar bhoomi aalvaar!

And then the anchor uttered sweet words like Kalki R Krishnamoorthy, Ponniyin selvan.... at last!

It started off with the camera panning the browsing center (Dreamsnet) (Correcta Krupa?) with Pavithra's intro on the background. The camera moved and zoomed in onto one system's Samsung monitor (romba mukkiyam!) and showed our group home page. Zoom out and turn, here's Pavithra. She said about the group, members from various geographical locations and walks of life, stats (177 then), induction date, owner, a short note on the group's objectives. In short, she made the point clear that we are definitely worth the 10 minutes on the satellites' airtime.

Next was Kamalakannan’s. He stated: engaloda URL http colon double slash groups dot yahoo dot com slash group slash ponniyinselvan. As he was saying, he was typing the URL, and the camera zoomed onto the monitor. He hits enter, and voila! Our home page! (adhuku evlo kashtapattangannu krupa sollidhan theriyum, anyway, prog-la paakarapa nalla irundhuchu..). He went on explaining the viewers how to become a member (it was a guided tour, basically). Then he spent some time explaining the links on the navigation bar on the left. He showed how to access archived messages, and as he said he showed the messages. He said: idhuvaraikum 952 messages vandhiruku groupla... ( and, mukkiyamana matter ingadhan varudhu... the camera zooms, ada, 952nd mail nammalodadhu! namma

He took us thru the database section where we store the pictures and scanned images. His segment was the essential technical stuff. Good, lucid.

Then came Lavanya's segment. She started with the trip, how they took the same route as Vandiyadevan’s, and about the kalvettu which gave info about Aditya Karikalan’s assassins Ravidasan and co. Hers was more about the fun, adventurous part of the group, or that’s what I thought. Waitaminit! Then came the main thing in her part: the current situation of the koils, sirpams, and what we can do about it. So finally she finished off with something like: the moral of the story is...

And it did have an impact. When I logged in the next time which was a couple of hours later, the number has risen to 200+, and now, as I am typing this, it is 206. Enna maari US-la iruka thamizh makkal inniku sun TV paathu konjam per sendhu irukangannu therinjukitten (like one of the newbie Srini/Ranjini) -welcome to all!

So, to sum it up: it was awesome. Pavithra, Kamalakannan and Lavanya, you did great. Krupa and Anuradha, you too, though we couldn’t see you on screen. Mudinja alavukku program-la vandha ellathayum cover pannitten.

Nan edhavadhu thappa sollirundha, participants correct pannunga. Mathavanga, mail pannunga........

A couple of things: Kamalakannan could have included something about the member database: there is nothing new about having a member database, but it would have helped in other members to incorporate their info there, but I understand you had time constraints.

This one’s for the Chennai Times producer. They could have included the participants names on the show (I don’t remember seeing, vandhucha?) We knew you all only from the mudhal yathirai pix and Pavithra’s and Krupa’s account on the shoot.

Mathapadi, ellam supera irundhuchu….

Edho, naan paathadha paatha maariye solla try pannen…. Paakadha makkale, paatha maari oru feeling vandhucha?

Comments welcome.

Vaazhga thamizh!

Lv,

Thilak
