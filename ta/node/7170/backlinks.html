Hi all
i was overwhelmed at the response to Kanchi's prolougue on my blog.

indeed it was tempting to finish and release kanchi first(while there are other books on my agenda ahead of "kanchi")
i wondering if I should release a chapter every week on my blog starting tamil new years day.'like the good old days serial"

authors like Gokul preferred to go this route before printing their novel and I am sure are very happy with the result.

it may sound philosophical but to me every novel is travel not a destination. what more can one ask than to travel with friends.

a host of our members travelled with me on the Kaviri mainthan path.
sps, swetha, muruganandam, sivaram, sathish, and it was omforting to what is a dreadful job( not writing but editing)

but to those who asked when its available, i can just tell kanchi will be my fourth novel, the third being 'MADURAI'

Thanks a lot

venketesh
