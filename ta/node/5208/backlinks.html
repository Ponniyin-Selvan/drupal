Visvanatha Nayak vs Nagama Nayak - Battle for Madura
Battle of TalaikOtta
Tirumalai Nayak's MukkaRuppu War
Vijayanagar's Last War with Golconda/Bijapur
Ramappayyan's Sethu War
Kilzavan Sethupathis Campaigns
Raghunatha Sethupathis Campaigns

Marudhu Brothers Third Sivagangai War
