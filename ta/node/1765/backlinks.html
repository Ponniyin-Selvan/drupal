I have been reading extensively on Rajendra Chozha.

Summarising : He was called Rajendra Chozha devar in the 4th yr
inscription of

Rajendra was the c-in-c for Kanthalurchalai conquest :: 988 AD.
Means he was all along in the limelight for about 25 years till installed him in Chozha throne in 1012, after Big temple consecration in
1010 and then he had an excellent innings - in the North and Overseas.

Rajendra accomplished two things which his father desired :
(1) brought India mudi and Sundara haaram from Rohana hills, Lanka; (2) Conquering Manyakedam @ Gulbargha :: Jalakkreedai till he conquored Manyakedam!

Rajendra conquored not only Then-Madurai (Pandyas) but also Vada Madurai
(Madura of Lord Krishna!) - confirms his Meykeerthi. He was a good friend
of Bojarajan of Ujjain (Vikramaditha famous), along with whom he fought
against ALLAVIDDIN QUILJI. Fearing the might of Rajendra, Allaviddin left
for Somnathpur and ransacked there. (Incidentally there is a Ujjain
Maakali near Samayapuram mariamman temple, Trichy, even today)

His ganges expedition was not a mere pilgrimage. His commanders overstayed
and ruled certain parts of North! (as pointed by his name-sake!)

dreams of any Tamil Kings.

Marghazhi Thiruvadhirai is his birth star. Annabishekam at Gangai Konda
Chozhapuram on that day - ARUDRA DARSANAM - is very famous even today.

Another interesting thing : In Aadalvallan (Nataraja) to Big Temple with 9 Sadais and Ganges in it.
Present one in Big temple is having 10 sadais. Original piece is not
there. Apart from this, there are very few Natarajas which had Ganges upto

After Rajendra's Ganges expedition only, it has become a MUST and praactice
to include Ganges in the Nataraja's sadai.
