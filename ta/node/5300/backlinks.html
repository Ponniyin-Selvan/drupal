Hi

Regarding mailing in Tamil -

Although its fine to write in Tamil, I would like to think that just like Kalki's books and indeed these incredible kings from our history, this group should definitely have a wider reach.

I would like the whole world to know

I also hope that together at some point of time, hopefully in the not too distant future, it will revive these golden periods of our history and erase the depraved and mediocre periods we had gone through for the last several centuries including this. The pride and glory of the Chola period is of a world standard.

Tamil discussions can always be done on Tamil sites and my only sense of discomfort is in the fact that some of those who join this group with the genuine interest of knowing and participating in discussions related to many aspects of our history will not be able to follow it if it is done mostly in Tamil script.

I hope this will be taken in the right spirit.

Best regards
Sandeep
