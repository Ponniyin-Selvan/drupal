Vijay,

Let me add a little to this confusion.

Thirumangai Azhwar, in his pAsurams on Parameswara Vinnagaram, says

"pAmbudaip pallavar kOn paNindha paramEcchura viNNagaramadhuvE"

and

"paRayudaip pallavar kOn paNindha paramEcchura viNNagaramadhuvE"

Does this mean that Pallavas also had "Snake" and/or "Drum" as their
emblem.

Interestingly, Mylai Seeni.Venkatasami sir, in his
book "Narasimhavarman", quotes the above pAsuram and says that Snake
was one of the emblems of Pallavas. Are there any records for this
other than the Azhwar's pAsuram.

Regards,
Venkatesh
