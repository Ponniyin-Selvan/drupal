Hi
The temple trip was very good. We were able to cover the 'Thirumuraithalangal ' as well as ' Divya Desams ' in the Trichy /Cbe area.
the route taken was Cbe- Palladam-Kangeyam- Kodumudi-Karur- Kulithalai- Trichy.
While at Trichy we stayed at Sri Veeraraghava Ramanuja Kootam run by Tridhandi Jeer's mutt. Good clean rooms, reasonably priced. Very close to the Rajagopuram.
The taste of Coffee at Murali's (he does only coffee ) is still lingering :)
The temple visits only made me feel how much we shd appreciate the nayanmaars for visiting each of them at a atime when transportation was almost nil & also the kings & others who built & maintained the temples.
The itenary I've given below.
For a detailed account of each temple visit http://www.sankriti.blogspot.com/ where the individual temples are posted -one a day.
Regards
Shobha

The study of Sr?rangam epigraphs of the time of Kul?ttuṅga-I
clearly
and in unmistakable terms show the personal interest of the king in
the temple, the devotion of the king , the queens and their
endowments, the provisions for the recitation of Thiruvaimoḻi
twice
in the same reign, the endowments of flower gardens, the
reclamation
of silted lands, institution of festivals etc., show a period of
great activity in the temple, and its prosperous condition, which
is
totally at variance with the Ramanuja legend. As per the records
the
King , the queen and his officers have particpated with in every
form
of pious deed prescribed by the tredtion On the basis of this
scientific study of the subject found in the actual contemporary
records of the temple the story that Kul?ttuṅga I, was an
antogonist
of ?r?vaiṣhṇavism and that he persecuted R?m?nuja is a
fiction of
imagination, can not be sustained and must be rejected.

SPS WROTE (13499 dt: 01.09.2006)

PRANAMS TO SAINT SRI RAMANUJAR.
====================================================

Kulothunga I ruled from 1070 to 1122 AD with Vikramachozhan
overlapping 1118 to 1135 AD. Vikrama's 13th year is AD 1130. There
were grants almost every year and in 1140 AD there was a grant by
Kulothunga II.as well.

It is evidenced that during the 120 years of lifetime of Sri
Ramanujar uninterruptedly Chozha dynasty has given grants to
Srirangam temple as could be seen from more 120 inscriptions ..

ENNAYIRAM : Our members are familiar with this place. On our trip
to Thanjavur, we got down about 10 Km after Tindivanam and found
a
recent stone pointing the direction of Ennayiram :: Kavi
Kalamegam's birth place. There are stories that 8000 Jains were
put on Kazhu.
Another story that Ashta Saharsra Brahmins hail from this place.
however as desired by SARVA SIVA PNDITHAR - GURU OF RAJENDRA I -
a
Siva temple was constructed .. nearby place is Esalam where
copper
plates confirming that Aditha Karikalan put Veerpandian's head at
the Fort Entrance of Thanjai was found about 20 years back...
-------------------------
" Pillailogam Jeeyar" in his "Ramanujarya Divya Satidhai"
mentioned
that Udayavar (Sri Ramanujar)has visited Thirumalai thrice and
each
time he did not fail to visit Thirukkaoilore "Idaikkazhi
Naayanar".

ENNAYIRATHU ECHCHAN a devotee of Udayavar was ordered certain
disciplines : Washing THIRUPPARIYATTAM - clothings of Sree
Viahsnavite devotees (like Meykandar - Sivanadiayar of Nallur! -
Thirukkoilore he went to Ennayiram and Blessed Ennayirathu
Echchan.

During Sri Ramanurjar's 120 yr life time (1017 - 1136) Rajendra I
(
1012 - 1044) Rajendra II (1052 - 1064) Rajamahendran (1059 -
1062)
veerarajendran (1063 - 1070), Adhirajendran (1068 - 1071)
Kulothubnga I (1070 - 1122), Vikrama chozhan (1118 - 1135) and
Kulothunga II (1133 to 1150) - EIGHT Chozha Kings were in Power.

RRC (985 - 1014) established RAJARAJA CHARUTVEDI MANGALAM which
included Ennayiram. In Ennayiram, AZHAGIYA NARASIMHA PERUMAL
(VINNAGARM) was constructed.

1. ARE 335 of 1917 Rajendra I - 25th yr : the income of the
temple
lands should be shared by (1) Moolasthanam udayar temple (2)
Rajaraja Vinnagara Azhwar @ NARASIMHA PERUMAL VINNAGARAM (3)
KUNDAVAI VINNAGARAM (Recall Ponniyinselvan !) @ DADAPURAM
KARIVARADHA PERUMAL TEMPLE and (4) SUNDARA CHOZHA VINNAGARAM.
Also
daily poojas of Surya .. sapthamatrika .. saastha.. Gramadevathas
etc., need to be performed ..

2. ARE 341 of 1917: Rajendra I - 30th yr : Aippasi Sadhayam and
Maasi Punarvasu (?) Vizha..

3. ARE 333 of 1917 : Rajendra I - 45 Veli grant for practicising
Thiruvaymozhi, Vedas, Vyakranas, Vedantha and Meemasa sastras..
Four persons appointed to hymn Thiruvaymozhi daily.. 3 kuruni nel
25 Shree vaishanas fed daily.. Aani Anusham Seven day festival.
60 kalam nel. 3 kazhanju gold for 1000 Shree Vaishnavites.
Beggers
given food and clothes. There was a Vedapadasala in Gangaikonda
Chozhan mandapam where 270 Brahmacharis studied and were given 6
naazhi nel daily basis. Teachers given 10506 kalam nel.. 8
Kazhanju
for those who taught 8 sargas and 12 for those who taught 12
sargas.

4. ARE 347 of 1917 : On Sreejayanthi (Lord Krishna's birthday ) -
Convocation - each successful student was awarded Gold Ring and
Golden Lotus! Long lists of grants and receivables;
5. ARE 330 of 1917 - Rajadirajan I - Kadaitharam - Least taxing..

6. ARE 340 of 1947 - KULOTHUNGA I - 7th year - Grant of Cows for
burning perpetual lamps.

7. ARE 344 of 1947 - KULOTHUNGA I - 39th year - 50th day :: Under
the auspicious of Puliyur (Our Kodambakkam) Verkkadu Kulothunga
Moovenda velalan PERFORMED TEMPLE RENOVATION, TANK FORMED AND
SAMPROKSHANAM !

ARE 333 of 1917 mentions of similar Samprokshnam by Rajendra I
earlier.

IT IS KULOTHINGA's inscription in his 41st year which is VERY
IMPORTANT :
THRIBUVANACHAKRAVARTHIYIN K-U-L-A-D-E-Y-V-A-M-A-N-A AZHAGIYA
NARASIMHA PERUMAL THIRUKKOIYILIL DEYVA PRADISTAIYUM,
JALAPRADISTAIYUM SEYDARULINAR. HE performed this on the eve of
commencing war against Kalingam !

There are grants from RAJRAJA II (326 OF 1917) and VEERARAJENDRAN
(345 OF 1917).

(Ennayiram) Narasimha Perumal was considered KULADEYVAM of
KULOTHUNGA I... as seen from the above inscriptions ..

hi all;

Just returned from a tour of some Shiva temples in the Kumbakonam Tanjore
belt. It too 4 days but was worth every minute. Went to some really remote
one.
Here's the list:

- *thingalur*-- moon temple
- *swami malai* -- murugan temple
- *thiruvazhansuzhi *-- vinayagar temple
- *patteeswaram* -- "durgai" temple
- *tharasuram* -- siva temple--UNESCO's done a really classy job with
this one.
- *thiruvarur*-- siva temple--incredible spiritual experience inspite
of not being a spiritual person
myself. Only siva temple with the Lord in human form,
why? the explanation given by the priest was vague.
- *- *thiruvarur* -- durgai temple for astham and chitrai star
- *alangudi-*- guru temple
- *suriyanar* --sun temple
- *thirunageswaram*-- ragu temple
- *thirupuvanam* -- sarabeswarar temple-nice temple.the inner
sanctum's pillars were more like the Pallava,Mughal type. Pandya,Chola
pillars are of rectangular cross section but these were more artistic. hope
some of our group can throw more light into this.
- *uppiliappan* -- vishu temple
- *kanjanur*-- sukkiran temple
- *ayyavadi* -- prathyankara devi temple--This goddess is said to have
been worshipped by the Cholas before going to war. Also Agasthiyar prayed
here. Any more details?
- *Gangai konda cholapuram*-- siva temple
- *thirunallar*-- sani temple
- *keeghaperumpallam *-- budan temple
- *thirukadaiyur temple*-- abirami temple
- *thirumayanam temple*-- siva temple--This was an incredible
experience. A must for people visiting that side. A single person is
maintaining this temple. This place was visited by our group sometime back
and i made it a point to visit this one after reading about it here. Thanks
to this group.
- *vaitheeswaran* -- chevvai & sivan temple
- *seerkhazhi *-- sattanathar & siva temple
- *chidambaram* -- siva temple--Surely the most beautiful temple of
the lot. The spadika linga pooja was wonderful. The pillar corners and
sanctum corners are so precise that it is incredible to believe that they
did thismanually with a chisel and hammer. We are struggling for simpler
designs with modern machinery. Incredible!
- *nachiar temple*-- vishnu and kal karudan temple
- *tanjore* -- siva temple
