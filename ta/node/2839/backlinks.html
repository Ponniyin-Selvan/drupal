Dear All,

Came across this paragraph in an article by Rajeev Srinivasan in
Rediff.comThought it might be of interest. Has anyone read Angus Maddison's *The World
Economy: A Millennial Perspective*?

As tracked in Angus Maddison's *The World Economy: A Millennial
Perspective*during the years 0 to 1000 CE, India was pretty much the
pre-eminent
economic power in the world, with China trailing close by. And the 1017 CE
military feat of Rajaraja Chola in sending a conquering armada of sailing
ships to defeat the Sriwijaya Empire in Sumatra -- all the way across
thousands of miles of the Indian Ocean -- was unmatched until the heydays of
European colonialism.
