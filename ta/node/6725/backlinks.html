Rahulji:

The noon sun is not directly overhead i.e. at 90 degrees except during a
short period due to precision of the earth, sun oscillating between the
equinoxes. Tanjore's lattitude, 10 deg 47 min is crossed twice, during
dakshinayana and uttarayana. If no shadow falls on the ground when the
sun is within 44 deg. from the true vertical, then the vimana's shadow
will fall within the gopuram's base throughout the year during "local"
noon.
