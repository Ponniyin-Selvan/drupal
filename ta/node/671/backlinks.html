It took an hour and a half to wrap it, but it was done. The ponniyinselvan
egroup got itself on TV.

The shoot was to be at Dreams Net, a browsing centre in West Mambalam, at
11.30 AM. Eventually though, the camera crew and the producer (Mr. Arul Raj
and Mr. Jabez) arrived only by 12.30 PM- which was a good thing in a way,
'cause we wanted to get our thoughts in order.

Kamalakannan, I, Lavanya, Anuradha and Krupashankar assembled at Dreams Net
(Phone calls and emails to the other Trippers wasn't fruitful- they couldn't
make it for various reasons). The first thing, of course, was to decide what
we must talk about- and how(because the actual news item is only gonna be 10
minutes- so we had to say a lot of things in short order).

Eventually, we decided that I would do the Introduction part of it, Kamal
would talk about how to enrol as a member to our group and how to access its
facilities, and Lavanya would describe our steadfastly refused to appear before the camera:-)

I suppose we were all jittery (I know I was:-)- but we decided to
extemporize, for all that ( notes have a way of failing you when you badly
need info).

The too-bright arclights blazed into existence (dazzling all of us), Mr.
Arul adjusted the camera and instructed me about how to pin the
mike-thingie, the browsing centre people crowded around us (with shushings
to everyone to 'be silent')- and the shoot started.

It all went without a hitch. I said my piece (all about how what its reach was,and), Kamal instructed everyone about how to log on to (with details about how to access everything from files to Photos), and
