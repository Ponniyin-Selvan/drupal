Regarding Pazhuvur,

There is village in trichy named as Pazhuvur.and it famous for
Shri Viswanathan temple for navagraha pooja.I think the village is

trichy-karur road.
========================
THAT PAZHUVUR is a Village populated more with Telugu Brahmin
communities. " Sri. Thirumalai" Rtd State Archeologist studied this
village and published.

LAND RECLAMATION - reducing water level in Cauvery in certain
places and reclaim land to cultivate paddy - was done in certain
villages like Thiruvaiayaru and the Queens bid the highest,
PURCHASED (not just acquired) gave the lands as Tax-Free lands to
Temples during Chozha period.

similar reclamation occured in Trichy Pazhuvur.

But Pazhuvettarayar's Pazhuvur is near the Thirumazhapadi which
Venkat mentioned... North of Kollidam - between Thirumanur and
Ariyalur.

=================

I heard from some sites that some cholas inscriptions found in
that temple.

Regarding Pazhayarai,

Its near patteswaram near kumbakonam.
Lot of temples around kumbakonam and most of the temples built
in chola period.

========================== VERY NICE ..

Palace in Tanjore:

There is palace in tanjore and it not belongs to chola. The
palace is Raja Serofoji's palace.

Saraswathy mahal Library:

This library is in thanjavur.And it has lot of details about
chola kingdom.If anyone have a plan to go thanjavur dont miss to

visit the saraswathy mahal.

Kadambur: EXCELLENT.

There are TWO KADAMBURs. One is WEST (MELA) KADAMBUR. Amirtha
Gadeswarar. Temple built on Chariot type structure. Very decorative
temple.

KEEZHA (East) KADAMBUR is having a Uthirapatheeswarar Temple.
===============================================================

Its near very near to katturmannargudi.This kadambur is famour
for one sivan temple.Katturmanaargudi founded by parantaka cholan.

Anbil:
Anbil is in lalgudi near trichy.
--------------------------------------

SAME ANBIL - Dharmalingam and his son hail from here.

native places in ponniyin selvan

Regarding Pazhuvur,

There is village in trichy named as Pazhuvur.and it famous for Shri Viswanathan temple for navagraha pooja.I think the village is trichy-karur road.
I heard from some sites that some cholas inscriptions found in that temple.

Regarding Pazhayarai,

Its near patteswaram near kumbakonam.
Lot of temples around kumbakonam and most of the temples built in chola period.

Palace in Tanjore:

There is palace in tanjore and it not belongs to chola. The palace is Raja Serofoji's palace.

Saraswathy mahal Library:

This library is in thanjavur.And it has lot of details about chola kingdom.If anyone have a plan to go thanjavur dont miss to visit the saraswathy mahal.

Kadambur:
Its near very near to katturmannargudi.This kadambur is famour for one sivan temple.Katturmanaargudi founded by parantaka cholan.

Anbil:
Anbil is in lalgudi near trichy.

Hi,This information is all just im haerd from others and also from books and net.Iam from thanjavur district.So, i know some places near that. But iam not sure this is all 100% sure.

In thanjavur district near madukur,there is lot of villages,but in chola period this are all forest.
I found one small and old temple(actually its not a temple) in one village and in very reserved area.I asked about this to my grand mother.She told once upon a time,one chola king came for huning in thsi forest and unfortunately he killed a cow.Killing a cow is not a good thing ,so he built such small temple in that forest for that cow.

Actually i did not know how much true in this matter.But its very interesting to hear thsi story.
