I agree with Venkatesh. I have not seen any other Tamil dictionary,
but Pals covers almost all the basics in Tamil. I have the CD version
and that too it was given free by (padikara kalathulaye dictionary vanginathu kidayathu, ippo yaaru kasu
kuduthu dictionary vangarathu).

But Madras university publication, Tamil Lexicon is the master of all,
I suppose. In 6 huge volumes, the lexicon costs just Rs.600 or so.
That too with the 50% off (is the offer still on?) for Rs.300 its real
good deal. I wanted to buy this for long, but have been delaying for
two reasons...

1. Lack of place to keep such huge volumes :(
2. Am I so knowledgable in tamil to have such huge lexicon to refer???

There are lots of online dictionaries/lexicon available. Cologne
university has a very good Tamil and Sanskrit online lexicon. search
for 'online tamil lexicon' and you get very good links.

So for my needs Pals and online lexicon suffices :)

Regards,
Satish
