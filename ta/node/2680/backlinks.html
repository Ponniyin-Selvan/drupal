"Kopparakesari vanmarana Udayar Shree Rajendra Chozha Devarkku
yaandu Irubathavathu, Udayar Shree Rajendra Devar Anukkiyar Paravai
Nangaiyar xxx
yaandu .. 18"th day 38 to 199 in Veedhi Vidangar Temple 'Kudathilum,
Padhmathilum, Kambilum Sigarathilum, Vaaymadayilum Naalu (four)
Naasiyilum Ull Kuttathilum " ... MEYNDHA PON (GOLD) 26640
kAZHANJU ...., "
An inscriptional evidence in Thiruvarur Thiageswar Temple that
Rajendra and his consort Anukkiyar Paravai POURED GOLD on this temple
and woven the roof, sanctum etc. with Golden sheets !~

Rajendra gave her Udanirukkai in his Chariot and was roaming in
Thiruvarur streets !
He named a village "Paravaipuram" now Known as PANAYAPURAM on the
way from Vikravandi to Pandruti (Diversion from GST Road to
Kumbakonam - 8 Kms from Vikravandi!) near Viluppuram. He
constructed a temple in the name of Paravai Eswaram !

After the death of Paravai, A stone statue of Paravai with Rajendra
was erected in Thiruvarur temple, daily pujas performed and
worshipped by the King - Son of Rajendra himself - none other than
RAJADHIRAJA !

Above message 6240 :: is related to inscriptional evidence that
Rajendra & Anukkiar Paravai have put about 26640 Kazhanju (@ 13 gm,
it is about 350 Kgs of Gold) on Tiruvarur temple Vimanam!

We know that Parantaka has put Gold roof to Chidambaram.
Kulothunga's daughter has also put gold roof to the same temple
subsequently.

We also know that supported with inscriptional evidence. (Ref files section same
subject).

We have seen an inscription dealing with " Rajarajeswaramudayar ..
sreevimanam .. pon .. meyndhu .."

NOT TO MENTION THIS ?!

or vigorously look for more evidence ?!
