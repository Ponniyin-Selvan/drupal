Dear friends,

I got the vcd and watched it. There are certain things that i
want to write. First of all , i want to thank all the people who
decided to prepare a cd and send it to all, who could not come for
the trip. I can understand and realize how much effort u people have
put in making this thing happen. If there is one word to say about
the cd , it can be none other than "arputham".

I am also one of among few who felt personally
that the intended directon, which is
discussing about lot more mails than it was two-three months ago.
There are topics which are not related to Thirupani, and the usage of thamizh in emails and what not. But,
beleive me, after seeing the vcd, i got answers to all the questions
that disturbed my mind about this group.I was excited to see that
there is a group of people who love to discuss about bad when the topics were off beat sometimes. After watching the cd, I
couldn't control my tears on seeing the condition in whch the temples
are maintained. Afterall, they represent our cultural heritage. a story, but a real story of what has happened some 1000 years back.
If we should say in Bala's bani, "raththamum sathayumai
vazhnthavargalai patriya kathai". 1000 varudangalukku mun vazhntha
maanudaththai patriya, nammai patriya kathai. Now, i can understand
why u people are talking about kovil thiruppani in many mails. Being
in DC, u dont get that kind of oppurtunity many times in life. We do
it for self satisfaction. Here the temples are pretty clean. In the
Cd, i can name few temples which are maintained really really bad. I
really envy you guys that u are able to contribute and i cannot. I
dont know how i am going to pay back my balances because of not doing
this work. I again envy you friends, that u people are going for
second trip to see all similar places like this. As i have mentioned
in one of earlier mails, it is unfortunate for any kalki fan to be in
US this year.

I dont know what was the purpose of making this cd. But, beleive me
friends, the seed for lot of things were already laid in my mind by
just watching this cd. It will be very difficult for anyone who read
anything for these temples.

I have one suggestion. What about attaching this cd with all the books hereafter. Will kalki publication be able to do that. We can
give it for free. In that way, we can say that but also a book which talks about our cultural heritage, which talks
about people like us who lived 1000 years ago. We can also let people
know about the condition of all these temples. Just a thought that
falshed in my mind.

I think the varnanai is given by Ezhuthu Chittar Balakumaran. Or
atleast , i'll say the script should have been written by him. The
commentary is awesome and the person who took the video can claim
himself as a video grapher. IT was so professional and i could hardly
miss anything. Obviously, nothing can match visiting those plces
live. But, otherwise, it was really really fantastic. Thanks sathya
for sending the cd. Ethanai copy venumnalum sollunga, nan pirathi
eduthu tharugiren.....

Veru ondrum illai Ezhuthuvatharkku,

Cd parthu mudithen....
Idhayam innum baramai irukkirathu nanbargalae......

Anbudan,
Manikandan
