young has become less bright nowadays.

do everything. The scientists say that even plants

remaining as a witness and enjoying all this, man
suffers through being possessed by the disease of 'I'
and 'mine'.
We are all the same. Difference is only in degree.

'I'-ness remains.

and that all is His action, we make resolves and plans
and thus create a world within ourselves, and suffer
out of it.

within him.
