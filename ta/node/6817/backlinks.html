????? A little information about Cave Temples.
??? 1. Tirukazhukundram - Vedagireeswarar Temple - Cave temple in

??? 2. Vallam near Chengalpatttu - 2 cave temples, one for Vishnu
and one for Sivan(Vedandeswarar)
??? 3. Pallavaram - 2 kms from Pallavaram there is Pancha Pandavar
Kugaikoil - one was converted into mosque. Inscriptions about Mahendra

Pallavan and his discovery of Sankeerana Sathi ( one of 5 Thalams in
music) is there.

??? 4. Mamandur - Kancheepuram to Cheyyar route 1.5 kms from
Mamandur 4 cave temples are there.- the inscriptions tells about

Mahendra Varman's Mathavilasa Prakasanam and Dakshina Sithiram.

???? 5. Kuranganil Muttam (owns sambandar devaram)- Kancheepuram to
Cheyyar route Dhoosi village. 2 kms from Dhoosi Kuranganil Muttam is

there.? The cave temple is below ground level.????

???? 6.Mahendravadi -? on the route from Kancheepuram to Sholingar,
Mahendra vadi village is there. This is also by Mahendra Varman.
???? 7.Seeyamangalam - Gingee to Vandavasi route a village Thesuris
there.? 1.5kms fromThesur Seeyamangalam cave temple is situated. Its

name is Avani Pajan(Mahendravarman's another name) and having?
inscriptions about Nandhi Varman.
